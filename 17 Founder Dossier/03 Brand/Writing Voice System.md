# Writing Voice System

## Desired qualities
- sharp
- human
- commercially aware
- grounded
- slightly provocative when useful
- direct without being cold

## Avoid
- fluff
- parallel contrast structures
- robotic startup jargon
- generic inspiration
- long abstract build-up

## Opening styles that fit
- sharp observation
- hidden truth
- operator lesson
- market tension
- founder confession with strategic point
