# Decision Making System

## Filters
- Does this compound?
- Does this reduce future confusion?
- Does this increase leverage?
- Does this align with the identity we are building?
- Does this create optionality or dependency?

## Biases to keep
- Favor clear outputs
- Favor systems over heroic effort
- Favor strategic depth over cosmetic activity
- Favor speed when reversibility is high

## Decisions to document
- strategic bets
- brand choices
- hiring standards
- venture priorities
- platform/tooling choices
