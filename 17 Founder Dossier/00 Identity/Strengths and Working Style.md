# Strengths and Working Style

## Core strengths
- Strategic synthesis across business, product, GTM, and systems
- Pattern recognition
- Positioning and narrative design
- Founder-level operating clarity
- Ability to connect markets, people, and execution layers

## Working style
- Operator-first
- High standards
- Bias toward action
- Prefers outputs that are usable immediately
- Likes structure in operational docs and fluidity in narrative writing
- Thinks in leverage, control, inevitability, and compounding advantage

## Best environment
- Clear outcomes
- Fast feedback
- High-agency collaborators
- Systems that can be improved continuously

## Watchouts
- Overbuilding before proof
- Carrying too many parallel fronts
- Frustration with low-signal communication
