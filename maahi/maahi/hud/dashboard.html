About Maahi

Local. Private. Yours.

Strict-local promise

Nothing leaves your Mac. The brain (Ollama), embeddings (nomic-embed-text), STT (Whisper), and TTS (Piper or macOS say) all run locally. Your conversations live in your own Obsidian vault.

Where files live

<project>/maahi/ project root ├── config.yaml (Settings panel writes this) ├── config.yaml.bak (last good copy) ├── maahi/ source ├── logs/maahi.log runtime log └── voices/ Piper voice files ~/.maahi/skills/ your skill packs <vault>/maahi/memory/ ├── facts.md durable facts about you ├── preferences.md stable settings ├── conversations/*.jsonl raw transcripts ├── consolidated.json ledger of processed dates └── vector_index/ semantic search index
