# Prompt: Energy audit — what's draining vs feeding

**Use when:** Monthly or whenever you notice persistent low energy / dread / Sunday-night anxiety. The vault should help surface what's draining you before it metastasizes.
**Reads:** `_AI/Contexts/meet.md`, last 4 weekly reviews, recent journal entries, `40 Areas/Health/` (when populated)
**Filed at:** `40 Areas/Health/Energy-Audits/YYYY-MM.md`

## Inputs
- <<recent low-energy moments>>: [Where did I feel drained — when, doing what, with whom]
- <<recent high-energy moments>>: [Where did I feel energized]
- <<sleep/training/nutrition baseline this period>>: [If known — be honest]
- <<commitments I've been carrying>>: [Things I said yes to that I now regret, or things I'm doing on willpower]

## Prompt body

Read `_AI/Contexts/meet.md` and the last 4 weekly reviews. Scan recent journal entries for energy signals.

Inputs: see above.

Run an energy audit using this frame:

```
# Energy Audit — [Month YYYY]

## Net energy direction this period
- Trending up / flat / down

## Drains (ranked by total energy cost, not frequency)
| Drain | Frequency | Cost per occurrence | Total impact | Reversible? |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| ... | ... | ... | ... | yes/no |

## Feeders (what consistently puts energy in)
- [Activity / person / time-of-day / context]
- ...

## Pattern surfacing
- [If a specific person / venture / activity shows up in both drains and feeders, name the asymmetry]
- [If energy is collapsing on a specific day of the week, name it]
- [If a commitment is now net-negative that was once net-positive, name it]

## Three uncomfortable observations
1. [Direct read on something I might be avoiding — be honest, be kind]
2. ...
3. ...

## Reversal plays (max 3)
- [Concrete change to make this month — not "be more present", but "no work meetings before 9am Tuesdays"]
- ...

## Things to consider stopping
- [Specific. Commitment, person, project, recurring meeting]

## Things to add (only if they're cheap)
- [Small additions, not "start meditating daily" — micro-interventions]

## Compass check
- Does my current energy allocation match `20 Compass/Vision/30 Next 90 Days.md`?
- If no — what's misaligned?

## Net recommendation
[One paragraph: should the next month look the same, slightly different, or meaningfully different?]
```

Be operator-honest. The job is not to make me feel better — it's to make me notice what I'm absorbing as "normal" that isn't.
