# Writing Voice and Tone

## Core qualities
- sharp
- founder-grade
- commercially aware
- human
- direct
- memorable
- high-signal

## Preferences
- clear structure for business/operating docs
- flowing prose for narratives and thought pieces
- real language over corporate jargon
- strong first lines
- content that feels lived, not manufactured

## Avoid
- robotic AI phrasing
- cliché startup talk
- fake motivation
- empty inspiration
- contrast pattern writing like "not X, but Y"
- poem-like broken lines unless intentionally cinematic

## Ideal result
The writing should sound like an experienced founder-operator with emotional range, conviction, and useful thinking.
