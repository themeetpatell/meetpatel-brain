## MediCore HMS

**Role:** Founder / Venture Builder

**What it is:** A full-stack hospital management system built for UAE, GCC, and India. It covers clinical operations, financial management, supply chain, patient records, and a B2B healthcare marketplace — all in one platform, built simultaneously to the regulatory standards of multiple markets (DHA, HAAD, MOH for UAE; ABDM, NABH, DPDP for India; HL7 FHIR, DICOM, and HIPAA internationally).

**Why it matters:** The moat is structural. A hospital running MediCore has years of live operational and clinical history inside the platform — patient records, financial ledgers, supply chain, staff data. Migrating away means migrating all of it simultaneously. No hospital CTO authorises that unless the platform catastrophically fails. Add a four-sided network effect across hospitals, vendors, patients, and partners, plus compliance infrastructure that took years to build and cannot be shortcut by a new entrant, and the position gets harder to attack over time.

I am currently positioning MediCore for investors as healthcare infrastructure — the operating system hospitals run on.

**Stage:** Fundraising / Investor conversations active
**Category:** HealthTech / B2B SaaS / Infrastructure

## BiggFam (formerly FamilyOS)

**Role:** Founder

**What it is:** A family operating system for Indian families in India and the GCC. The product covers shared family calendars, memory vaults, financial planning tools built around Indian family dynamics, and AI-powered features that understand multigenerational household context.

**Core idea:** Modern Indian families are geographically separated but culturally tight. The tools they use — WhatsApp threads, shared Notes files, scattered spreadsheets — are not built for this. BiggFam is. The market is 1.4 billion people in India plus tens of millions of NRI families across the GCC, US, UK, and Canada. The launch starts in Dubai, the highest concentration of high-income Indian expat families in the world.

**Stage:** Seed fundraise underway, Dubai-first launch strategy
**Category:** Consumer Tech / Family Tech / AI

## How to Think About This Portfolio

Five themes run across every company I have built or am building.

**Systems over one-off effort.** Every company moves toward frameworks, workflows, and operating leverage. I build things that scale, not things that need me in every decision.

**AI as infrastructure.** AI is a native layer in execution, not a marketing add-on. The question at the start of every build: what is the human job here, and how much of it should an AI be doing instead?

**Category redesign over market entry.** The ambition is usually bigger than entering a space. It is about redesigning how the space operates — from the inside out.

**Human behaviour matters.** Even in B2B and operational contexts, I care about psychology, trust, narrative, and decision flow. Products that ignore how people actually behave do not survive contact with users.

**GCC/UAE relevance with global ambition.** The regional operating context shapes GTM and business logic, especially in the early stages. But the thinking is designed to scale past one geography.
