# Product Strategy

# **LifeOS Product Strategy (Need-First)**

## **Positioning**

- LifeOS is a private Life Operating System: your secure vault + identity graph + decisioning twin designed for multi-decade durability.
- Promise: "Swiss-bank security, second-brain clarity, heirloom permanence." Not another notes app or chatbot.

## **Pain Map (What hurts today)**

- Fragmented life data: photos, chats, docs, IDs, milestones spread across clouds and devices; no canonical source of truth.
- Unreliable memory and provenance: cannot reconstruct context around key events or prove decisions/outcomes later.
- Decision fatigue: important choices happen without remembering values, past decisions, or commitments.
- Security anxiety: consumer clouds and AI models feel porous; fear of leaks, lockouts, and loss on device failure.
- Legacy risk: families lose stories, lessons, and access to critical assets when accounts expire or people pass away.
- Continuity gaps: no reliable recovery kit if devices die, travel incidents occur, or accounts are compromised.

## **Who we serve (primary personas)**

- Builders/Founders: want a personal system of record, decision audit trail, and recovery/continuity for company and life.
- Families/Archivists: want heirloom-grade memory vaults, structured timelines, and controlled sharing across generations.
- Operators/Executives: need context-rich briefings, relationship intelligence, and private AI that reflects their standards.

## **Differentiated approach**

- Private-by-default ingestion and AI: client-held keys, sandboxed AI execution, signed runs, no default model training.
- Identity graph + time-aligned memory vault to rebuild context, not just store files.
- Autopilot playbooks tied to real pains (decision guardrails, crisis recovery, continuity handoffs) vs. generic chat.
- Longevity and portability: exports include data + policies + twin state; design for 25+ year utility.

## **Feature map (v0-v1)**

- ***V0: Prove the painkiller***
- Memory Vault: import from photos, files, chats, calendars; dedupe and stitch into a timeline with entities/people.
- Identity Layer: auto-tag skills, roles, values, and relationships; capture life shifts and milestones.
- Decision Brief: daily cockpit with risks, commitments at risk, and suggested actions.
- Recovery Kit: offline export with IDs, insurance, critical contacts, device/account recovery steps.
- Policy Engine: client-held keys, programmable access (time locks, dual approvals), audit log per action.
- ***V1: Autopilot and legacy***
- Digital Twin: local-first model fine-tuned on your graph; runs simulations and proposes plans with citations.
- Continuity/Legacy Packages: time-locked heirloom capsules (letters, videos, instructions) with multi-key unlock.
- Relationship Intelligence: detect drift, recommend follow-ups, log interaction quality.
- Intangible Assets Bank: balance sheet of lessons, beliefs, promises; track outcomes against them.
- Cross-platform ingestion at scale: email/parsing, voice-to-structured entries, API for third-party apps.

## **Core workflows (need-focused)**

1) ****Memory reconstruction:**** Drop a folder/chat export; system rebuilds narrative, tags people, outcomes, and surfaces discrepancies.

2) ****Decision guardrails:**** Before a big decision, Twin surfaces similar past cases, values, and outcomes; returns a 3-step playbook.

3) ****Continuity plan:**** User sets triggers (time, geofence, inactivity); system packages legal docs, credentials, and messages for heirs with multi-key unlock.

4) ****Relationship heatmap:**** Pulls from communication logs; flags drift and delivers ready-to-send follow-ups.

5) ****Crisis recovery:**** Device stolen—one-click secure kit with IDs, insurance, key contacts, and temporary comms channel.

## **Trust and security stance**

- Encryption: client-held keys, E2E for storage; per-action signing for AI calls and exports.
- Execution: AI runs inside isolated sandboxes with policy checks; no silent model training.
- Storage: user-chosen storage or isolated LifeOS vault clusters with hardware-backed enclaves.
- Auditability: immutable logs for every share, export, or AI run; user sees and can revoke access anytime.
- Portability: full export of data + policies + twin state so users are never trapped.

## **Activation and GTM**

- Target early adopters: founders/operators (decision guardrails + recovery kit) and families (memory reconstruction + heirloom mode).
- High-intent channels: partner with executive coaches, estate planners, family offices, and security-minded communities.
- Onboarding motion: 1) ingest starter pack (calendar + photos + chat sample), 2) show first reconstructed memory and decision brief within 24 hours, 3) ship recovery kit.
- Pricing: blended SaaS + storage; base subscription for vault + AI runs, add-ons for storage tier, additional family seats, and premium support.
