### About QKard

Qkard is a secure virtual payment instrument that can be used at any online and offline gaming, adventure and gambling platforms.

- Qkard is a gaming exclusive e-wallet.
- Qkard also allows cross platform purchase of collected game points on any game.
- At Qkard users can covert game points into coupons and shopping vouchers.
