# CampusPay

Key Features: Accepted across all college canteens and fests
Key Benefits: Free canteen/stationary from your campus
Users will get equal points for every transactions made and that point can be used to make any gaming purchase. 
Key Llimitations: Amount needed to be added to Qkard from bank account in order to make payment. 
Pricing: Zero transaction fee

They no longer need to carry cash to the schools/colleges for purchases

Authorises parents to refill the wallet for purchases including budgeting features where they can analyse how much is going to be spent

**For Parental Locks/features:**

- Parents can recharge this wallet and students can make purchases (eg: school inventory) or payments (eg: mess expenses) through this wallet.
- Parents can also make use of the money to pay the school fees.
- Parents can track the transactions via wallet through their logins (web and mobile app).
- Parents get transaction alerts on their registered mobile numbers.
- This wallet is independent of the academic session i.e. one can use wallet money across the academic sessions without any hurdle.
