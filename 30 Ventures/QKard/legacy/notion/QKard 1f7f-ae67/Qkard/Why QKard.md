### Why QKard?

- Qkard provides the most secure payment system to make payment across any platform and keep your original mode of payment unexposed.
- At Qkard, anyone can cash-in their gaming points to get shopping vouchers and cross game purchase coupons.
- Qkard is the only payment mode in India that allows its users to pay below INR 50 from UPI transaction across any fantasy gaming/gaming/betting websites.
- Users earn extra points on every transaction made through Qkard.
