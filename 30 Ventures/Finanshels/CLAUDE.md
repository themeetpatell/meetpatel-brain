# Finanshels — Agent Context

> Read this before doing any work on Finanshels. This is the canonical agent-readable brief.

## One-paragraph snapshot

Finanshels is a UAE-based accounting / tax / AML / audit / financial operations platform for SMEs. Real revenue. Operating environment Meet is inside daily as **Head of Centre of Excellence**. The CoE is the execution engine — pre-sales, ops, SOPs, training, dashboards, WhatsApp-first SMB sales motion. Strategic role inside Meet's portfolio: **revenue anchor + real GTM lab + trust-heavy regulated category** that proves AI-native operations can work in a regulated industry. This is the venture that funds and de-risks the others.

## Why it matters (to Meet)

- Real cash flow. Real customers. Real deadlines (VAT, AML, audit cycles).
- A regulated category where AI-native execution is *trust-multiplying*, not trust-eroding — exactly the kind of moat Meet thinks compounds.
- Inside operating role gives Meet direct authorship over the GTM playbook + ops cadence — which then becomes IP for StartupOS.

## Current state (refresh quarterly)

- Stage: revenue, scaling
- Team: CoE team (Meet leads), broader Finanshels org around it
- Stage motion: WhatsApp-first SMB acquisition, channel partner growth, AI-native demand engine in development
- Operating system: EOS adopted — V/TO, Rocks, Scorecard, weekly L10s

## ICP (who buys)

- UAE SMEs needing compliant accounting / tax / AML / audit at SMB pricing
- Sub-segments: NRI-owned SMBs, mainland trading companies, free zone SMEs, e-commerce sellers, professional services firms
- Pain triggers: VAT deadline coming, AML registration, audit due, owner tired of running books manually, growing past first finance hire
- Decision-maker: typically the owner-founder (not a CFO — they don't have one)
- Channel: WhatsApp inbound is currently the highest-converting channel (>4x paid social)

## Current strategic priorities

1. **AI-native demand engine** — automate qualification + nurture + booking with quality gates; replace human pre-sales for top-of-funnel
2. **EOS execution maturity** — make weekly L10s, quarterly rocks, scorecards habitual across the CoE
3. **Monetization** — channel partner motion (referral structure, commissions, partner SOPs)
4. **Operating system productization** — codify the CoE so it can scale beyond Meet

## Voice + brand

- **Trust, calm, regulated.** Not flashy. Not "disrupting accounting". Boring works.
- Bilingual register awareness — English-first, Arabic-aware, Hindi/Gujarati for sub-continent owner-founders
- Customer communications: warm but professional. WhatsApp is fast and friendly, never slangy.

## What NOT to do on Finanshels work

- Don't use "innovative" / "revolutionary" / "AI-powered" in customer-facing copy. Regulated buyers want competence signals, not hype.
- Don't propose pricing or scope changes without Meet's confirmation — there are commercial constraints not in this file.
- Don't draft regulatory / tax / AML claims without flagging for human review — Finanshels has FTA / regulator exposure.
