## SECTION 2 — RFP Response Document

### 2.1 Cover Letter
[Short letter to the issuer. Thank them for the opportunity, state Finanshels'
interest, name the services in scope, reference the submission deadline.]

### 2.2 Executive Summary
[Half a page. WHY first — every founder/business deserves financial clarity —
then WHAT Finanshels proposes for this mandate. Anchor one proof point. State
the headline reason Finanshels is the right partner.]

### 2.3 Company Overview
[Finanshels is the Finance OS for the GCC's fastest-growing SMEs — an
infrastructure partner, not just an accounting firm. UAE-based, GCC-focused,
AI-native delivery. Proof points: 7,000+ businesses served; 150+ qualified
accountants; trusted by Abwaab, Silkhause, Dubai Future District Fund, Cotu
Ventures. No invented additions.]

### 2.4 Delivery Methodology
[How Finanshels will deliver this specific mandate — onboarding, the recurring
cycle (bookkeeping, VAT, CT, payroll, reporting as scoped), communication
cadence, tools, quality control. Concrete and issuer-specific.]

### 2.5 Team & Capability
[The team model — qualified accountants, review layers, AI-native delivery.
Describe roles, not invented named individuals. Map capability to the RFP's
service scope.]
