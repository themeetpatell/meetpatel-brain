# Discovery Question Modules — by Service Line

> Modular question bank for the `scoping-questionnaire` skill. Pick only the
> modules that match the services in scope. Each question shows a founder-friendly
> phrasing and, in [brackets], the scoping driver it feeds. Never send brackets
> to the prospect. Verify all thresholds against current FTA guidance.

---

## MODULE A — Entity & Licensing (always include)
- What is your company's legal name and trade licence number?
- Are you mainland (DED), free zone, or offshore? If free zone, which one? [entity type → QFZP / scoping complexity]
- How many UAE entities does your group have? [entity count → engagement size]
- What is your financial year-end date? [drives CT deadline, close timing]
- What is your main business activity? [industry → complexity]
- When was the company incorporated? [back-period scope]

## MODULE B — Revenue & Transaction Volume (always include)
- What was your revenue last financial year (or expected this year)? Ranges fine: <500K / 500K–3M / 3M–10M / 10M–50M / >50M AED [revenue band → CT tier, SBR, audit need]
- Roughly how many transactions do you process a month (sales + purchases + bank lines)? [volume → bookkeeping tier]
- How many bank accounts and credit cards does the business use? [accounts → reconciliation effort]
- Do you sell in foreign currencies or hold foreign-currency accounts? [FX complexity]
- Do you have inventory or stock to account for? [complexity]

## MODULE C — Current Bookkeeping State & Systems
- How are your books kept today — accounting software, spreadsheets, or not yet? [current state]
- If software, which one (e.g. Zoho, QuickBooks, Xero, Tally)? [system → migration effort]
- Are your books up to date, or are there months not yet recorded? How many? [catch-up months → back-period scope]
- Who currently handles your accounting — in-house, another firm, or no one? [transition effort]
- Do you have a chart of accounts already set up? [setup effort]

## MODULE D — VAT
- Are you registered for VAT? If yes, what is your TRN? [VAT status]
- Are your annual taxable supplies above, near, or below AED 375,000? [registration obligation — verify threshold with FTA]
- Do you file VAT monthly or quarterly? [filing frequency → recurring scope]
- Do you have any zero-rated or exempt supplies (e.g. exports, certain services)? [VAT complexity]
- Are your past VAT returns filed and up to date? Any penalties or notices? [remediation scope]

## MODULE E — Corporate Tax
- Are you registered for Corporate Tax on EmaraTax? [CT registration status]
- Have you filed a CT return before, or would this be your first? [first-return effort]
- Do you expect taxable income above AED 375,000 this period? [CT tier — verify with FTA]
- If free zone: do you believe you qualify as a Qualifying Free Zone Person? [QFZP scoping]
- Do you have related-party or connected-person transactions (e.g. with owners or group companies)? [transfer pricing disclosure]
- Could you use Small Business Relief — is revenue at or below AED 3M? [SBR eligibility — verify with FTA]

## MODULE F — Payroll & WPS
- How many employees are on payroll? [headcount → payroll tier]
- Do you pay salaries through WPS today? [WPS setup]
- Do you have UAE/GCC nationals requiring GPSSA contributions? [complexity]
- Do you need end-of-service gratuity and leave tracking? [scope]
- How often do you run payroll — monthly only, or with off-cycle runs? [frequency]

## MODULE G — Audit Support
- Do you need audited financial statements? If so, by when and for whom (bank, free zone, investor)? [audit-readiness scope, deadline]
- Do you already have an appointed auditor? [coordination]
- Have your financials been audited before? [first-audit effort]
- Are your records likely to be audit-ready, or do they need cleanup first? [pre-audit scope]

## MODULE H — Back-Period / Catch-Up
- How many months or years of bookkeeping need to be brought up to date? [catch-up volume]
- Do you have the source documents (invoices, bank statements) for those periods? [data availability]
- Are there any overdue filings or known penalties from past periods? [remediation scope]
- Is there a deadline forcing the catch-up (e.g. CT return, audit, loan)? [urgency]

## MODULE I — Deadlines & Urgency
- Are there any filing or compliance deadlines in the next 3 months? [prioritisation]
- When would you ideally want Finanshels to start? [onboarding timing]
- Is anything time-critical right now (a penalty notice, an investor request, a loan application)? [escalation]
