# Meeting Message Set — Fill-in Templates

Reusable templates for every message around a booked Finanshels discovery call.
Replace every `[bracketed slot]` with a genuinely known fact. Keep messages
short, warm, and founder-first. Never include a fee. A Finanshels team member
reviews before sending.

> Verify any tax figure against current FTA guidance. Last reviewed: 2026-05-18.

---

## 1. Booking confirmation — send immediately

**Email subject:** Confirmed: your call with Finanshels — [Date], [Time] GST

> Hi [First name],
>
> You're booked in. Here are the details:
>
> - **When:** [Day, Date] at [Time] GST
> - **Length:** [15-20] minutes
> - **Where:** [Video link / phone — we'll call you on [number]]
> - **Who you'll meet:** [Finanshels person, role]
>
> This is a no-obligation call. We'll get a quick picture of [company]'s setup
> and tell you what's actually relevant to you on [the hook — e.g. your
> Corporate Tax position]. No prep needed — it just helps if you have a rough
> idea of your revenue and whether you've registered for [CT / VAT].
>
> If anything changes, just reply and we'll move it.
>
> Talk soon,
> [SDR name], Finanshels

**WhatsApp version:**
> Hi [First name] — confirmed your call with Finanshels for [Day, Date], [Time]
> GST. [Length] mins, no obligation. [Link]. Reply here if you need to move it.

## Send-timing summary

| Message | Send timing | Default channel |
|---------|-------------|-----------------|
| Booking confirmation | Immediately after booking | Email + WhatsApp |
| Pre-call value primer | 24-48h before | Email |
| 24-hour reminder | 24h before | Email or WhatsApp |
| 1-hour reminder | 1h before | WhatsApp / SMS |
| No-show Touch 1 | Same day as missed call | Email or WhatsApp |
| No-show Touch 2 | +2 working days | Email |
| No-show Touch 3 | +5 working days | Email |
| Reschedule message | On request | Same channel as request |

## Rules for every message

- Never include a fee, retainer, or pricing hint.
- Use only approved proof points and approved client names.
- Skip reminders that would land outside reasonable hours or on a UAE holiday.
- Always allow an easy reply-to-change-or-cancel.
- Never guilt-trip a no-show — assume good faith.
- Keep tone warm, plain, founder-first; confident, never alarmist.
