# UAE Filing Deadlines — Quick Reference

> **Finanshels internal reference. Verify all dates against current FTA guidance before advising clients.**
> Last reviewed: 2026-05-18.
> Governing authority: Federal Tax Authority (FTA) | EmaraTax portal: https://eservices.tax.gov.ae

## Corporate Tax (CT)

Governing law: Federal Decree-Law No. 47 of 2022

| Obligation | Deadline | Notes |
|-----------|---------|-------|
| CT Registration on EmaraTax | Staggered by trade licence issuance month — confirm each entity's deadline with FTA/EmaraTax | Mandatory for all taxable persons |
| CT Return + Tax Payment | **9 months after the end of the tax period** | Example: FY ending 31 Dec 2024 → due **30 Sep 2025** |
| TP Disclosure Form | Filed together with the CT return | Required if related-party or connected-person transactions exist |
| Master File / Local File | Available to FTA on request; prepared before the CT return due date | Applies above prescribed thresholds — verify thresholds with FTA |
| CbCR notification | Varies — verify with FTA | Large MNE groups with consolidated revenue ≥ EUR 750M |

### CT Deadline Calculator

To calculate the CT return due date for any financial year:

```
CT Return Due Date = FY End Date + 9 months

Examples:
FY 01 Jan 2024 – 31 Dec 2024 → Due: 30 Sep 2025
FY 01 Apr 2024 – 31 Mar 2025 → Due: 31 Dec 2025
FY 01 Jun 2023 – 31 May 2024 → Due: 28 Feb 2025
FY 01 Jul 2023 – 30 Jun 2024 → Due: 31 Mar 2025
FY 01 Oct 2023 – 30 Sep 2024 → Due: 30 Jun 2025
```

Always verify the exact date with EmaraTax — the FTA may publish transitional guidance for first-year returns.

## VAT

Governing law: Federal Decree-Law No. 8 of 2017

| Obligation | Deadline | Notes |
|-----------|---------|-------|
| VAT Registration (mandatory) | When taxable supplies exceed AED 375,000 in the past 12 months or are expected to in the next 30 days | Late registration = significant FTA penalty |
| VAT Registration (voluntary) | Taxable supplies / expenses exceed AED 187,500 | Optional |
| VAT Return + Payment | **28th of the month following the end of the tax period** | Whether quarterly or monthly |
| VAT Refund Application | Filed via EmaraTax; no fixed deadline but process promptly | Subject to FTA verification |

### Standard Quarterly VAT Due Dates (Calendar-Year Filer)

| VAT Period | Period End | Return + Payment Due |
|-----------|-----------|---------------------|
| Q1 | 31 March | **28 April** |
| Q2 | 30 June | **28 July** |
| Q3 | 30 September | **28 October** |
| Q4 | 31 December | **28 January** |

> Note: Some entities are assigned non-calendar VAT quarters by the FTA. Confirm the client's VAT period on EmaraTax.

### Monthly VAT Filers

Return and payment due on the **28th of each following month** (e.g. January return due 28 February).

## Registration Deadlines Summary

| Registration | Trigger | Deadline | Portal |
|-------------|---------|---------|--------|
| CT Registration | Mandatory for all taxable persons | Staggered by licence-issuance month — verify with FTA | EmaraTax |
| VAT Registration (mandatory) | Taxable supplies > AED 375,000 | As soon as threshold is met or expected | EmaraTax |
| VAT Registration (voluntary) | Taxable supplies / expenses > AED 187,500 | At the entity's election | EmaraTax |
| Excise Tax Registration | If liable for excise tax | Before first excise activity | EmaraTax |

## Other Compliance Deadlines

| Obligation | Frequency / Trigger | Deadline | Authority |
|-----------|-------------------|---------|-----------|
| Audited Financial Statements | Annual | Required before CT return is filed; timing depends on auditor engagement | Auditor / FTA |
| UBO Register — Initial Filing | On incorporation | File with registrar promptly (verify with DED / free zone authority) | DED / Free Zone |
| UBO Register — Update | When any ownership change occurs | Within **15 days** of the change (verify with registrar) | DED / Free Zone |
| Shareholder Register | On incorporation; update on change | Promptly; verify timelines with registrar | DED / Free Zone |
| WPS Payroll | Monthly | Within the WPS cycle agreed with MOHRE — typically by the last day of the month | MOHRE |
| GPSSA Pension Contributions | Monthly | Deadline varies — confirm with GPSSA | GPSSA |
| AML/CFT — CDD Refresh | Risk-based | High-risk clients: annually; Medium-risk: every 3 years; Low-risk: every 5 years (confirm with goAML guidance) | FIU / goAML |
| AML/CFT — STR/SAR Filing | When suspicion arises | Promptly upon identification of suspicious activity — no fixed window | goAML portal |

## Key Rate Thresholds (for deadline triggering)

Cross-reference `../../_shared/finanshels-context.md` for full details.

| Threshold | Amount (AED) | Purpose |
|----------|-------------|---------|
| CT 0% band | 375,000 | Taxable income at 0% |
| CT 9% band | Above 375,000 | Taxable income above threshold |
| Small Business Relief | ≤ 3,000,000 revenue | Available for tax periods ending on/before 31 Dec 2026 |
| VAT mandatory registration | 375,000 | Taxable supplies in any 12-month period |
| VAT voluntary registration | 187,500 | Taxable supplies or expenses |
| Audited financials — mandatory | > 50,000,000 revenue | Also mandatory for QFZPs |
| Natural person CT | > 1,000,000 business turnover per calendar year | CT applies |
| Large MNE DMTT | ≥ EUR 750M consolidated group revenue | 15% DMTT applies; from FY starting on/after 1 Jan 2025 |
| IFRS for SMEs permitted | ≤ 50,000,000 revenue | IFRS for SMEs may be used |

## Penalty Reference (indicative only — verify with FTA)

| Violation | Indicative Penalty |
|-----------|-------------------|
| Late CT registration | AED 10,000 |
| Late CT return filing | AED 500/month (first year), AED 1,000/month thereafter — verify with FTA |
| Late VAT registration | AED 20,000 — verify with FTA |
| Late VAT return | AED 1,000 first offence, AED 2,000 subsequent — verify with FTA |
| Late VAT payment | 2% of unpaid tax immediately, plus 4% monthly — verify with FTA |
| Failure to maintain records | AED 10,000 first offence — verify with FTA |

> **All penalty figures are indicative and subject to change. Always confirm current amounts with the FTA penalty schedule on the FTA website or EmaraTax before communicating to clients.**
