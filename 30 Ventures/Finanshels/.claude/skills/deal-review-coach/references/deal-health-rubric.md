# Deal Health Rubric — UAE SME Sales Pipeline

> Reference for the `deal-review-coach` skill. Score each open deal on four
> dimensions (1-5 each), sum to a /20 health score, band it, then check stage
> fit. Score honestly — an inflated pipeline helps no one.

---

## The four health dimensions

### 1. Engagement (max 5)

How actively is the prospect participating?

| Score | Signal |
|-------|--------|
| 5 | Replies fast, asks questions, sends documents, drives the conversation |
| 4 | Responsive and warm, replies within a couple of days |
| 3 | Replies but slowly; you do most of the chasing |
| 2 | One-word replies, or only after several nudges |
| 1 | Gone silent — no reply to the last 2+ touches |

### 2. Fit (max 5)

How well does the prospect match the Finanshels ICP and service scope?

| Score | Signal |
|-------|--------|
| 5 | UAE SME, AED 0.5-10M revenue, clear multi-service need, in scope |
| 4 | Good UAE fit, AED 10-50M or single strong service need |
| 3 | UAE entity but small/early, or unclear scope |
| 2 | Borderline — offshore-heavy, or scope edges Finanshels' range |
| 1 | Poor fit — no real UAE compliance need, or beyond scope (Big-4-level work) |

### 3. Urgency (max 5)

Is there a real reason to act now?

| Score | Signal |
|-------|--------|
| 5 | Hard deadline weeks away — CT return due, VAT penalty, audit, fundraise diligence |
| 4 | Clear near-term trigger — first CT filing, behind on books, year-end approaching |
| 3 | Wants it sorted but no specific deadline |
| 2 | "Someday" — exploring, no felt pressure |
| 1 | No urgency at all; happy with current setup |

### 4. Decision access (max 5)

Can you reach and influence the person who decides?

| Score | Signal |
|-------|--------|
| 5 | Founder / CFO directly engaged, decision path confirmed |
| 4 | Founder / CFO engaged, decision path mostly clear |
| 3 | Finance manager engaged, decision-maker aware but not in the room |
| 2 | Gatekeeper only; decision-maker not yet reached |
| 1 | No idea who decides, or decision-maker is unreachable |

---

## Health score bands

Sum the four dimensions (max 20):

| Score | Band | Meaning |
|-------|------|---------|
| 16-20 | **Healthy** | Real, advancing — protect and progress it |
| 11-15 | **Watch** | Live but soft on one or two dimensions — strengthen the weak spot |
| 6-10 | **At Risk** | Drifting — needs one decisive action or it dies |
| <= 5 | **Stalled** | Effectively dead — recover with one clear attempt, else close-lost |

---

## Stage-fit check

A deal's stage on the board must match its evidence. Flag mismatches.

| Stated stage | Expected evidence | Flag if... |
|--------------|-------------------|------------|
| Discovery booked | A call is scheduled | No call on the calendar |
| Discovery done | A clean debrief exists | No debrief; not scored |
| Proposal sent | Sent < 10 days ago, prospect engaging | Sent 14+ days ago, silent — not "live proposal", it is At Risk |
| Verbal yes | Explicit intent + a path to signature | "Verbal yes" with no contact in 2 weeks — downgrade |
| Negotiation | Active back-and-forth on scope | No movement in 10+ days |

**Rule:** a deal cannot sit in an active stage while its activity says otherwise.
Re-band it on evidence, not hope.

---

## Risk reason taxonomy

When flagging an At Risk or Stalled deal, name the specific reason:

- **Gone quiet** — no reply to recent touches
- **No decision-maker access** — stuck with a gatekeeper
- **No real urgency** — nothing forcing a decision
- **Unresolved objection** — a price/trust/timing blocker not addressed
- **Competitor winning** — another option is gaining ground
- **Scope drift** — the ask keeps changing; no firm definition
- **Poor fit** — should not have been qualified in

## Next-action routing

Match the diagnosis to the right sibling skill:

| Diagnosis | Route to |
|-----------|----------|
| Gone quiet after a proposal | `sales-followup-sequencer` |
| An objection is the blocker | `objection-handling-playbook` |
| A competitor is in the deal | `competitive-battlecard` |
| Decision-maker never engaged | `discovery-call-guide` (re-engagement call) |
| Scope unclear / needs re-scoping | `engagement-budget-calculator`, `proposal-generator` |
| New lead not yet qualified | `prospect-qualification` |
| Poor fit confirmed | Close-lost with reason; graceful exit |

---

## Prioritisation lens

Rank where to spend time:

1. **High priority:** Healthy or Watch + high value band + a clear next action that advances it.
2. **Medium priority:** Watch deals needing one decisive action; At Risk deals worth one recovery attempt.
3. **Low priority / close-lost:** Stalled deals with no path, or poor-fit deals. Close them so attention frees up.

A focused rep working five real deals beats a rep nursing fifteen ghosts.
