# ZeroHuman — Agent Context

> Read this before doing any work on ZeroHuman.

## One-paragraph snapshot

ZeroHuman is an **AI-native marketing and demand engine company** targeting UAE/GCC service categories. Two roles inside the portfolio: (1) **distribution arm** that cross-applies to the rest of Meet's ventures, (2) **standalone agency-as-service** for external clients. Bet: most UAE service businesses are *under-served* by traditional agencies and *over-served* by AI tools without operator competence — ZeroHuman sits in between, offering operator-led AI-native demand-gen as a service.

## Why it matters (to Meet)

- Distribution leverage across the portfolio — every other venture can plug into ZeroHuman for growth
- Cash-generating from day one (services revenue)
- A real-world testbed for AI agent workflows that later become StartupOS features
- UAE-specific GTM advantage Meet has direct context for

## Current state (refresh quarterly)

- Stage: build / positioning
- Team: small / founder-led, first ops hire targeted
- Status: positioning crisp needed, first 3 lighthouse clients to land
- Service lines: TBD (currently three plausible — see strategic priorities)

## ICP (current thinking)

- UAE-based service businesses: clinics, salons, restaurants, fitness, professional services (legal, accounting, real estate brokerages)
- Revenue band: $500k–$5M ARR — too big for hand-cranked owner-operator growth, too small to hire a real CMO
- Geography: Dubai + Abu Dhabi first, Sharjah/RAK opportunistically
- Pain triggers: stuck growth, owner doing marketing on weekends, tried agencies and got burned

## Current strategic priorities

1. **Positioning crisp + 3 service-line one-pagers** — what we sell, what we don't, what the buyer gets
2. **Land first 3 lighthouse clients** — proof of model before scaling sales
3. **Define the agency-as-service operating model** — what's productized, what's bespoke, what's pricing
4. **Internal AI agent stack** — what tools/agents we use to deliver (and how this becomes IP)

## Voice + brand

- **Pragmatic, results-led, no-BS.** Not "creative agency" energy. Not "growth hacker" energy.
- Speaks to owner-operators who have been disappointed before
- Visual register: clean, business-grade, photo of real work over abstract design
- Brand neighbors in tone: McKinsey-but-affordable, GrowthLab early, Bessemer's portfolio aesthetic

## What NOT to do on ZeroHuman work

- Don't sell "AI-powered marketing" as the headline. Sell **outcomes** — leads, customers, revenue — with AI as the cost-structure innovation under the hood.
- Don't price by hours — price by outcome or retainer
- Don't take on every category — service businesses with high LTV first
- Don't compete with traditional agencies on creative awards — compete on results
