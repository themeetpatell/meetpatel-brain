# Market Segmentation

### Primary Segments (Year 1 Focus)

**1. E-commerce Brands (SMB to Mid-Market)**

- **Size**: 5,000+ potential customers in GCC
- **Pain Points**:
    - Need Arabic video ads for product launches
    - High production costs ($5K-20K per ad)
    - Slow turnaround (weeks to months)
    - Limited Arabic content creation tools
- **Value Prop**: Create Arabic video ads in 60 seconds, 90% cost reduction
- **Channels**: Direct sales, digital marketing, e-commerce platform partnerships
- **Target**: 500 customers in Year 1

**2. Marketing Agencies (Mid-Market to Enterprise)**

- **Size**: 200+ agencies in GCC
- **Pain Points**:
    - Client demand for Arabic video content
    - Need to scale creative production
    - High production costs eating into margins
    - Competition from international agencies
- **Value Prop**: White-label solution, scale production 10x, higher margins
- **Channels**: Direct sales, partnerships, industry events
- **Target**: 50 agencies in Year 1

**3. Fashion & Lifestyle Brands (Mid-Market)**

- **Size**: 1,000+ brands in GCC
- **Pain Points**:
    - Seasonal campaigns require rapid content creation
    - Cultural sensitivity (modesty, regional preferences)
    - High production costs for multiple SKUs
    - Need diverse representation
- **Value Prop**: Culturally appropriate ads, rapid iteration, cost-effective
- **Channels**: Direct sales, fashion industry events, influencer partnerships
- **Target**: 200 customers in Year 1

**4. Real Estate Developers (Enterprise)**

- **Size**: 500+ developers in GCC
- **Pain Points**:
    - Project launches require video marketing
    - High production costs per project
    - Need Arabic and English content
    - Government compliance requirements
- **Value Prop**: Rapid project launch videos, compliance-ready, bilingual
- **Channels**: Direct sales, real estate associations, government partnerships
- **Target**: 50 customers in Year 1
