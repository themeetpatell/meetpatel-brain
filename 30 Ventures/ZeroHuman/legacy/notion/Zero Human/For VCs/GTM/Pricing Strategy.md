# Pricing Strategy

## SaaS Pricing Tiers

### Starter Plan: $99/month

- **Target**: SMBs, freelancers
- **Features**:
    - 10 video ads/month
    - Basic AI Co-Director
    - 1080p quality
    - Arabic + English support
    - Basic analytics
- **Value Prop**: "Create 10 Arabic video ads for $99 vs. $10K traditional cost"

### Professional Plan: $299/month

- **Target**: Mid-market, agencies
- **Features**:
    - 50 video ads/month
    - Advanced AI Co-Director
    - 4K quality
    - Arabic + English support
    - Advanced analytics
    - Brand guidelines
    - Team collaboration (5 users)
- **Value Prop**: "Scale production 10x, save $50K+ per quarter"

### Enterprise Plan: Custom ($5K-50K/month)

- **Target**: Large brands, agencies, government
- **Features**:
    - Unlimited video ads
    - Custom AI Co-Director training
    - 4K quality
    - All languages
    - Enterprise analytics
    - Custom integrations
    - Dedicated support
    - SLA guarantees
- **Value Prop**: "Enterprise-grade solution with custom AI training"

### Marketplace Pricing

### For Creators (Sellers)

- **Free**: List up to 10 ads
- **Pro**: $49/month - List unlimited ads, featured placement
- **Commission**: 20% on sales, 30% on rentals

### For Buyers

- **Buy**: $50-500 per ad (based on performance)
- **Rent**: $10-50 per ad per month
- **License**: $100-1,000 per ad (extended license)

### Premium Features

- **Featured Listings**: $50-200/month
- **Performance Analytics**: $10-50/month
- **Creator Verification**: Free for top performers
