# Metrics & KPIs

### Acquisition Metrics

- **Monthly Active Users (MAU)**: Target 5,000 by Month 6
- **New Customers**: Target 100/month by Month 6
- **CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)**: Target $300 (SMB), $1,000 (Enterprise)
- **CAC Payback Period**: Target 3 months

### Engagement Metrics

- **Video Ads Created**: Target 1,000/month by Month 6
- **Marketplace Listings**: Target 10,000 ads by Month 6
- **Marketplace Transactions**: Target 500/month by Month 6
- **User Retention**: Target 80% Month 1, 70% Month 3

### Revenue Metrics

- **ARR (Annual Recurring Revenue)**: Target $1M by Month 6, $3M by Month 12
- **MRR Growth**: Target 20% month-over-month
- **Average Revenue Per User (ARPU)**: Target $200/month
- **Marketplace GMV**: Target $50K/month by Month 6
- **Marketplace Commission Revenue**: Target $10K/month by Month 6

### Product Metrics

- **Arabic Content Generation Rate**: Target 80%+
- **Generation Time**: Target <90 seconds average
- **Customer Satisfaction (NPS)**: Target 50+
- **Feature Adoption**: Target 60%+ for key features
