# Marketing Channels & Tactics

### 1. Digital Marketing (40% of budget)

### Paid Advertising

- **LinkedIn Ads** (Primary):
    - Target: Marketing directors, founders, CMOs in GCC
    - Budget: $10K/month
    - Creative: Case studies, product demos, Arabic content
    - KPIs: 50 leads/month, $500 CAC
- **Google Ads** (Primary):
    - Target: Arabic keywords (إنشاء إعلانات فيديو, إعلانات فيديو بالذكاء الاصطناعي)
    - Budget: $8K/month
    - Creative: Landing pages, demo videos
    - KPIs: 100 leads/month, $300 CAC
- **Meta Ads** (Secondary):
    - Target: E-commerce founders, marketers (25-45, GCC)
    - Budget: $5K/month
    - Creative: Video demos, testimonials
    - KPIs: 200 leads/month, $100 CAC
- **TikTok Ads** (Secondary):
    - Target: Creators, marketers (18-35, GCC)
    - Budget: $3K/month
    - Creative: Behind-the-scenes, creator stories
    - KPIs: 150 leads/month, $80 CAC

### Content Marketing

- **Blog** (Arabic + English):
    - 4 posts/month (2 Arabic, 2 English)
    - Topics: AI video trends, GCC marketing, case studies
    - SEO focus: Arabic keywords
- **Video Content**:
    - YouTube channel (Arabic + English)
    - 2 videos/month (tutorials, case studies)
    - TikTok/Instagram Reels (daily)
- **Case Studies**:
    - 2 per month (GCC customers)
    - Arabic + English versions
    - Performance data included

### Email Marketing

- **Newsletter**: Monthly (Arabic + English)
- **Drip Campaigns**: Onboarding, feature updates
- **Nurture Sequences**: By persona and industry

### 2. Direct Sales (30% of budget)

### Sales Team Structure

- **Month 1-3**: 2 sales reps (Saudi, UAE)
- **Month 4-6**: 4 sales reps (add Kuwait, Qatar)
- **Month 7-12**: 6 sales reps (add enterprise, government)

### Sales Process

1. **Outreach**: LinkedIn, email, cold calls
2. **Discovery**: Understand pain points, Arabic content needs
3. **Demo**: Live product demo (Arabic + English)
4. **Trial**: 14-day free trial
5. **Close**: Pricing negotiation, contract signing
6. **Onboard**: Customer success handoff

### Sales Targets

- **SMB**: 20 deals/month per rep ($500-2K ARR each)
- **Mid-Market**: 5 deals/month per rep ($2K-10K ARR each)
- **Enterprise**: 1 deal/month per rep ($10K-50K ARR each)

### 3. Partnerships (20% of budget)

### Agency Partnerships

- **Target**: Top 50 GCC agencies
- **Model**: White-label, revenue share (20-30%)
- **Support**: Dedicated account manager, training, co-marketing
- **Goal**: 20 agencies in Year 1

### E-commerce Platform Partnerships

- **Noon**: Native integration, co-marketing
- [**Amazon.ae**](http://amazon.ae/): Seller tools integration
- **Namshi, Sivvi, Ounass**: Brand partnerships
- **Goal**: 3 platform partnerships in Year 1

### Technology Partnerships

- **Shopify, WooCommerce**: App marketplace
- **Canva, Figma**: Plugin integrations
- **Meta, Google, TikTok**: Ad platform partnerships
- **Goal**: 5 technology partnerships in Year 1

### Government Partnerships

- **Saudi Vision 2030**: Initiative partnerships
- **UAE AI Strategy**: Program participation
- **Government Entities**: Direct contracts
- **Goal**: 2 government partnerships in Year 1
