## Content Templates

### Blog Post Template

1. **Headline**: Compelling, keyword-rich
2. **Introduction**: Hook, problem statement
3. **Body**: 3-5 key points with examples
4. **Conclusion**: CTA (trial, demo, download)
5. **CTA**: Clear next step

### Social Media Post Template

1. **Hook**: Attention-grabbing first line
2. **Value**: Key benefit or insight
3. **Visual**: Image or video
4. **CTA**: Link, comment, share
5. **Hashtags**: 3-5 relevant hashtags

### Email Template

1. **Subject**: Clear, benefit-focused
2. **Greeting**: Personalized
3. **Value**: Key benefit or update
4. **CTA**: Clear next step
5. **Signature**: Name, title, contact

## Crisis Management

### Negative Feedback

1. **Acknowledge**: Respond quickly, acknowledge concern
2. **Investigate**: Understand the issue
3. **Resolve**: Fix the problem, offer solution
4. **Follow-up**: Ensure satisfaction
5. **Learn**: Improve product/process

### Competitor Attacks

1. **Stay Calm**: Don't engage in negative tactics
2. **Focus on Value**: Emphasize our unique value
3. **Customer Success**: Focus on customer wins
4. **Differentiate**: Highlight marketplace, Arabic support, GCC focus
