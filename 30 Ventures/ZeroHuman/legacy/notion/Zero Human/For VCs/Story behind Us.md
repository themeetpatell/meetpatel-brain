## **The 5-minute story behind us…**

## I'm going to tell you about a moment that changed everything for me.

Two years ago, I was running Neuractory, an AI real-estate startup. I had 300+ clients, great product, but I realized something: every one of my customers was drowning in the same problem. They wanted to make video ads. Instagram, TikTok, YouTube—everyone's moving to video. But here's the brutal truth: a single professional video costs $1,500 to $5,000 and takes 6 to 8 weeks to produce.

So what happened? 90% of small businesses just... didn't make videos. They couldn't afford it. They couldn't wait that long. They were locked out of the fastest-growing ad channel on the planet.

I spent $20K on a single real-estate demo video. And I watched SMBs—entrepreneurs who were just as talented, just as driven as me—sit on the sidelines because they couldn't drop five grand on an ad.

That stuck with me.

## **(Why Now?)**

"Then something changed. Sora came out. Runway came out. Gradually, AI could generate cinematic-quality video. And I thought: this is it. I studied, researched, worked on it for a few years behinf the tech and market and understood this is the moment.

But here's what I noticed: these tools are blank canvases with hundreds and thousands of generations and prompting to make a full meaningful video. You need to write a perfect prompt, understand shot composition, music theory, color grading. They're tools *for* filmmakers, not *for* SMBs or entrepreneur who want to focus on building business than on tech thats time consuming, manual at the cost of budget and time.

So I asked a different question: What if we didn't build a blank canvas? What if we built an AI **Co-Director**?

Think about it. When you hire a real director for $5K, what are you paying for? You're not paying them to hold a camera. You're paying for their *judgment*. Their taste. Their ability to say: 'For this product, at this time of day, in this market, we need THIS shot, THIS music, THIS pacing.' That's what separates a $500 video from a $50K video—not execution, but *strategy*."

## **The Product (The Magic)**

So we built Zero Human.

A founder says: 'I need a luxury watch ad for Gen-Z on TikTok. The brand is minimalist, premium, aspirational.'

Our AI Co-Director:

- Reads that brief
- Recommends a script (based on thousands of high-performing watch ads)
- Designs a multi-shot cinematic sequence (close-up on the dial, wrist reveal, lifestyle moment, product in context)
- Selects music (ambient, modern, not cheesy)
- Optimizes pacing for short-form attention
- Generates it all in 4K
- Adds English lip-sync and localization if needed (working on more languages…)

And all of this? 90 seconds. From voice to final ad.

Our beta users said: 'This is what I would have paid an agency $3K for, and I got it for nearly free in 2 minutes.'

We're not replacing directors. We're replacing the $1K-$5K barrier that keeps 90% of SMBs out of video advertising in this digitally adopted world.

## **The Business Model (How We Make Money)**
