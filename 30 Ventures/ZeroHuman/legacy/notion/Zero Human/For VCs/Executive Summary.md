**Market**: GCC digital advertising market projected to reach $3.5B by 2027, with video advertising representing $1.2B+ and growing at 28% CAGR. The creative economy marketplace (buy/sell/rent ads) represents an additional $150M+ opportunity in GCC.

**Problem**: 78% of GCC SMBs cite video production cost and Arabic content creation complexity as barriers to effective digital marketing. 65% of businesses want to access pre-made high-performing ads without production overhead.

**Solution**: Zero Human—voice-directed AI with native Arabic support that creates professional 4K video ads in 60 seconds, plus a marketplace where creators monetize ads and businesses buy/sell/rent high-performing video ads

### What We Do

Zero Human is the world's first voice-directed cinematic AI engine **and ad marketplace**. Businesses simply speak their vision, and our AI Co-Director creates fully customized, broadcast-quality 4K video ads in under 60 seconds—no cameras, actors, or production teams needed. Beyond creation, Zero Human operates as a two-sided marketplace where creators can monetize their AI-generated ads and businesses can buy, sell, or rent high-performing video ads—creating a new creative economy for the advertising industry.

**Key Features**:

- Voice-to-video generation (60-90 seconds) with native Arabic support
