### **Use cases**

**1. E-commerce and Retail**

**Virtual Try-Ons**

- **Problem:** Customers hesitate to purchase online due to uncertainty about fit and appearance.
- **Solution:** AI models simulate how clothes look on diverse body types, enhancing the shopping experience.
- **Impact:** Increases customer confidence, reduces returns, and boosts sales.

**Personalized Marketing**

- **Problem**: Generic advertisements fail to engage diverse audiences.
- **Solution**: Use AI models to create personalized ads that reflect the diversity of the customer base.
- **Impact**: Higher engagement rates and improved customer loyalty.

**2. Advertising and Marketing**

**Diverse Ad Campaigns**

- **Problem**: Lack of diversity in advertising fails to connect with all audience segments.
- **Solution**: Create inclusive ad campaigns using AI-generated models representing various demographics.
- **Impact**: Improved audience connection, brand reputation, and campaign effectiveness.

**Rapid Campaign Iteration**

- **Problem**: Long lead times for photoshoots delay campaign launches.
- **Solution**: Quickly generate and customize models for different campaigns.
- **Impact**: Faster time-to-market and greater agility in responding to trends.

**3. Entertainment and Media**

**Character Development**

- **Problem**: Limited diversity in characters reduces audience engagement.
- **Solution**: Create a wide range of diverse, lifelike characters for films, video games, and virtual reality.
- **Impact**: Enhanced storytelling and broader audience appeal.

**Interactive Experiences**

- **Problem**: Static content lacks engagement.
- Solution: Develop interactive, AI-driven characters for immersive media experiences.
- **Impact**: Increased viewer engagement and satisfaction.

**4. Healthcare and Wellness**

**Telehealth Avatars**

- **Problem**: Limited personal interaction in telehealth services.
- **Solution**: Use AI-generated patient avatars for remote consultations and diagnostics.
- **Impact**: Improved patient-doctor interaction and diagnostic accuracy.

**Diverse Patient Simulations**

- **Problem**: Training materials lack representation of diverse patient cases.
- **Solution**: Develop diverse patient avatars for medical training and practice.
- **Impact**: Better preparedness for treating a diverse patient population.
