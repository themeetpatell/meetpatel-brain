# Limited Personalization Options in Current Market

Category: Customer Experience
Consequences: Missed opportunities to deeply resonate with target audiences; campaigns lack uniqueness and fail to stand out in a crowded market.
Current Process: Brands often rely on a limited pool of human models with predefined attributes, restricting creativity and personalization.
For Whom: Retail, E-commerce
Impact: Brands struggle to find models that perfectly match their unique vision, leading to generic campaigns.
No of Occurrences: Frequently
Opportunity: Personalized marketing, enhanced brand identity.

Empowers brands to push the boundaries of creative expression, leading to more impactful and memorable campaigns.
Probable Solution: Head-to-toe customizable AI models

Developing AI models with advanced emotional expressiveness and personalization capabilities can help bridge the gap, creating digital humans that resonate on an emotional level
Urgency / Impact Level: Very High
Why?: Traditional modeling limits creativity and fails to meet the growing demand for highly personalized, brand-specific content in a competitive marketplace.
