## Main Questions

1. **Introduce yourself/company/the role**
2. **How did you two work together?**
    - 
3. **What are the times you’ve been most impressed with this person?**
    - 
4. **Can you describe this person’s communication style with other team members?**
    - 
5. **What are the top 3 areas this person has an opportunity to grow?**
    - 
    - 
    - 
6. **Do you have any advice on how we can set this person up to be successful if they join? Specifically, what areas can we make sure to provide support/guidance in the first few months?**
    - 
7. **Would you hire / work with this person again?**
    - 
8. **Is there anything else you’d like to mention?**
    - 
9. **Thank the references!**

### Additionally

- **Ask role specific questions:**
    - If they’re an engineering candidate, ask about technical skills or how they communicate with non-technical team members.
- **Ask value specific questions:**
    - Do you need someone who is biased towards action?
    - Someone who is a team player?
    - Someone who can be independent?
