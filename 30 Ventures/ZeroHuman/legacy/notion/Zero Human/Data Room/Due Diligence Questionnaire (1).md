# Due Diligence Questionnaire (1)

This is a sample list of questions. This should be a rolling list of questions you hear from LPs.

| **Idea + Market Pull** | **Why is now the right time to start this startup?** Why couldn't this business have succeeded 24-48 months ago? What newly made it possible whereas formidable entrants couldn't exist before? If there's nothing new, incumbents would have line-extended into here already. |
| --- | --- |
| **Idea + Market Pull** | **Find out if they're thorough**: Do they have a full understanding of the problem? - Can you describe the history of this idea and how it builds on prior attempts to solve this problem? - Ask them the why behind their decisions to help them go deeper. |
| **Idea + Market Pull** | **What is “hard” about what they’re trying to build?** |
| **Idea + Market Pull** | **Do they have basic risk awareness?** What are the biggest risks? Where does this fail? And what are you doing to mitigate each risk. |
| **Economics** | **Does the company understand its unit economics?** How confident are they, and how correctly so, in their understanding of which parts of the business have operating leverage? |
| **Economics** | **Is it big enough?** Is the addressable market big enough to support at least $200M/year in revenue? |
| **Economics** | **Pace of movement and capital efficiency** (e.g. how far can they can on little capital). |
| **Economics** | **How capital intensive will this business be over time?** |
| **Economics** | **Sanity check margin potential over time**—will they get squeezed and are they raising enough money. In today's market, it's important they have a path to breakeven before running out of cash. |
| **Team** | **Are the founders driven to do this for a long time**; is this the last business they’ll ever start. Is starting this company the natural culmination of what the founder has been working toward in the years leading up to incorporation? |
| **Team** | **Do the founders seem unstoppable?** Find out if they're relentless: Probe what they're doing to keep learning their market and iterate faster. |
| **Team** | **I would tell my best friend to join this team.** |
| **Team** | **Are they desperate for success or for their product to exist or are they larping as founders?** |
| **Team** | **Can the founder(s) both build and sell?** |
| **Speed** | **Are they moving fast?** What do you want to get done by the end of the year? |
