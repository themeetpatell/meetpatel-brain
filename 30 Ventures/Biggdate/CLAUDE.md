# Biggdate — Agent Context

> Read this before doing any work on Biggdate. Pair with the `biggdate-brand-voice` and `biggdate-social-image` skills for any output.

## One-paragraph snapshot

Biggdate is a **high-intent dating product** — better-fit connections, emotional resonance, stronger long-term relationship outcomes. Premium brand perception, sharp voice, trust-led onboarding, creator/community-driven growth. The thesis is that the dating category has been optimized for casual / quantity / superficial filtering, and there is room for a brand that takes the work of finding a real partner seriously — without becoming "matchmaking app" cringe.

## Why it matters (to Meet)

- A consumer brand bet in a category Meet has thought about deeply (relationship intelligence, behavioral matching, trust architecture)
- Complements the B2B/SaaS cluster (Finanshels, StartupOS, ZeroHuman) with a consumer + brand surface
- The brand voice + creative discipline required for Biggdate sharpens Meet's overall craft

## Current state (refresh quarterly)

- Stage: build / brand activation
- Team: small / founder-led
- 22 legacy files including Positioning, Brand Voice, Growth System, Product Thesis, Trust & Safety, Database, Pitch, Launch Plan, 10-D Compatibility Framework
- Brand voice already drafted in legacy — needs activation, not re-drafting

## ICP (current thinking)

- Adults 25–40 actively wanting a long-term partner, exhausted by Hinge/Bumble/Tinder
- Premium aesthetic: would pay for quality, not free-with-ads
- Tech-comfortable but not necessarily early-adopter
- Sub-segments: working professionals, founders/operators, post-divorce, returnees from arranged-match disappointment
- Geography start: UAE + Indian diaspora globally, then UK/US

## Current strategic priorities

1. **Trust & Safety architecture** — category permission to win. Best-in-class verification, harm prevention, response to incidents.
2. **Brand voice activation** — visible voice across product, social, comms (use `biggdate-brand-voice` skill)
3. **Competitive Atlas build** — full dossiers on Hinge, Bumble, Match, Boo, Wisp, Hily, Aisle
4. **Creator/community growth motion** — define the founder-led + creator-amplified acquisition loop
5. **Decide: Soulmap as standalone or as Biggdate layer** (decision needed Q3)

## Voice + brand

- **Premium, emotional, sharp.** Not horny. Not coach-y. Not sterile.
- Speaks to adults who have been around the block and are tired of the games
- Visual register: editorial, generous use of white space, photography over illustration, restrained color
- Hard nos: emojis as personality (allowed as quiet accent), gen-Z slang, "👀" or "🔥" energy, "we're not just a dating app" cringe
- Brand neighbors in tone: Aesop, Le Labo, Hinge's early branding, Apple's relationship-focused ads

## What NOT to do on Biggdate work

- Don't market the AI. The AI is invisible infrastructure, not the brand promise.
- Don't compare directly to competitors in public — sharpen the positioning, but punch up only on category dynamics, never on named competitors
- Don't write copy that could be on every other dating app — anonymity-test every draft
- Don't ship anything that could compromise user safety for speed (trust is the moat — once broken, gone)
