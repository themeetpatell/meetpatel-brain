# Pitch

**1) Title / Vision**

- BiggMate — “AI co-founder that ships your startup with you.”
- Vision: Compress the time from idea to revenue from months to days for every builder.

**2) Problem**

- Solo founders and lean teams lose 70% of time on context-switching: research, outreach, ops, PM, and content.
- Building momentum (customers, product, capital) stalls because execution is fragmented and slow.
- Existing tools are siloed; AI copilots are chatty but not accountable for outcomes.

**3) Solution**

- BiggMate: An outcome-driven AI co-founder that plans, builds, and executes across product, GTM, and ops.
- Unified workspace with autonomous agents + human-in-the-loop: launches campaigns, drafts product specs, manages sprints, handles outreach, and tracks metrics end-to-end.
- “Done-for-you” loops: idea → spec → build tasks → outreach → feedback → iteration, all in one place.

**4) Product (Today)**

- Modules: AI Discovery, GTM & outreach, Sprint & PM, Pitch/Investor CRM, Stakeholder CRM, Team workspace.
- Autonomy: Agents execute on targets (e.g., book 10 calls, ship v1 landing, run user interviews), not just answer questions.
- Integrations: Email, Calendly, Slack, Notion/Jira, CRM; shipping hooks for code and content.
- UX: Founder cockpit with live pipelines (customers, stakeholders, tasks, revenue).

**5) Why Now**

- AI agent reliability crossed a usability threshold; infra costs falling.
- Explosion of solo/remote founders; “agentic SaaS” still unsolved for execution and accountability.
- Enterprises also seeking faster venture-building and GTM motion.

**6) Market**

- Beachhead: 10M+ solo/lean founders & indie hackers; $15B+ productivity/GTM TAM.
- Expansion: corporate venture studios, accelerators, agencies → $50B+ adjacent services ripe for automation.
- Bottom-up adoption; land small, expand to teams and ventures.

- Early revenue: $X ARR; MoM growth: XX%.
- Case studies: [Founder A] shipped MVP + 15 intro calls in 10 days; [Team B] closed pilot after AI-led outreach.

**8) Business Model**

- SaaS: $49–$149/user/month for solo & lean teams.
- Usage-based credits for heavy agent actions (multi-channel outreach, long runs).
- Enterprise/Studio plans: seats + outcome-based fees (per launch/per pipeline).

**9) Moat**

- Outcome graph: tasks, contacts, pipelines, code/content linked to measurable goals.
- Proprietary playbooks (GTM, fundraising, product sprints) that self-improve across customers.
- Data/feedback loops: model-tuning on execution traces, not just chat.
- Integrations & switching cost: lived-in workspace with pipelines, not standalone chat.

**10) Competition**

- General copilots (ChatGPT, Gemini): broad but non-owning, non-operational.
- Point tools (Notion, Jira, Apollo, HubSpot, ClickUp): siloed; no autonomous orchestration.
- Agent startups: few with integrated workspace + accountability; most are narrow or services-heavy.
- Differentiator: outcome-ownership + full-stack workflow + measurable KPIs.

**11) Go-To-Market**

- Wedge: solo/lean founders and micro-studios; free-to-try workspace, paid for execution.
- Channels: founder communities, YC/OnDeck/Builders, indie hacker platforms, product hunts; referral from success cases.
- Sales-assist for studios/agencies; partnerships with accelerators to power cohorts.

**12) Team & Ask**

- Team: Founder/CEO (you) — repeat builder/operator; supporting team: AI/ML lead, PM/ops, GTM.
- Ask: $X seed at $Y pre; 18–24 months runway to 0→$2–$3M ARR; major milestones: 10K paid users or 300 studios; 50%+ tasks automated end-to-end.
