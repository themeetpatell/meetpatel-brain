## **DAILY NON-NEGOTIABLES (Every Single Day)**

These happen **daily**, regardless of week theme:

- 1 founder LinkedIn post (from you)
- 10–15 meaningful comments on founder posts
- Reply to every comment on your post
- 20–30 founder DMs (value-first, no pitch)
- Track waitlist + follower delta nightly

## **JANUARY — WAITLIST + BELIEF BUILDING**

**Goal by Jan 31**

- 5,000–10,000 waitlist signups
- 3,000+ LinkedIn followers (founder + brand combined)
- 300–500 highly engaged “core believers”
- BiggMate becomes a *known name* in founder circles

### **Day 1–2**

This becomes:

- Every post’s undertone

**Waitlist landing page only**

    >

## **JAN PHASE 2 — DAILY FOUNDER CONTENT ENGINE (Jan 6–31)**

This is the **engine**. No ads required.

### **Daily LinkedIn cadence (Mon–Sat)**

**1 post per day from YOU (not brand page)**

Rotate between these formats:

### **Format A — Founder truth**

Examples:

- “The biggest startup risk is not product. It’s people.”
- “A bad cofounder decision kills faster than no funding.”
- “Most founders don’t fail. They quit after choosing wrong partners.”

Short. Punchy. No fluff.

### **Format B — Data-backed insight**

Use pitch stats:

- Solo founders success rates
- Accelerator bias against solo founders
- Time wasted in networking

End every post with:

> Building something for founders who don’t want to build alone.
> 

### **Format C — Story / observation**

- Something you observed in founders
- Something you personally experienced
- Something painful but real

No pitching. Only resonance.

---

### **Weekly content rhythm**

- **Mon–Thu:** Thought posts
- **Fri:** Question post
    - “If you had to find a cofounder today, what would scare you most?”
- **Sat:** Screenshot of waitlist growth / DMs / comments (social proof)

**DM strategy (daily 15–20 DMs)**

No follow-ups unless they reply.

---

### **Jan 20–31**

**Community amplification**

    - “This is exactly what I needed.”

Post as screenshots.
