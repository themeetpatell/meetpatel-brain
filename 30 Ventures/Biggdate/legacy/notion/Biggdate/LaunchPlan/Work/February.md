# February

# **FEB 1–13: D-14 PRE-LAUNCH**

**Goal:** Anticipation + readiness

This phase converts belief into **intent**.

---

## **PRE-LAUNCH MOVES**

### **1. THE BIG PROMISE (Not Features)**

Communicate clearly:

> “BiggMate helps you find someone you can actually build with.”
> 

No demos yet.

No screenshots yet.

Hold the reveal.

---

### **2. EARLY ACCESS SELECTION**

- Email selected founders
- Make rejection polite and respectful
- Scarcity builds credibility

---

### **3. SOCIAL PROOF DRIP**

- Quotes from early users
- Insights learned from founder data
- “We noticed something interesting…”

---

### **4. COUNTDOWN ENERGY**

- 14 days
- Every day reveals:
    - A belief
    - A founder mistake
    - A hard truth

# **FEB 14: LAUNCH DAY**

**Goal:** Moment, not noise

### **Launch Day Structure**

- Founder post: calm, confident, inevitable
- BiggMate post: simple, direct, welcoming
- Email to waitlist
- Founder circle gets access first

No discounts.

No hype words.

Confidence beats excitement.

---

# **FEB 15–28: POST-LAUNCH (D+14)**

**Goal:** Flywheel ignition

This phase turns users into advocates.

---

## **POST-LAUNCH FLYWHEEL**

### **1. Founder Wins**

- Share first matches
- Share stories
- Celebrate quietly but consistently

### **2. Feedback → Build → Share**

- “We heard you”
- “We shipped this”
- Public learning builds trust

### **3. Content Evolves**

- From belief → proof
- From theory → outcomes

### **4. Community First**

- Founder circle expands
- Power users invited deeper

---

# **WHAT MAKES THIS UNICORN-GRADE**

1. Founder-led trust beats paid reach
2. Scarcity builds seriousness
3. Community creates gravity
4. Belief compounds faster than features
5. This GTM scales globally without burning cash
