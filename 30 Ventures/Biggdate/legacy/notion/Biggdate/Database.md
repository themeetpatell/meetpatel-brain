### Revenue Model Builder

1. **Pricing Simulator & Revenue Projector**
    1. **Interactive Pricing Calculator**: Allow founders to test different pricing models (subscription tiers, one-time, usage-based, freemium) with real-time revenue projections
    2. **Scenario Modeling**: Create multiple pricing scenarios with adjustable variables (user growth rate, conversion rates, churn) to see best/worst/realistic case projections
    3. **Market Benchmarking**: Compare your pricing against industry standards and competitors in your space
    4. **Price Elasticity Testing**: Estimate demand changes at different price points based on your target market research
2. **Unit Economics**
    1. **Cost Structure Breakdown**: Track fixed costs (rent, salaries, software) and variable costs per customer (hosting, support, delivery)
    2. **Margin Calculator**: Automatically calculate gross margin, contribution margin, and net margin at different price points
    3. **Break-even Analysis**: Show exactly how many customers you need at each pricing tier to become profitable
    4. **Path to Profitability**: Visual timeline showing when you'll reach positive unit economics
3. **CAC & LTV**
    1. **Customer Acquisition Cost Tracker**: Calculate CAC by channel (paid ads, organic, referrals) with detailed spend tracking
    2. **Lifetime Value Calculator**: Project LTV based on average revenue per user, retention rates, and customer lifespan
    3. **LTV:CAC Ratio Monitor**: Real-time dashboard showing if your ratio is healthy (target 3:1 or better)
    4. **Payback Period**: Calculate how long it takes to recover CAC from customer revenue
4. Subscription Builder
    1. **Tier Designer**: Create and compare multiple subscription tiers with feature differentiation
    2. **Pricing Psychology Tools**: Apply anchoring, decoy pricing, and value-based pricing principles
    3. **Add-on Revenue Planner**: Model additional revenue from upsells, cross-sells, and premium features
    4. **Churn Modeling**: Project monthly/annual churn rates and their impact on MRR/ARR
    5. **Discount Strategy**: Plan annual vs monthly discounts and their effect on cash flow
5. Templates
    1. **Industry-Specific Models**: Pre-built templates for SaaS, marketplace, e-commerce, service businesses
    2. **Stage-Based Templates**: Different models for pre-launch, MVP, growth, and scale stages
    3. **Investor-Ready Financial Models**: 3-year projections with revenue, expenses, and key metrics
    4. **Pricing Strategy Playbooks**: Step-by-step guides for penetration pricing, value-based pricing, competitive pricing
6. **AI Recommendations**
    1. **Sensitivity Analysis**: Show how changes in key assumptions affect your bottom line
    2. **Competitor Pricing Intelligence**: Space to track and compare competitor pricing changes over time
    3. **Customer Feedback Integration**: Link pricing decisions to actual willingness-to-pay data from customer interviews
