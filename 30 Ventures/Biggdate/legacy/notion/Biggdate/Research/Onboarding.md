# Onboarding

## **Goals of World-Class Onboarding**

1. Build **trust + excitement** → users should feel they’re entering an exclusive club.
2. Capture **enough data** for matching → but without form-fatigue.
3. Showcase **differentiation** → this isn’t another Tinder; it’s a *Pitch-first movement*.
4. Make users feel **seen + unique** → personalization kicks in from step one.
5. **Progression:** 3 stages = Seed → Series A → Unicorn. The onboarding itself mirrors the philosophy of the app.

### **Step 1 – Who Are You? (Role + Stage + Identity)**

*What’s your role in the startup world?*

- Role:
    - Founder
    - Investor
    - Builder
- Path Stage:
    - Founder
        - **Idea Stage:** *“Dreaming it, shaping it, pitching napkin sketches.”*
        - **Seed Stage:** *“Built an MVP, chasing early users and funding.”*
        - **Growth Stage:** *“Scaling users, hiring team, sleepless nights.”*
        - **Exit Stage:** *“Exited, mentoring, angel investing, maybe starting again.”*
    - Investor
        - **Angel:** *“Backing bold dreamers, writing the first checks.”*
        - **VC Seed:** *“Hunting the next big wave, funding early believers.”*
        - **Growth Capital:** *“Scaling rockets, chasing unicorns.”*
        - **Family Office / LP / Exited Operator:** *“Legacy building, shaping futures.”*
    - Builder
        - **Early Builder:** *“First 10 hires, wearing 10 hats.”*
        - **Scaling Operator:** *“Building systems, leading teams, sweating OKRs.”*
        - **Growth Leader:** *“Managing orgs, driving culture, steering scale-ups.”*
        - **Veteran Operator:** *“Exited, advising, angel building, teaching the next gen.”*
- **Pick Your Mask (Avatar):**
    - Option A: AI-generated unique avatar.
    - Option B: Symbolic mask (Rocket 🚀, Diamond 💎, Flame 🔥).

👉 Emotion: *“I belong here. This is my tribe. I’m masked until I pitch.”*

---

### **Step 2 – Why Are You Here? (Mission + Values)**

*Behind every pitch is a reason. What’s yours?*

- **Mission Statement (Mini Prompt):**
    - *“I’m building the future of…”*
    - *“I believe love is like…”*
    - *“What I want most is…”*
        
        (User answers in short text or 20-sec voice note.)
        
- **Core Values (Rank Top 3):**
    - Options: Innovation, Growth, Adventure, Authenticity, Impact, Freedom, Legacy, Connection.
    - Drag-and-drop ranking → fun micro-interaction.
- **Intent Choice (Your Why):**
    - ❤️ Love / Relationship
    - 🌍 Life + Work Partner
    - 👀 Exploration / Networking

👉 Emotion: *“I’m not filling a form, I’m declaring my Why.”*

---

### **Step 3 – Your Pitch (The First Impression)**

*Every story starts with a pitch. This is yours.*

- **Elevator One-Liner:**
    
    Example prompts:
    
    - *“I’m building the next…”*
    - *“People say my superpower is…”*
    - *“If love was a startup, I’d be the…”*
- **Pitch Format Options:**
    - ✍️ Text pitch (freeform, no 140-char limit).
    - 🎤 Voice pitch (20-sec audio).
    - 🎨 Visual pitch (AI-generated symbolic image).
- **Preview Screen:** Show their pitch card as it will appear to others (role + stage + mask + pitch).
- **CTA:** *“Launch My Pitch 🚀”*
