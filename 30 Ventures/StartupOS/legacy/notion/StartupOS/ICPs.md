# ICPs

# 1. Founders

### Overview

| **Category** | **Details** |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | Solo founders, early-stage co-founders (0–10 team size) |
| **Position** | Founders / CEOs / Builders / Hustlers |
| **Business Model** | SaaS, D2C, marketplaces, fintech, healthtech—B2B or B2C |
| **Primary Goals** | Validate idea, raise funds, ship MVP, get early traction |
| **Their Opportunity** | Become VC-backable, raise their first/next round, build structured business |
| **Our Opportunity** | Become their operating system from day 1—own their workflows, metrics, hiring, and leadership layers |

### Core Needs

| **Category** | **Relevance** |
| --- | --- |
| **Execution Tools** | They need systems to build, launch, and grow—faster than they can hire |
| **Leadership Gaps** | They lack access to affordable, trusted expert guidance (CXOs) |
| **Clarity + Speed** | They suffer from scattered tools and chaotic decision-making |
| **Funding Readiness** | They don’t know how to “look ready” for investors |
| **Structured Thinking** | They build messy and learn slowly—they need strategic defaults |

### Who are they?

| **Prompt** | **Response** |
| --- | --- |
| **What they work on?** | MVPs, pitch decks, landing pages, early revenue channels |
| **Where they work?** | Often remote, co-working, or incubators (or from home) |
| **For whom they work?** | End users + potential investors simultaneously |
| **How do they earn?** | Trying monetization via freemium, pilots, or pre-seed capital |
| **Trust level with clients** | Low—building initial reputation and market proof |

### Why they’re a fit?

| **Reason** | **Relevance** |
| --- | --- |
| **No existing OS for execution** | StartupOS becomes their de facto system of record |
| **They crave structured momentum** | Our copilots and dashboards make progress visible and trackable |
| **They can’t afford full-time help** | Fractional CXOs + AI copilots = dream combo |
| **They love to try new tech** | Founders are early adopters if ROI is clear |

### Pain Point Mapping

| **Their Pain** | **Our Pain-Killer** |
| --- | --- |
| No time, no team, no tools | Plug-and-play OS for workflows, metrics, and leadership |
| Don’t know what to do next | Stage-aware copilots and guided execution flows |
| Can’t afford a CFO/COO/CMO | Access elite CXOs on demand, outcome-based |
| Always context-switching | One dashboard. One flow. One OS to run it all |

### Objection Handling

| **Objection** | **Counter** |
| --- | --- |
| “I already use Notion + ChatGPT + Excel” | StartupOS unifies workflows, talent, and copilots into one execution layer. We replace chaos. |
| “Too early for this” | StartupOS scales with you. Use only what you need. Pay when you grow. |
| “Looks complicated” | Designed to feel like a co-founder. Stage-aware. Role-aware. Setup in minutes. |

### **Buying Triggers**

- Post-idea validation
- Applied to accelerator / VC
- Building pitch deck
- Hiring first team member
- Preparing investor data room
- Just raised pre-seed and want “to get serious”

### **Decision Making Process**

- Try MVP tools, compare with Notion/Airtable/manual systems
- Ask peers or startup mentors
- Join accelerators or startup WhatsApp/Slack groups
- Prefer freemium → premium transition
- Trust execution over hype

### **Ideal Pitching**

> “What if your startup had an operating system that came with strategy, leadership, AI execution, and investor readiness—out of the box?”
> 

Or:

> “You don’t need a full-time team to start executing like one. StartupOS is your fractional co-founder stack—from copilots to CXOs.”
> 

### **Qualifying Filters**

- Is pre-seed to Seed stage
- Has team size <200
- Has product live or MVP in dev
- Expresses confusion, overload, or lack of direction
- Doesn’t have a full CXO team
- Ambitious + open to new systems
- Has applied or aims to raise funding in <12 months

# **2. Fractional CXO (Finance, Ops, GTM, Product)**

### Overview

| **Category** | **Details** |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | Experienced professionals: ex-founders, ex-CXOs, operators from scaled startups |
| **Position** | CFO, COO, CGO, CMO, CTO (Fractional / Interim / Consultant) |
| **Business Model** | Independent, consulting firms, boutique advisory networks |
| **Primary Goals** | Get matched to relevant, serious startups; build personal brand; earn outcome-based revenue |
| **Their Opportunity** | Serve multiple startups with less hustle for gigs; access qualified, investor-backed startups |
| **Our Opportunity** | Create supply liquidity for our founder-side users; elevate quality of execution outcomes |

### Core Needs

| **Category** | **Relevance** |
| --- | --- |
| **Qualified Leads** | They’re tired of chasing leads or dealing with time-wasters |
| **Outcome-Based Work** | Prefer impact-driven, time-boxed roles—not full-time jobs |
| **Portfolio Building** | Need visibility, reputation, and network expansion |
| **Clarity of Scope** | Want clear mandates, timelines, and KPIs from day one |

### Who are they?

| **Prompt** | **Answer** |
| --- | --- |
| **Where they work?** | Remote, hybrid, sometimes from firms or solo |
| **What they work on?** | Finance strategy, GTM design, hiring process, ops setup, metrics/KPI builds |
| **How they earn?** | Project-based, retainers, success fees, or fractional allocations |
| **Trust level?** | High trust, but need structured onboarding with founders |

### Why they’re a fit?

| **Fit Reason** | **Relevance** |
| --- | --- |
| **We reduce friction** | They don’t need to hustle for leads—our platform matches intelligently |
| **Outcome-first** | They want to focus on what they’re good at—not selling or scoping constantly |
| **Brand-building platform** | Visibility to startups + VCs = more deal flow & credibility |
| **AI + CXO pairing** | Copilots make their job easier; they plug in at critical junctures, not manually all the time |

### Pain Point Mapping

| **Their Pain** | **Our Solution** |
| --- | --- |
| Wasting time on unqualified leads | AI matching engine qualifies based on stage, goals, and urgency |
| Lack of structured projects | Clear mandates, timelines, and compensation models |
| No visibility or credibility in new markets | Get discovered via StartupOS reputation graph |
| Hard to scale without a team | Copilots and playbooks let them do more with less effort |

### **Objections & Counters**

| **Objection** | **Counter** |
| --- | --- |
| “I already get gigs through LinkedIn/network” | We bring you startups you’ve never met—who are ready to move |
| “Will I get paid on time?” | Contracts are secured via milestone-based outcomes with startup accountability built in |
| “I don’t want just gig work” | You get the flexibility to choose: part-time, interim, retainer, or advisor mode—on your terms |

### Buying Triggers

- Want to earn without full-time employment
- Tired of cold outreach and weak networks
- Looking for curated startup deal flow
- Want performance-based work and track record validation

### **Ideal Pitch**

> “StartupOS brings you ready-to-scale founders with real needs—no chasing, no fluff, just high-leverage roles matched to your expertise.”
> 

Or:

> “Plug into the next 100 breakout startups—not as a full-time hire, but as the execution expert who actually gets them there.”
> 

### **Qualifying Filters**

- 5+ years in a strategic role (C-suite, founder, director)
- Clear area of expertise (Finance, Ops, GTM, Product, etc.)
- Open to outcome-based work models
- Has availability or interest in working with 1–3 startups
- Strong communicator with portfolio examples or case studies

# 3. Investors & Accelerators

### Overview

| **Category** | **Details** |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | Early-stage VC funds, accelerators, venture studios, micro-VCs, family offices |
| **Position** | Managing Partners, Program Directors, Portfolio Heads, Startup Success Managers |
| **Business Model** | Equity-based returns + optional service fee models (for accelerators/studios) |
| **Primary Goals** | Help portfolio startups scale fast, raise next round, avoid failure |
| **Their Opportunity** | Improve portfolio quality, success rates, and signal to future investors |
| **Our Opportunity** | Plug StartupOS as their portfolio enablement layer, creating viral adoption and network flywheel |

### Core Needs

| **Category** | **Relevance** |
| --- | --- |
| **Portfolio Enablement** | They need tools to help 10–100 startups build, hire, raise, and grow—without building infra themselves |
| **Visibility into Progress** | Want dashboards that give real-time snapshots of startup health & metrics |
| **Scalability of Support** | They don’t have the bandwidth to help every founder with GTM, hiring, ops, etc. |
| **Access to Trusted Talent** | Need vetted CXOs, advisors, or domain experts to plug gaps across their portfolio |

### Who are they?

| **Prompt** | **Response** |
| --- | --- |
| **Where they work?** | VC firms, accelerator programs, coworking/incubators, virtual startup schools |
| **What they work on?** | Fundraising, coaching, connecting startups with talent and capital |
| **How do they earn?** | Carried interest, equity returns, success bonuses, sometimes SaaS or cohort fees |
| **Trust level with startups** | Very high—they are seen as guides, mentors, gatekeepers |

### Why they’re a fit?

| **Reason** | **Relevance** |
| --- | --- |
| **They want standardization** | StartupOS offers a unified way to support all startups in a portfolio |
| **They need leverage** | With 20–50 startups per partner, automation and guided systems are gold |
| **They value ecosystem intelligence** | Our dashboards + outcomes data creates strategic portfolio insights |
| **They’re outcome-aligned** | Our KPIs, playbooks, and CXOs increase chances of startup success—faster next rounds, fewer failures |

### Pain Point Mapping

| **Their Pain** | **Our Solution** |
| --- | --- |
| Can’t support all founders equally | We offer a scalable platform founders can use directly |
| Struggle to track progress post-investment | Our dashboards + execution tracking make founder progress visible |
| Gaps in leadership at portfolio companies | Inject on-demand CXOs + copilots instantly |
| Differentiating their accelerator/VC from others | StartupOS becomes their “infra advantage” to attract and retain better startups |

### Objection handling

| **Objection** | **Counter** |
| --- | --- |
| “We already have Notion/CRM/GSheets” | StartupOS is not just a tool—it’s an execution infrastructure, with AI copilots + leadership systems |
| “Startups won’t use it” | We co-create onboarding flows with your team—aligned to each stage and use case |
| “What if it doesn’t fit all our portfolio types?” | Modular structure = founders only see what’s relevant to them, based on stage, sector, and goals |

### **Buying Triggers**

- Launched new cohort or fund
- Portfolio company just raised
- Scaled past 20+ portfolio startups
- Planning to improve “founder support stack”
- Looking to improve portfolio KPI visibility

### **Ideal Pitch**

> “StartupOS is your unfair advantage—give every founder a fractional team, smart dashboard, and AI copilots without hiring anyone.”
> 

Or:

> “Portfolio enablement, redefined. From pre-seed to Series A, give your founders the systems, leadership, and clarity to grow—so you can focus on the next fund.”
> 

### **Qualifying Filters**

- Manages or supports 10+ startups
- Has limited team capacity to support all founders 1-on-1
- Interested in improving value-added services (not just capital)
- Open to platform partnerships or preferred tools
- Wants visibility into portfolio execution, KPIs, and gaps

# 4. Startup Communities & Platforms

---

### Overview

| **Category** | **Details** |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | Online communities, creator-led platforms, startup support networks, founder Slack groups, forums |
| **Position** | Community managers, founders, growth leads, partnership heads |
| **Business Model** | Freemium or membership-based; monetized via sponsors, courses, content, partner tools |
| **Primary Goals** | Drive member engagement, deliver real value, retain members, monetize platform influence |
| **Their Opportunity** | Provide tools, perks, and infrastructure to help startups go from idea to execution |
| **Our Opportunity** | Use them as distribution hubs and value-multipliers via integrations, partnerships, and co-branded OS flows |

### Core Needs

| **Category** | **Relevance** |
| --- | --- |
| **Engagement Tools** | Need valuable tools for members to interact with and come back to regularly |
| **Actionable Value** | Need to give members tools they can use—not just read/watch |
| **Monetization Leverage** | Seeking sponsors, tool partners, or affiliate/revenue-sharing models |
| **Startup Enablement** | Want to support startup success without building infra themselves |
| **Community Identity** | Want branded, aligned tools that reflect their mission and voice |

### Who are they?

| **Prompt** | **Response** |
| --- | --- |
| **Where they work?** | Online-first platforms (Slack, Discord, Web), sometimes tied to media brands or creator ecosystems |
| **What they work on?** | Curating events, content, expert AMAs, startup showcases, perk partnerships |
| **How do they earn?** | Membership fees, sponsorships, affiliate tools, upsells to programs |
| **Trust level with users** | Very high—seen as independent, founder-friendly, authentic voices |

### Why they’re a fit?

| **Reason** | **Relevance** |
| --- | --- |
| **They need new value layers** | StartupOS becomes a real, usable perk/toolset for their members |
| **We reduce churn** | More tools = more stickiness = more community retention |
| **We offer co-branding** | Communities can offer StartupOS-branded dashboards tailored to their tribe |
| **Aligned mission** | We both exist to make founders succeed—not sell to them blindly |

### Pain Point Mapping

| **Their Pain** | **Our Solution** |
| --- | --- |
| Content alone doesn’t retain founders | We offer actionable infrastructure that drives daily use |
| Lack of monetization paths | StartupOS offers revenue share, referral commissions, and affiliate partnerships |
| Members drop off after early engagement | Gamified, stage-based OS keeps members moving and progressing |
| Too much noise, not enough clarity | StartupOS brings structured growth and execution into chaotic founder journeys |

### Objection Handling

| **Objection** | **Counter** |
| --- | --- |
| “We already share free tools with our community” | StartupOS isn’t a tool—it’s the execution layer they’ll actually stick with |
| “We’re too small to matter” | We grow with you. Even 20 startups using StartupOS boosts your perceived value and community brand. |
| “Will this dilute our identity?” | You get a co-branded experience, aligned to your mission and messaging |

### **Buying/Integration Triggers**

- Launching a new cohort, membership tier, or support program
- Exploring monetization of community via tools/affiliates
- Partnering with sponsors or SaaS to add value
- Want to track or support member outcomes
- Building “founder journey” playbooks or toolkits

### **Ideal Pitch**

> “Give your founders more than content—give them an operating system to build, fund, and scale their startups—under your brand.”
> 

Or:

> “Your community helped them get started. Now give them the infrastructure to finish the race—with you still in their corner.”
> 

### **Qualifying Filters**

- Active founder/member community
- At least 100+ engaged users or a specific niche (e.g., climate startups, indie hackers, fintech)
- Focused on providing real value, not just discussion
- Exploring partnerships, perks, or platform integrations
- Strong brand voice and a desire to grow stickiness/retention
