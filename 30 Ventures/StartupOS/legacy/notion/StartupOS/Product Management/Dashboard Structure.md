# Dashboard Structure

Dashboard : Tagline + Filter of days

### OKR Progress & KPIs

- **Overview:** 3 OKR and its progress Card
- **Card details:** OKR Name, Status, Target, Current, Progress %, Key result areas & their progress, Owner, Due date
- **CTA:** Add OKR

![image.png](Dashboard%20Structure/image.png)

### Analytics

- **KPIs: Revenue, Users, Engagement, Conversion with Number**
- **Charts:** Revenue Trends, User Growth
- **CTA:** Export + View All

![image.png](Dashboard%20Structure/image%201.png)

### Quick Actions & Recent Activities

- Quick Actions:
    - Create New Project
    - Hire AI Employee
    - Schedule Meeting
    - Generate Report
- Recent Activities
    - Activities 1,2,3,4,5 etc
    - View All

![image.png](Dashboard%20Structure/image%202.png)

### Insights & Recommendation

- AI Insights & Suggestions: Title, Overview, Action, Impact
- Immediate Attention Required: Title, Risk level, One-liner, CTA
