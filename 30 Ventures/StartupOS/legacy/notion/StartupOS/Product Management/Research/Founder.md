# Founder

    - Dashboard to manage and monitor key startup metrics and performance.

    - Connect with potential investors and showcase the startup's progress.
    - Share updates, financial reports, and fundraising activities.
- **Team Collaboration:**
    - Collaboration tools to work with the startup team and remote employees.
    - Share documents, manage tasks, and schedule meetings.
- **Pitch & Fundraising Support:**
    - Access to resources and templates for creating compelling pitches.
    - Guidance on fundraising strategies and best practices.
- **Market Research & Insights:**
    - Access to market research data and industry insights for informed decision-making.
    - Tools to analyze competitors and customer feedback.
- **Startup Dashboard:**
