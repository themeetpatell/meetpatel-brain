# Employees

**1. Career:**

**Normal Users:**

- Talks and Webinars: Access to webinars, workshops, and career talks from industry experts and professionals.
- Job Listings: A database of job opportunities tailored to the user's skills, location, and preferences.
- Articles and Resources: Curated articles and resources on career development, resume writing, interview tips, etc.
- Skill Assessments: Self-assessment tools to identify strengths and areas for skill improvement.
- Resume Builder: User-friendly resume builder with customizable templates.
- Interview Preparation: Interview tips, mock interviews, and preparation guides.
- Networking Events: Virtual networking events to connect with professionals and potential employers.
- Skill Development Courses: Access to a selection of skill development courses.

**Premium Users (In addition to Normal Users):**

- Personalized Job Matching: Highly tailored job recommendations based on the user's profile and preferences.
- Career Counseling: One-on-one sessions with career counselors for personalized guidance.
- Early Access to Jobs: Priority access to newly posted job opportunities.
- Premium Skill Development: Access to advanced and specialized skill development courses.
- Resume Review and Optimization: Professional review and optimization of resumes.
- Exclusive Networking Opportunities: Invitations to exclusive networking events with industry experts and recruiters.

**2. Company (Work) - Including features for Normal and Premium Users:**

- My Company: Access to company news, updates, and internal resources.
- People/Chat - My Company: Internal chat and collaboration platform for team members.
- Announcements: Company-wide announcements from management or HR.
- Idea/Suggestions Submission: Employees can submit ideas and suggestions to improve the company's processes or products.
- Bug/Issue Reporting: Channel to report technical issues or bugs.
- Employee Handbooks: Access to company policies and guidelines.
- Yearly Holiday Calendar: Display of company holidays and important dates.

**3. HR:**

**Normal Users:**

- Apply for a Leave / Leave Management: Leave application and management system.
- Attendance Tracker: HR's attendance tracking tool.
- Make a Complaint: Channel to file complaints or raise concerns with HR.
- Payroll Receipts / Salary Slips: Access to electronic salary slips and payroll-related documents.
- Warning Tracker: HR's warning or disciplinary actions management system.

**Premium Users (In addition to Normal Users):**

- Enhanced Leave Management: Advanced features for leave planning and analytics.
- HR Support Line: Access to a dedicated support line for HR-related inquiries and assistance.
- Performance Review and Coaching: Personalized performance review and coaching from HR experts.
- **Career Growth & Development:**
    - Tools to set career goals and track professional development.
    - Access to training and learning resources to upskill or reskill.
- **Performance Management:**
    - Features to track performance reviews and goals within the company.
    - Receive feedback from supervisors and colleagues.
- **Networking Opportunities:**
    - Connect with other professionals in the industry or similar roles.
    - Participate in industry-specific forums and discussions.
- **Internal Communication:**
    - Messaging and collaboration tools to communicate with team members.
    - Share updates, announcements, and important company information.
- **Job Opportunities:**
    - Access to internal job postings and opportunities for career advancement.
    - Explore potential career paths within the company.
