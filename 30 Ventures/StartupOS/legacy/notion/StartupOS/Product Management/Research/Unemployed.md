# Unemployed

**1. Career:**

- Talks for me: Webinars, workshops, and sessions focused on career development, job search strategies, and skill enhancement.
- Articles for me: A collection of articles covering various industries, job trends, resume writing tips, interview preparation, etc.
- Jobs for me: Job listings tailored to the user's skills, location, and preferences.
- My jobs / Saved Jobs: A place to save and manage job applications and track their progress.
- My Documents: An area to upload and store relevant documents like resumes, cover letters, certificates, etc.
- Skills Assessment: Offer a skills assessment tool that helps unemployed users identify their strengths and areas for improvement, suggesting relevant job opportunities based on their skills.
- Career Counseling: Provide access to career counselors or mentors who can guide users in making informed career decisions and setting career goals.
- Resume Builder: Offer a user-friendly resume builder with customizable templates to help users create compelling resumes.
- Interview Preparation: Provide interview preparation resources, including mock interviews and tips from industry experts.
- Networking Opportunities: Facilitate virtual networking events or online meetups to connect job seekers with industry professionals or potential employers.
- Skill Development Courses: Partner with educational platforms to offer discounted or free skill development courses to enhance employability.

**2. Work:**

- Work Opportunities: Offer internships, freelance gigs, and temporary work opportunities that can help unemployed individuals gain experience and build their network.
- Gig Economy: A section for short-term or freelance projects where job seekers can offer their skills or services to potential employers or clients.
- Work from Home Jobs: A dedicated category for remote job opportunities, catering to individuals looking for flexible work arrangements.
- Gig Reviews and Ratings: Allow users to rate and review their experiences with gig-based work to help others make informed decisions.
- Side Hustles: Provide information and resources for individuals interested in starting their own small businesses or side projects.
- Entrepreneurship Support: Offer guidance and resources for aspiring entrepreneurs looking to launch their own startups.

**Premium Career Features:**

1. Personalized Job Matching: Premium users can receive highly tailored job recommendations based on their skills, preferences, and career goals.
2. Priority Job Applications: Premium users get priority status when applying for jobs, increasing their chances of being noticed by employers.
3. Career Coaching Sessions: Access to one-on-one career coaching sessions with experienced career counselors or industry experts.
4. Resume Review and Optimization: Premium users can submit their resumes for professional review and optimization to make them stand out to employers.
5. Premium Skill Development Courses: Offer premium users access to more advanced and specialized skill development courses that are not available to free users.
6. Early Access to Job Listings: Premium users get early access to newly posted job opportunities, giving them a head start in the application process.

**Premium Work Features:**

1. Exclusive Gig Opportunities: Premium users can access exclusive high-paying gig opportunities or projects with reputable clients.
2. Gig Promotion: Premium users' gig listings receive enhanced visibility and promotion on the platform.
3. Virtual Workshops and Masterclasses: Provide premium users with access to virtual workshops and masterclasses led by industry experts.
4. Entrepreneurship Incubator: Premium users interested in entrepreneurship can join an incubator program with resources, mentorship, and guidance to start their own businesses.
5. Gig Portfolio: Premium users can create a professional portfolio showcasing their work and accomplishments.
6. Premium Networking Events: Offer premium users invitations to exclusive networking events where they can connect with top industry professionals and potential employers.
