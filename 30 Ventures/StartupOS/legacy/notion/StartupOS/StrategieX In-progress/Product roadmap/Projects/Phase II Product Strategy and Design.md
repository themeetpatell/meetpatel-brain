## About this project

**Objective:** Lay the groundwork for the platform’s core features, design, and architecture.

**Epic 1: Product Requirements Documentation (PRD)**

**Objective:** Develop a comprehensive Product Requirements Document (PRD) that outlines all features, user stories, technical requirements, and success criteria for the MVP.

**Epic 2: UX/UI Design and Prototyping**

**Objective:** Create a seamless and intuitive user experience through detailed UX/UI design, prototyping, and validation to ensure high engagement and usability.

**Epic 3: Technical Architecture and Infrastructure Planning**

**Objective:** Define a scalable and secure technical architecture that supports rapid development, deployment, and future scalability.

**Epic 4: Agile Development Preparation**

**Objective:** Prepare teams, processes, and tools to operate in an agile development environment for rapid iteration and continuous improvement.

**Epic 5: Product Validation and Feedback Loops**

**Objective:** Ensure the product design and strategy align with user needs and expectations through continuous validation and feedback.

**Epic 6: Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy Alignment with Product Development**

**Objective:** Align the GTM strategy with product development to ensure that the product’s features and messaging resonate with the target audience and market needs.

**Epic 7: Final Preparations for Phase 3 (Core Platform Development)**

**Objective:** Ensure that the product, design, and engineering teams are fully prepared and aligned for the development phase, minimizing risks and ensuring a smooth transition.
