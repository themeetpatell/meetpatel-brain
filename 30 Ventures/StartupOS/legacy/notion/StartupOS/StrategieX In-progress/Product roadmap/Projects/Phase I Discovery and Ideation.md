## About this project

**Objective: Establish a clear understanding of the market, user needs, and technical requirements to lay a strong foundation for the platform.**

**Epics:**

**Epic 1: Market Research and Validation:** 

**Objective:** Understand the market landscape, validate demand, and identify gaps to differentiate Strategix from competitors.

**Epic 2: Value Proposition and Business Model Development**

**Objective:** Craft a unique value proposition that resonates with both startups and CXOs and develop a sustainable business model.

**Epic 3: User Persona and Journey Mapping**

**Objective:** Build detailed user personas and map out the user journey to ensure an optimal user experience.

**Epic 4: Brand Identity and Positioning**

**Objective:** Define a strong brand identity, including vision, mission, values, and visual aesthetics that differentiate Strategix in the marketplace.
