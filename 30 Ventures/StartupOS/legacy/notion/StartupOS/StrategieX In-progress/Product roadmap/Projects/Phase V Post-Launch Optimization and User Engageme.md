## About this project

- Our goal is to maximize the value delivered to users by continuously improving the platform, enhancing user experience, and increasing engagement and retention. This phase is critical to ensure that early adopters stay active, provide valuable feedback, and help drive the platform’s growth through word-of-mouth and network effects.

**Epic 1: Analyzing User Feedback and Iterating Product Features**

**Objective:** Collect and analyze user feedback to prioritize and implement improvements that enhance user satisfaction and platform usability.

**Epic 2: Enhancing User Onboarding and Engagement Flows**

**Objective:** Improve onboarding experiences to increase user activation and develop engagement flows that keep users returning to the platform.

**Epic 3: Implementing User Engagement Strategies**

**Objective:** Develop and implement strategies to retain users and encourage ongoing engagement with the platform.

**Epic 4: Ongoing Performance Optimization and Bug Fixes**

**Objective:** Ensure the platform is reliable, fast, and free of bugs to provide a seamless user experience.

**Epic 5: Expanding Customer Support and Success Operations**

**Objective:** Scale customer support and success operations to provide a high level of service and proactively help users achieve their goals.
