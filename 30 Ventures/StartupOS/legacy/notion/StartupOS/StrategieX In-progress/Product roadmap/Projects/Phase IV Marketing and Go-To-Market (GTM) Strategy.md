## About this project

- we will focus on preparing a comprehensive marketing and sales strategy to ensure a successful launch of the MVP. This phase is crucial for building market awareness, driving user acquisition, and setting the foundation for rapid growth post-launch.

**Epic 1: GTM Strategy Development and Alignment with Product Features**

**Objective:** Develop a strategic GTM plan that aligns with the product’s core features, value propositions, and target audience to maximize market impact and user adoption.

**Epic 2: Pre-Launch Marketing Content Creation and Campaign Planning**

**Objective:** Develop and execute pre-launch marketing campaigns that build anticipation, drive early sign-ups, and establish a strong market presence.

**Epic 3: Sales and Partnerships Strategy Development**

**Objective:** Develop a strong sales and partnerships strategy to accelerate user acquisition, drive revenue growth, and build strategic alliances.

**Epic 4: Launch Event Planning and Execution** 

**Objective:** Plan and execute a launch event (virtual or physical) to generate buzz, showcase the platform, and engage potential users and partners.

**Epic 5: Post-Launch Marketing and User Acquisition Campaigns**

**Objective:** Maintain momentum after the launch by executing post-launch marketing campaigns to drive user acquisition, engagement, and retention.
