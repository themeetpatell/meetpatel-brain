## About this project

- **Core Platform Development**, we shift from planning and design to the actual building of Strategix’s core platform. This is where all the research, strategy, and design work come to life. The goal of this phase is to develop a Minimum Viable Product (MVP) that is robust, scalable, and provides immediate value to users, enabling early adoption, feedback collection, and continuous improvement.

**Epic 1: AI-Driven Matching Algorithm Development**

**Objective:** Develop the core AI-driven matching engine that aligns startups with suitable CXOs based on multiple criteria, such as skills, industry experience, growth stage, cultural fit, and engagement needs.

**Epic 2: User Onboarding and Profile Management Development**

**Objective:** Build a robust and engaging onboarding experience for startups and CXOs, allowing them to create comprehensive profiles that aid in accurate matching and engagement success.

**Epic 3: Engagement Management and Collaboration Tools Development**

**Objective:** Develop tools that enable seamless collaboration between startups and CXOs, including project posting, proposal management, communication, and real-time collaboration.

**Epic 4: Real-Time Dashboards and Analytics Development**

**Objective:** Build comprehensive dashboards that provide real-time analytics and insights for startups and CXOs, enabling data-driven decision-making and performance tracking.

**Epic 5: Quality Assurance, Security, and Performance Optimization (Continued)**

**Objective:** Ensure the platform is robust, secure, and performs well under load to deliver a seamless user experience while protecting sensitive data and complying with regulatory standards.

**Epic 6: Beta Testing and User Feedback Collection**

**Objective:** Validate the platform with real users (startups and CXOs), gather actionable feedback, and make necessary improvements before the official MVP launch.

**Epic 7: MVP Launch Readiness and Rollout Preparation**

**Objective:** Ensure all teams, systems, and strategies are fully prepared and aligned for a successful MVP launch.
