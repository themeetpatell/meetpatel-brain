# Financial Model and Projections

- **Revenue Model**: Define the revenue streams (e.g., subscription fees, success fees, project-based fees). Include customer acquisition rates, pricing models, and churn rates.
- **Expense Model**: Break down the costs, including product development, marketing, sales, operations, and overhead.
- **Cash Flow Projections**: Provide a monthly cash flow forecast for at least the next 18-24 months, showing cash inflows, outflows, and runway.
- **Profit and Loss Statement (P&L)**: Detail projected revenues, costs of goods sold (COGS), gross margin, operating expenses, EBITDA, and net income.
- **Balance Sheet**: Outline assets, liabilities, and equity projections.
- **Key Financial Metrics**: Highlight key metrics such as customer acquisition cost (CAC), lifetime value (LTV), burn rate, runway, and break-even analysis.
- **Scenario Analysis**: Prepare different scenarios (best case, base case, worst case) to demonstrate preparedness for various market conditions.
