### Problem

- **Overview**: Startups often struggle with leadership gaps at critical growth phases—whether they need financial, operational, technical, or marketing expertise.
- **Key Pain Points**:
    - Difficulty finding experienced leadership without full-time commitments.
    - High costs and risks associated with hiring the wrong executive.
    - Lack of flexibility to adapt leadership based on evolving needs.
- **Quote or Statistic**: *“75% of startups fail due to team and leadership issues.”* - CB Insights

### Solution

- **Strategix Platform**: A marketplace connecting startups with seasoned, fractional, or interim CXOs tailored to their specific needs.
- **Key Features**:
    - **AI-Driven Matching**: Advanced algorithms to match startups with the right CXOs based on industry, growth stage, and strategic goals.
    - **Flexible Engagement Models**: Fractional, interim, or project-based roles, customized to the startup’s immediate needs.
    - **Outcome-Based Contracts**: Engagements tied to measurable outcomes, ensuring transparency and accountability.
- **Unique Value Proposition**: *“Leadership as a Service (LaaS) that scales with your startup’s needs.”*

### Market Opportunity

- **Total Addressable Market (TAM)**: $50 billion globally (leadership and management consulting market).
- **Serviceable Available Market (SAM)**: $10 billion (fractional and interim executive market).
- **Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM)**: $1 billion in key startup hubs (North America, Europe, Middle East).
- **Market Trends**:
    - Increasing demand for flexible executive roles.
    - Growth in remote and fractional work due to economic uncertainty.
- **Quote or Insight**: *“The fractional executive market is expected to grow by 20% annually over the next 5 years.”*

### Product

- **Platform Overview**:
    - **Matching Engine**: AI-powered engine that analyzes startup needs and CXO profiles for the best fit.
    - **Engagement Dashboard**: Real-time performance tracking, feedback loops, and outcome monitoring.
    - **Knowledge Hub**: Resources for startups and CXOs, including strategic playbooks, case studies, and community forums.
- **Product Roadmap**:
    - **MVP Launch (Q1 2025)**: Core matching and engagement features.
    - **Expansion (Q3 2025)**: Advanced analytics, AI-driven recommendations, and CXO marketplace.
    - **Global Scaling (2026+)**: Expand to new regions and add additional features like CXO certifications and leadership academies.

### Business Model

- **Revenue Streams**:
    - **Subscription Fees**: Monthly retainer for continuous access to fractional CXO services.
    - **Project-Based Fees**: One-time fees or hourly rates for specific engagements.
    - **Success Fees**: Percentage-based fees tied to successful achievement of key milestones (e.g., fundraising, market entry).
- **Pricing Tiers**:
    - **Basic**: Access to vetted CXOs, starting at $1,500/month.
    - **Pro**: Advanced features like AI matching and performance analytics, starting at $5,000/month.
    - **Enterprise**: Custom packages for startups with complex needs, starting at $10,000/month.
