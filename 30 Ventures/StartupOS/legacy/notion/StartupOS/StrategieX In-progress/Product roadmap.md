# Product roadmap

This page contains two connected databases:

- [Projects](Product%20roadmap/Projects.csv): This is your overview of all the projects in the pipeline.
- [Tasks](Product%20roadmap/Tasks.csv)**:** This is your detailed breakdown of every task under your projects. 
It's where you can track task status, due dates, priority levels, dependencies, and the responsible team member.

↓ Click through the different database tabs to see the same data in different ways. 
Hover over any project name and click `◨ OPEN` (or click the project card in the timeline) to view more info and its associated tasks.
