# High uncertainty regarding the startup’s financial stability, leadership culture, and potential for success.

Category: Risk Management
Consequences: Hesitance to engage with lesser-known startups.

Missed opportunities with potentially great startups due to lack of transparency.

Reduced trust in startups and platforms.
Current Process: Conducting their own due diligence (e.g., financial analysis, background checks on founders).

Relying on feedback from their network or references, which may not always be accurate or up-to-date.
For Whom: Risk-averse CXOs or those who have faced challenges in prior engagements.
Impact: Risk of reputational damage and financial loss if the startup fails or pivots abruptly.
No of Occurrences: Regularly, particularly with early-stage startups or those in unstable markets.
Opportunity: Build a transparent, trusted marketplace where CXOs feel confident in engaging with vetted startups.
Probable Solution: Strategix offers a detailed vetting and rating system for startups, including financial stability scores, founder backgrounds, and culture fits.

Option for CXOs to review past engagements and feedback to gauge potential risks better.
Urgency / Impact Level: Medium
Why?: Insufficient reliable data on startup viability and lack of standardized, accessible due diligence resources.
