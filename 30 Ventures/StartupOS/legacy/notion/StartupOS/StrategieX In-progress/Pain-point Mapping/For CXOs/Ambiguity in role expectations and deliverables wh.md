# Ambiguity in role expectations and deliverables when engaging with startups.

Category: Role Clarity and Expectations
Consequences: Conflicts between CXOs and startup teams regarding expectations.

Poor outcomes due to unclear goals, leading to reputational risks for both parties.
Current Process: Lengthy negotiations on scope and deliverables, often without clear benchmarks.

Dependence on vague contracts or verbal agreements.
For Whom: CXOs who prefer clarity and structured engagements; startups seeking clear, efficient onboarding of CXOs.
Impact: Misalignment leads to dissatisfaction, conflict, and underperformance.
No of Occurrences: Often, especially with rapidly evolving startups.
Opportunity: Set industry standards for fractional and interim executive roles, ensuring consistent, high-quality engagements.
Probable Solution: Clear engagement templates and standardized contracts provided by Strategix, with pre-defined KPIs and deliverables tailored to specific roles and startup needs.
Urgency / Impact Level: High
Why?: Startups often lack experience in defining executive roles clearly, and CXOs need clarity to align with business goals effectively.
