# Difficulty finding consistent, impactful projects that match their expertise and career goals.

Category: Project Acquisition
Consequences: Time wasted on unsuitable projects or roles.

Burnout or dissatisfaction from repetitive, non-strategic engagements.

Missed opportunities for projects that could leverage their full skill set.
Current Process: Rely on personal networks or word of mouth.

Use general consulting platforms (e.g., Upwork, Toptal), which often lack high-quality, executive-level opportunities.

Cold outreach to startups or VCs, which is time-consuming and inefficient.
For Whom: Senior executives (CXOs) seeking part-time, interim, or fractional roles across various industries.
Impact: Financial instability due to unpredictable project flow; frustration from taking on low-impact or poorly matched roles
No of Occurrences: Frequently, especially for niche or highly specialized CXOs.
Opportunity: Create a trusted, go-to platform for senior executives looking for flexible, impactful work.

Reduce friction in project acquisition, boosting satisfaction and retention of top-tier CXOs.
Probable Solution: Strategix platform curates high-quality, vetted projects based on CXO expertise, experience, and preferred engagement style (interim, fractional, project-based).

AI-driven matching to reduce time spent on project search and increase relevance.
Urgency / Impact Level: High
Why?: Fragmented market and lack of specialized, high-quality project marketplaces catering specifically to CXO-level executives.
