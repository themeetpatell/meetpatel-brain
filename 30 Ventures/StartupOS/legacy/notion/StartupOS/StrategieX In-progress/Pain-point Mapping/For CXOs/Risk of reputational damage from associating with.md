# Risk of reputational damage from associating with poorly managed or failing startups.

Category: Reputation Management and Credibility
Consequences: Limited opportunities and hesitance to engage with potentially high-growth startups.

CXOs might reject promising projects due to perceived risks.
Current Process: Conducting extensive due diligence on each potential engagement.

Avoiding early-stage startups with less visibility or proven success.
For Whom: Seasoned CXOs and those who have faced reputation challenges in the past.
Impact: Long-term impact on personal brand and future opportunities; loss of trust from their network.
No of Occurrences: Frequently, especially in volatile industries like deep-tech or crypto.
Opportunity: Build a trusted environment where both CXOs and startups can engage confidently, knowing they have full transparency.
Probable Solution: Strategix introduces a "Startup Credibility Score" based on transparent data such as funding rounds, founder experience, and investor backing.

CXOs can choose engagements based on risk levels that align with their reputation management strategies.
Urgency / Impact Level: Medium
Why?: The executive consulting market values reputation highly, and any negative experience can be damaging.
