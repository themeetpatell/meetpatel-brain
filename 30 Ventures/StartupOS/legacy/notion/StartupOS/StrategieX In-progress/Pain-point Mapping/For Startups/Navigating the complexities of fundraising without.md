# Navigating the complexities of fundraising without experienced financial leadership.

Category: Fundraising Strategy and Support
Consequences: Diluted equity, extended fundraising periods, or inability to secure necessary funds.
Current Process: Relying on founders or inexperienced team members to handle negotiations and strategy.

Hiring fundraising consultants who charge high fees without committing to the startup’s long-term success.
For Whom: Startups at pre-seed to Series B stages looking for strategic fundraising expertise without permanent hires.
Impact: Missed funding opportunities, unfavorable deal terms, or failed fundraising rounds.
No of Occurrences: Often, especially for startups raising Series A or B.
Opportunity: Build a reputation for providing high-caliber financial leaders who can guide startups through critical fundraising stages.
Probable Solution: Fractional CFOs and experienced fundraisers available on the Strategix platform, providing targeted, strategic fundraising support.

Dedicated workshops and resources within the platform to help startups prepare for fundraising rounds.
Urgency / Impact Level: High
Why?: Founders often lack the financial acumen and experience needed to navigate complex funding landscapes.
