Category: Interim Leadership during Transition Phases
Consequences: Suboptimal decision-making, delays in executing critical initiatives, and potential employee morale decline.
Current Process: Temporary internal promotions or overburdening existing executives.

Long, costly search for full-time replacements.
For Whom: Startups in transition or facing unexpected leadership gaps.
Impact: Strategic drift, operational inefficiencies, and potential loss of investor confidence.
No of Occurrences: Often in startups undergoing leadership changes or rapid growth.
Opportunity: Become the go-to platform for startups needing temporary executive leadership during transitional phases.
Probable Solution: Strategix provides access to interim CXOs (CEOs, CFOs, COOs) who can step in immediately to stabilize operations and provide continuity.
Urgency / Impact Level: Very High
Why?: Leadership gaps can derail growth, disrupt strategy, and harm the startup’s valuation and potential exit opportunities.
