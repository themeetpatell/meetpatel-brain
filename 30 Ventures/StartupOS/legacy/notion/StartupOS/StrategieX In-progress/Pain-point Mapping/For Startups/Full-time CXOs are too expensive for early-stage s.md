# Full-time CXOs are too expensive for early-stage startups, leading to delayed or no hiring.

Category: Cost Constraints
Consequences: Suboptimal decision-making, increased workload on existing team members, and higher burnout rates.
Current Process: Avoid hiring altogether or hiring less qualified personnel at lower salaries.
For Whom: Startups at early growth phases, startups with limited funding.
Impact: Strained budgets, reduced runway, and increased financial risk.
No of Occurrences: Often, especially in early-stage startups with limited funding.
Opportunity: Position Strategix as a cost-effective solution, providing high-impact leadership without financial strain.
Probable Solution: Flexible pricing models for fractional CXO services that align with startup budgets and growth trajectories (e.g., equity-based compensation, success fees).
Urgency / Impact Level: High
Why?: High salary expectations for experienced CXOs, coupled with budget limitations in early stages.
