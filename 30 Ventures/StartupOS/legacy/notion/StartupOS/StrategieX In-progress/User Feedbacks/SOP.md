# SOP

### **Purpose**

- Centralize the collection, tracking, and resolution of client feedback and complaints.
- Enable data-driven decision-making by identifying recurring issues and quantifying user pain points.

---

### **Process**

1. **Collection:**
    - We will be utilizing the following channels for feedback collection:
        - **Review meetings with clients:** Collect structured feedback using forms during these meetings.
        - **In-app reviews and surveys:** Implement feedback mechanisms within the product.
        - **User interviews/demo calls:** Conduct interviews and demos to gather qualitative insights.
        - **Support tickets:** Track and analyze issues reported by users.
        - **Cross-functional and brainstorming sessions:** Encourage internal teams to share feedback and ideas.
2. **Logging:**
    - All feedback and complaints should be logged in a designated system
    - Each entry should include relevant details:
        - User/client identifier - People who gave it
        - Date and time of feedback
        - **Feedback type**: Positive, Constructive, bugs, feature-request, usability issue, general
        - Feedback/complaint description
        - **Severity** level (critical, high, medium, low)
        - **Product area** affected
        - Feedback Source (Review-meeting, in-app survey, observation by analytics, etc)
        - Attachment (if any)
        - **Priority level:** (Based on MoSCoW Framework)
        - Assigned team member (for resolution)
        - Status (open, in progress, resolved, communicated back)
    - Categorize and tag feedback based on type, severity, product area, and Priority level.
    - Product Team will collect feedbacks from here, and do further analysis and prioritisation as Jira backlogs
3. **Analysis & Prioritization:**
    - Track the number of occurrences of specific pain points to quantify their impact.
    - Prioritize feedback using the MoSCoW framework and considering its impact, feasibility, and alignment with product strategy.
4. **Resolution:**
    - Develop action plans to address high-priority feedback.
    - Assign ownership of feedback items to relevant team members and Incorporate feedback into future product iterations.
    - Track progress on feedback implementation and measure its impact.
    - Communicate feedback responses and planned actions to users.

---

### **Tools & Technologies**

- Feedback Collection Tools**: In-app feedback widgets**, **survey tools/Forms**, etc
- Centralized Feedback Repository: Issue tracking system (e.g., Jira)
- Data Analytics & Visualization Tools: Microsoft Clarity and analytics, Mixpanel
