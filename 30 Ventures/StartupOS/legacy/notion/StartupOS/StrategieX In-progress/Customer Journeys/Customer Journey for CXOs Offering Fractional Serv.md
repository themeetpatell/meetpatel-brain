# Customer Journey for CXOs Offering Fractional Services

### **Stage 1: Awareness**

- **Touchpoints**:
    - LinkedIn Ads, Professional Networks, Referral Programs, Executive Events, Webinars.
- **Actions**:
    - A CXO learns about Strategix through their professional network or an ad targeting fractional executives looking for flexible engagements.
- **Emotions**:
    - Interest and caution—curious about new opportunities but wary of time-wasting or mismatched engagements.
- **Outcomes**:
    - The CXO visits the Strategix website to learn more.

### **Stage 2: Consideration**

- **Touchpoints**:
    - Strategix Website, Case Studies, Testimonials, Platform Tour, Blogs.
- **Actions**:
    - The CXO reads about the platform, reviews testimonials from other CXOs who have successfully engaged with startups, and signs up for a webinar or reads a detailed blog post about maximizing value from fractional roles.
- **Emotions**:
    - Optimism and skepticism—the CXO is considering the platform but needs more assurance of its value.
- **Outcomes**:
    - The CXO registers on the platform and begins the onboarding process.

### **Stage 3: Registration and Onboarding**

- **Touchpoints**:
    - Platform Interface, Onboarding Emails, Profile Setup Wizard.
- **Actions**:
    - The CXO creates a profile detailing their experience, industry expertise, availability, and preferred engagement types (e.g., interim, project-based).
    - The platform verifies the CXO’s credentials and suggests potential startup matches.
- **Emotions**:
    - Confidence and eagerness—the CXO feels that the platform is professionally managed and aligns with their needs.
- **Outcomes**:
    - The CXO profile is verified, and they become eligible to engage with startups.

### **Stage 4: Matching and Engagement Proposal**

- **Touchpoints**:
    - Matching Engine, Startup Profiles, In-App Messaging, Video Call Scheduler.
- **Actions**:
    - The CXO reviews potential startup matches suggested by the platform.
    - They reach out to interested startups or receive inbound requests for a consultation or engagement.
    - The CXO schedules an introductory call to understand the startup’s needs and propose a tailored engagement plan.
- **Emotions**:
    - Confidence and excitement—empowered by transparency and clear communication channels.
- **Outcomes**:
    - The CXO agrees to an engagement and finalizes terms through the platform.

### **Stage 5: Engagement Execution**

- **Touchpoints**:
    - Project Management Tools, Collaboration Hub, Communication Features.
- **Actions**:
    - The CXO begins the engagement, leveraging the platform’s tools to set up a project plan, assign tasks, and communicate regularly with the startup team.
    - They track progress against set goals and adjust strategies as needed.
- **Emotions**:
    - Fulfillment and focus—the CXO is engaged in meaningful work and sees their impact in real-time.
- **Outcomes**:
    - The engagement is successfully completed, and both parties achieve their objectives.

### **Stage 6: Feedback and Reputation Building**

- **Touchpoints**:
    - Feedback Forms, Performance Reports, Public Profile Ratings.
- **Actions**:
    - The CXO receives feedback from the startup and reviews their performance report.
    - Positive feedback is added to the CXO’s profile, boosting their reputation on the platform.
- **Emotions**:
    - Pride and motivation—a good performance leads to more visibility and future opportunities.
- **Outcomes**:
    - The CXO is encouraged to continue using the platform for more engagements.

### **Stage 7: Retention and Community Engagement**

- **Touchpoints**:
    - Invitations to Networking Events, Advanced Training Programs, Community Forums.
- **Actions**:
    - The platform invites the CXO to exclusive webinars, networking events, and leadership forums to share insights and connect with peers.
    - The CXO engages with the community, sharing their experiences and learning from others.
- **Emotions**:
    - Belonging and loyalty—the CXO feels part of a valuable network of professionals.
- **Outcomes**:
    - The CXO becomes a long-term user and an advocate for the platform.
