# Customer Journey for Startups Seeking Fractional CXO Services

### **Stage 1: Awareness**

- **Touchpoints**:
    - Digital Ads, SEO Content, Social Media Posts, Webinars, Startup Events, VC Referrals.
- **Actions**:
    - Startup founders come across Strategix through various channels while searching for leadership solutions or attending startup-related events.
- **Emotions**:
    - Curiosity mixed with skepticism—startups are intrigued by the concept of fractional CXO services but unsure of the value.
- **Outcomes**:
    - The startup founder visits the Strategix website or landing page.

### **Stage 2: Consideration**

- **Touchpoints**:
    - Strategix Website, Case Studies, Blog Articles, Testimonials, Product Demos.
- **Actions**:
    - The founder explores the website, reads case studies about other startups that benefited from fractional CXOs, and watches a demo video on how the platform works.
- **Emotions**:
    - Growing interest and cautious optimism—seeing real-world examples builds confidence in the platform’s value proposition.
- **Outcomes**:
    - The founder decides to sign up for a free trial or schedule a demo session with the Strategix team.

### **Stage 3: Registration and Onboarding**

- **Touchpoints**:
    - Strategix Platform Interface, Onboarding Emails, Guided Setup Wizard.
- **Actions**:
    - The founder registers on the platform, creates a startup profile, and completes the onboarding questionnaire detailing their specific needs, business stage, and goals.
    - The platform’s AI-driven onboarding wizard suggests relevant CXO roles (e.g., CFO, CMO) based on the startup’s profile.
- **Emotions**:
    - Confidence and anticipation—the founder feels guided and supported through the setup process.
- **Outcomes**:
    - The startup profile is fully set up, and the system is ready to match them with suitable CXOs.

### **Stage 4: CXO Search and Engagement**

- **Touchpoints**:
    - AI-Driven Matching Engine, CXO Profiles, In-App Messaging, Video Calls.
- **Actions**:
    - The founder searches for CXOs using filters (e.g., industry experience, specific skills, availability) and reviews the suggested matches.
    - The founder reads detailed profiles, reviews ratings, and shortlists potential CXOs.
    - The founder initiates a conversation with shortlisted CXOs via in-app messaging or schedules a video call for a deeper discussion.
- **Emotions**:
    - Confidence and excitement—the founder feels in control and appreciates the transparency and ease of finding the right match.
- **Outcomes**:
    - The founder selects a CXO and sends a formal engagement request.

### **Stage 5: Engagement and Collaboration**

- **Touchpoints**:
    - Strategix Project Management Tools, Communication Features (Messaging, Video Calls), Collaboration Hub.
- **Actions**:
    - The startup and CXO finalize terms and begin the engagement.
    - Both parties use the platform’s project management tools to set goals, assign tasks, monitor progress, and communicate regularly.
    - The CXO provides strategic guidance, helps implement solutions, and tracks key performance indicators (KPIs) using the platform’s dashboard.
- **Emotions**:
    - Trust and satisfaction—the founder sees value in the CXO’s expertise and how it positively impacts the startup.
- **Outcomes**:
    - Project milestones are achieved, and the startup sees measurable improvements (e.g., successful fundraising, market expansion).

### **Stage 6: Review and Feedback**

- **Touchpoints**:
    - In-App Feedback Forms, Post-Engagement Surveys, Performance Reports.
- **Actions**:
    - Upon completion of the engagement, the founder provides feedback on the CXO’s performance and overall platform experience.
    - The founder also receives a performance report detailing the impact of the engagement on key business metrics.
- **Emotions**:
    - Satisfaction or constructive criticism—depending on the outcome of the engagement.
- **Outcomes**:
    - The founder decides to continue using the platform for future needs or recommends it to peers.

### **Stage 7: Retention and Loyalty**

- **Touchpoints**:
    - Follow-Up Emails, Loyalty Programs, Invitations to Exclusive Webinars and Events.
- **Actions**:
    - The platform sends personalized follow-up emails with recommendations for future CXO engagements based on the startup’s evolving needs.
    - The founder is invited to join the Strategix community and participate in exclusive webinars, events, or forums.
- **Emotions**:
    - Loyalty and belonging—the founder feels valued as part of a growing community.
- **Outcomes**:
    - The startup remains a loyal customer and actively engages with the platform for ongoing needs.
