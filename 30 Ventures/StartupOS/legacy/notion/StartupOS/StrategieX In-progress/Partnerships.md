### Crafting Partners Experience

**A. Partner Experience Pillars**

These are foundational themes that define the quality of experience across all touchpoints:

1. Speed – Fast onboarding, quick resolutions, rapid feedback loops.
2. Clarity – Simple, jargon-free communication and assets.
3. Support – Human-first help with clear escalation paths.
4. Value – Every interaction delivers ROI for the partner.
5. Delight – Personal, celebratory, and emotionally engaging moments.

**B. Partner Experience Lifecycle Framework**

This is the full journey from first touch to evangelist, mapped with touchpoints and experience goals:

| **Stage** | **Experience Name** | **Touchpoints** | **Experience Objective** | **Internal Notes & Best Practices** |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1. Awareness | The First Impression | - LinkedIn/email outreach- Intro video- Event booth or meetup | Build curiosity & credibility | Personalized messaging, founder video or leadership hook |
| 2. Evaluation | The Trust Phase | - Discovery call- Partner intro deck- Use cases shared- Qualification checklist | Build trust & alignment | Tailored ROI narrative + pain-point mirroring |
| 3. Conversion | Sealing the Deal | - Proposal doc- Revenue model walk-through- Agreement signing | Create clarity and urgency | Use DocSend analytics to track views; time your follow-up |
| 4. Onboarding | First 7 Days Win | - Welcome email- WhatsApp/Slack group- Access to Partner Portal- Training calendar- “First Win” checklist | Ensure setup, excitement, and enablement | Gamify activation: badge or tier on completion |
| 5. Enablement | Power to Sell | - Product training modules- Sales pitch battle cards- Demo walkthrough kits- Vertical use-case decks | Equip for confidence & speed | Partner Academy LMS + certification track |
| 6. Activation | Win Together | - First referral submitted or customer closed- Internal celebration (Slack shoutout)- Partner dashboard update | Reinforce momentum | Public recognition builds stickiness |
| 7. Ongoing Engagement | The Partnership Flywheel | - Biweekly check-ins- Co-branded campaigns- Partner newsletter- Quarterly partner reviews | Deepen engagement, align goals | Add “deal clinic” sessions or shared KPIs |
| 8. Advocacy | Evangelist Mode | - Case study spotlight- Partner Leaderboard- Invite to Partner Council or Beta Group | Turn into power partner | Build social proof + exclusive access perks |
| 9. Retention/Upgrade | Growth Partner | - Annual bonus- Strategic tier upgrade- Joint planning workshop | Cement loyalty & grow share | Introduce new revenue streams or incentives |
