# Research & Data

### Product

- **Product Overview**
    1. **Financial Insights & Reports Portal**
        - **Target Audience:** Existing customers (B2B - SMBs, CFOs, financial managers). Small to medium-sized businesses, entrepreneurs, CFOs, and financial managers struggling with financial insights and compliance.
        - **Key Features:**
            - KPIs on financials
            - Receivables and payables tracking
            - Financial statements generation
            - Financial accounting reports
    2. **Tax and Compliance Calendar Generator**
        - **Target Audience:** Any user (potential new customers, existing customers)
        - **Key Features:**
            - Generate tax and compliance calendars (VAT, CT, AML, CSR, ESR, FATCA)
            - Deadline reminders and CTAs to reach out to the Finanshels team
            - Company profile creation and team member addition
    3. **Admin Side Team App**
        - **Target Audience:** Finanshels internal team
        - **Key Features:**
            - Client list and profiles
            - Tax and compliance calendar management on behalf of clients
            - KPI tracking
- **User Onboarding & Activation**
    - **Objective:** Ensure that new users have a seamless and engaging onboarding experience to drive self-service adoption and satisfaction, leveraging a Product-Led Growth (PLG) approach.
    - **Actions:**
        - **Welcome Emails:** Send personalized welcome emails that introduce new users to the product's core benefits and features, guiding them on how to get started.
        - **Self-Service Tutorials:** Provide intuitive, self-paced tutorials and video guides within the app to help users understand and utilize key features.
        - **Interactive Onboarding:** Implement interactive in-app walkthroughs that guide users through initial setup and essential actions, ensuring they experience value quickly.
        - **In-App Messaging:** Use contextual in-app messages and tooltips to highlight features and provide tips based on user behavior and progress.
        - **Resource Center:** Create an accessible, in-app resource center with FAQs, how-to articles, and troubleshooting guides to support users at every stage.
        - **Automated Check-Ins:** Schedule automated check-in emails or notifications to encourage users to complete onboarding steps and explore more features.
        - **User Feedback Loop:** Implement feedback forms and in-app surveys to gather user insights and continuously enhance the onboarding experience.
        - **Community Engagement:** Foster a community environment through forums, user groups, and regular webinars to support peer learning and engagement.
        - **Freemium Upsell:** Strategically place upsell prompts within the app to encourage users to explore premium features, based on their usage patterns and needs.

### Research & Preparation

- **Market Research & Analysis**
    - **Objective:** Understand market needs, competition, and identify unique value propositions.
    - **Actions:**
        - Conduct surveys and interviews with target users.
            - **Methodology:**
                - Develop detailed questionnaires focusing on pain points, desired features, and current solutions.
                - Use online survey tools like SurveyMonkey or Typeform to distribute the surveys.
                - Schedule one-on-one interviews with key stakeholders such as CFOs, financial managers, and entrepreneurs.
            - **Execution:**
                - Reach out to existing clients for feedback and insights.
                - Partner with industry forums and professional networks to access a broader audience.
                - Offer incentives for survey participation, such as discounts or free trial periods.
            - **Key Questions:**
                - What are your biggest challenges in financial management and compliance?
                - Which accounting software do you currently use?
                - What features do you find most valuable in a financial management tool?
                - How do you currently manage your tax and compliance deadlines?
                - What would make you switch to a new financial management platform?
        - Analyze competitors’ offerings and identify gaps.
            - **Research:**
                - Perform a SWOT analysis (Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats) on top competitors like QuickBooks, Xero, and TaxJar.
                - Evaluate features, pricing models, user reviews, and customer support.
                - Use tools like SEMrush or Ahrefs to analyze competitors’ online presence and marketing strategies.
            - **Gap Analysis:**
                - Identify unaddressed pain points in existing solutions.
                - Highlight areas where competitors lack comprehensive features, particularly in tax compliance and financial insights.
        - Define unique selling points (USPs) for each product feature.
            - **Feature Mapping:**
                - List all key features of the financial insights and tax compliance platform, and the admin-side team app.
                - Align each feature with specific user needs and pain points identified from surveys and interviews.
            - **USP Development:**
                - Financial Insights Platform:
                    - **Comprehensive Financial Overview:** Provides real-time KPIs, receivables, payables, and financial statements.
                    - **Automated Compliance Calendar:** Generates tax and compliance schedules with reminders for VAT, CT, AML, CSR, ESR, FATCA.
                    - **User-Centric Design:** Simplified company profile creation and team member management.
            - **Admin-Side Team App:**
                - **Centralized Client Management:** Allows easy access to client profiles and compliance tracking.
                - **Real-Time KPI Monitoring:** Facilitates efficient performance tracking and reporting.
                - **Collaborative Tools:** Enhances team collaboration through shared access and task management.
- **Competitive Analysis**
    - **SWOT Analysis (Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats)**
        - **Objective:** Understand the competitive landscape and identify areas for improvement.
        - **Actions:**
            - Conduct a SWOT analysis of Finanshels and key competitors.
            - Identify strengths to leverage, weaknesses to address, opportunities to pursue, and threats to mitigate.
            - Use insights to refine product features and marketing strategies.
    - **Market Positioning**
        - **Objective:** Define a clear and compelling market position for the product.
        - **Actions:**
            - Analyze competitors’ positioning and identify gaps in the market.
            - Develop a unique value proposition and key messaging for the product.

    - **Efficiency:** Automate financial insights and compliance tasks to save time.

    - **User Experience:** Simplified user interface for easy navigation and quick access to information.

### Strategies

- **User Adoption Strategy**
    - **Pilot Program:** Start with a small group of users to gather feedback and make improvements.
    - **Incentives:** Offer incentives for early adopters and power users who provide valuable feedback.
    - **Feedback Loop:** Continuously collect and implement user feedback to improve the app.
- **Product-Led Growth (PLG) Strategy**
    - **Freemium Model:** Offer a basic version with essential features for free, and premium tiers with advanced features.
    - **Self-Service Onboarding:** Design an intuitive onboarding process with tutorials and in-app guidance.
    - **In-App Upsell:** Promote premium features through contextual in-app messaging based on user behavior.
    - **Community Building:** Create forums, webinars, and user groups for peer support and product advocacy.
    - **Referral Program:** Encourage existing users to refer new customers with incentives.
- **User-Led Growth (ULG) Strategy**
    - **Customer Feedback Loop:** Implement feedback mechanisms within the app to gather user insights for continuous improvement.
    - **Personalized User Experience:** Use data analytics to personalize the user journey and tailor features to individual needs.
    - **User Education:** Develop comprehensive educational content (videos, blogs, webinars) to empower users to maximize the platform’s potential.
    - **Customer Success Team:** Provide proactive support to guide users in achieving their goals, fostering loyalty and advocacy.
    - **Customer Advocacy Program**
        - **Identify Early Adopters:** Engage with early adopters and provide them with exceptional support and exclusive access to new features.
        - **Build Case Studies:** Develop detailed case studies showcasing the success of early adopters.
        - **Customer Testimonials:** Encourage satisfied customers to provide testimonials and reviews on key platforms.
- **Pricing Strategy**
    - **Freemium Model:** Offer a basic version for free with the option to upgrade to premium features. Basic features free, with tiered pricing for advanced functionalities.
    - **Tiered Pricing:** Provide multiple pricing tiers based on the number of users, features, and support levels. Align pricing with the value delivered to users, considering different business sizes and needs.
- **Sales Strategies**
    - **Free Trials/Demos:** Offer a limited-time free trial to showcase the platform's capabilities. Offer a 14-day free trial with full access to premium features.
    - **Webinars/Workshops:** Conduct educational webinars and workshops to demonstrate how the platform works. Conduct live demos and Q&A sessions to showcase the platform.
    - **Onboarding Support:** Provide excellent onboarding support to ensure smooth adoption and satisfaction.

### Execution Plan

- **Product Launch Plan**
    - **Objective:** Strategically introduce the products to the market.
        - **Soft Launch:** Release to a select group for feedback and initial traction.
        - **Official Launch:** Comprehensive launch with PR, press releases, and events.
    - **Actions:**
        - **Pre-Launch:**
            - Generate buzz through teaser campaigns on social media and email newsletters.
            - Collaborate with industry influencers for product previews.
            - Offer exclusive beta access to key customers for testimonials.
        - **Launch:**
            - Host a virtual launch event showcasing product demos and success stories.
            - Deploy press releases to industry publications.
            - Utilize paid ads (LinkedIn, Google) targeting SMBs, CFOs, and financial managers.
        - **Post-Launch:**
            - Continue engagement through webinars and workshops.
            - Collect user feedback for continuous improvement.
- **Step-by-Step Approach:**
    1. **Survey Distribution and Data Collection:**
        - Send surveys via email to existing clients.
        - Share survey links on social media and professional networks.
        - Conduct follow-up interviews with willing participants for deeper insights.
    2. **Competitor Analysis:**
        - Assign team members to focus on different competitors and compile a comprehensive report.

    3. **USP Definition and Validation:**
        - Create a draft of USPs and share them with a select group of users for feedback.
        - Refine USPs based on feedback and ensure they resonate with the target audience.
    4. **Feature Mapping:**
        - List all key features of the financial insights and tax compliance platform, and the admin-side team app.
        - Align each feature with specific user needs and pain points identified from surveys and interviews.
    5. **USP Development:**
        - Financial Insights Platform:
            - **Comprehensive Financial Overview:** Provides real-time KPIs, receivables, payables, and financial statements.
            - **Automated Compliance Calendar:** Generates tax and compliance schedules with reminders for VAT, CT, AML, CSR, ESR, FATCA.
            - **User-Centric Design:** Simplified company profile creation and team member management.
- **Implementation Timeline**
    
    **Week 1: Preparation Phase**
    
    - Conduct market research and finalize positioning & messaging.
