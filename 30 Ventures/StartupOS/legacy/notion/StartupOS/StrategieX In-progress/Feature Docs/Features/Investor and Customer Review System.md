# Investor and Customer Review System

Product Area: Validation
Feature Description: A feature that allows investors and customers to provide reviews, ratings, and feedback on startups directly on their profiles. This system is designed to validate startups through social proof, helping other potential investors and customers make informed decisions. Reviews can be structured around specific criteria, such as product quality, team effectiveness, growth potential, and customer satisfaction.

Feature Status: Not started
Priority Level: Could-Have
Users: CXOs, Startup Founders
