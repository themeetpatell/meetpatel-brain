# CXO Peer Reviews and Endorsements

Product Area: Validation
Complexity: 3
Demand: 20
Depedencies: Review Moderation Tools: To ensure reviews are constructive and relevant.

Notification System: To alert CXOs when they receive new reviews or endorsements.

UI/UX Design: To create a user-friendly interface for managing and displaying reviews.
Efforts: 55
Feature Description: A system that allows CXOs to receive reviews and endorsements from peers, previous clients, and startups they have worked with. This feature adds a layer of social proof, allowing CXOs to build their credibility and trust within the platform. It includes detailed review criteria such as leadership skills, strategic impact, communication effectiveness, and more. CXOs can also endorse each other, highlighting specific skills or accomplishments.
