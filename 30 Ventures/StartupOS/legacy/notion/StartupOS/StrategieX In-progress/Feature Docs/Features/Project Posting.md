Product Area: Project Management
Complexity: 3
Demand: 15

Feature Description: The Project Posting feature allows startups to publish their created projects on the Strategix platform, making them visible to potential CXOs who are looking for opportunities. This feature includes visibility settings, filters for targeting specific types of CXOs, and options for editing or pausing a project post. It ensures that startups can effectively broadcast their needs to the right audience.
