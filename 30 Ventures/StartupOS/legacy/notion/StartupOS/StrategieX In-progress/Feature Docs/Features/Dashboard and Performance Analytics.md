Feature Description: The Dashboard and Performance Analytics feature provides both startups and CXOs with an overview of their activities, engagement history, and key performance metrics. Startups can view the performance of engaged CXOs, track project milestones, and see engagement ROI. CXOs can analyze their proposal success rates, client feedback, and areas for improvement. The goal is to provide actionable insights that help users optimize their strategies and engagement effectiveness.
