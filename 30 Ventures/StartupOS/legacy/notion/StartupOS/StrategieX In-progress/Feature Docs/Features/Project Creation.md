Feature Description: The Project Creation feature allows startups to define their project requirements, objectives, timelines, budgets, and other essential details. This feature will guide startup founders through a structured process to clearly articulate their needs, making it easier for potential CXOs to understand the scope and requirements. The goal is to ensure that projects are well-defined from the start, reducing miscommunication and setting clear expectations.
