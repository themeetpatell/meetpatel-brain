# CXO Role Rotation and Dynamic Team Building

User Interface for Team Management: UX/UI to support adding, removing, and rotating CXOs seamlessly.

Efforts: 70
Feature Description: This feature allows startups to dynamically build their leadership teams by rotating CXOs in and out based on their evolving needs. Startups often face rapidly changing challenges as they grow, requiring different types of leadership expertise at different times. The CXO Role Rotation feature enables startups to set specific timeframes for fractional CXO roles (e.g., 3 months for a turnaround CFO, followed by 6 months of a growth-focused CMO), ensuring they always have the right expertise at the right time.
