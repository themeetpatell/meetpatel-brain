# Meet’s Overview

### **System Architecture Overview**

- **Core Modules:**
    - **CRM (Leads & Customer Management):** Lead tracking, customer segmentation, communication logs.
    - **Marketing Automation:** Campaigns across LinkedIn, Instagram, Twitter, email, and WhatsApp.
    - **CSR Management:** Track CSR activities, impact reports, proposals, and partner collaborations.
    - **Accounting & Invoicing:** Integrated billing, PO tracking, and financial reports.
    - **Document Repository:** Centralized storage for logos, certificates, product manuals, awards, proposals.
    - **Logistics Management:** Shipment tracking, distribution workflows, and government order handling.
    - **Customer Training Portal:** Product guides, video tutorials, impact stories.
    - **Support & Help Desk:** Ticketing system, knowledge base, customer feedback loops.

### **Leads Management System**

- **Lead Sources Integration:**
    - **Direct:**
        - APIs for **Alibaba**, **IndiaMart** to auto-capture leads.
        - Social Media Integration: **LinkedIn, Instagram, Twitter** via marketing platforms like HubSpot or Zoho CRM.
    - **Cold Calls/Emails:** Import data from CSR databases, track visits, and outreach metrics.
    - **Customer Reach Channels:**
        - **Calls, Mails, WhatsApp** integrated with CRM for centralized tracking.
        - **Exhibitions/Referrals/Other:** Custom lead forms for quick data capture.
- **Lead Categorization:**
    - B2G | B2B | B2C | Export | CSR (tag-based system for easy filtering).

### **Marketing Automation**

- **Email Campaigns:** Templates for CSR outreach, product launches, newsletters.
- **Social Media Scheduler:** Pre-plan posts, track engagement, maintain branding consistency.
- **Analytics Dashboard:** Lead conversion rates, campaign performance, and ROI tracking.

### **Centralized Document Repository**

- Organized folders:
    - **Logos:** Torchit, partners, customers.
    - **Awards & Certifications:** Easily accessible for marketing or proposals.
    - **Product Resources:** Manuals, usage guides, feedback, impact videos.
    - **CSR Reports & Impact Proposals:** Ready for government/CSR submissions.
    - **Invoices, Work Orders, Completions:** Integrated with the accounting module.

### **Accounting & Finance**

- **Invoices & PO Management:** Track government POs, CSR funds, B2B invoices.
- **Expense Management:** Linked with logistics for distribution costs.
- **Financial Reporting:** CSR fund utilization reports, impact audits.

### **Customer Training & Support**

- **Training Hub:** “How-to” videos, impact stories, manuals.
- **Support Center:** Ticketing for product issues, FAQs, WhatsApp integration for quick support.
- **Feedback Loop:** Surveys to measure customer satisfaction and gather insights.

### **CSR & Impact Reporting**

- **CSR Dashboard:** Track partnerships, activities, budgets, and impact metrics.
- **Automated Reports:** For government and corporate stakeholders (impact reports, fund utilization).
- **Proposal Builder:** Templates for CSR grant applications.
