# Implementation Plan

### **Project Objectives**

- Streamline operations across CSR, Leads Management, Accounting, Mail, Logistics, Customer Training, Support, and Customer Satisfaction.
- Integrate key platforms (Alibaba, IndiaMart, LinkedIn, Instagram, WhatsApp) with internal systems.
- Centralize data and documents for efficiency and impact reporting.

### **System Architecture Overview - Core Modules**

1. **CRM (Leads & Customer Management)**
2. **Marketing Automation Platform**
3. **CSR Impact & Activity Management System**
4. **Accounting & Finance Management Tool**
5. **Document Repository System**
6. **Logistics & Distribution Management**
7. **Customer Training Portal**
8. **Support/Help Desk System**
- **CRM & Lead Management:** **Zoho CRM or Hubspot (Costlier)** (both support Alibaba, IndiaMart, LinkedIn integrations).
- **Marketing Automation:** **Mailchimp** (for emails) + **Buffer/Hootsuite** (for social media scheduling). (Or Zoho One)
- **CSR Impact Management:** Zoho One and **Notion** (for tracking CSR activities, reports, and proposals). (Notion and Zoho One Custom Module development)
- **Document Repository:** **Google Drive** or Zoho Workdrive (for centralized document management). (G Drive or Zoho One)
- **Accounting:** **Zoho Books**
- **Customer Support:** **Zoho Desk** (for ticketing and support automation).
- **Logistics:** **Shiprocket** or **EasyPost** (integrates with eCommerce and government logistics APIs).
- **Integration Platform:** **Zapier** for automating workflows without coding.
