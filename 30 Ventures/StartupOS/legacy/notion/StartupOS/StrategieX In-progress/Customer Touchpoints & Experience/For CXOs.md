# For CXOs

### **1. Awareness Touchpoints**

- **LinkedIn Ads and Professional Networks**
    - **Experience**: High-impact ads on LinkedIn and professional networks highlighting the benefits of joining Strategix as a fractional CXO. Testimonials from other CXOs who have had successful engagements.
    - **Key Elements**: Strong messaging around flexibility, quality of engagements, and income stability, with a clear CTA ("Join as a CXO Today").
    - **Goal**: Attract high-quality CXO talent to the platform.
- **Webinars and Peer Networking Events**
    - **Experience**: Exclusive webinars and events for CXOs discussing industry trends, the benefits of fractional roles, and how to succeed on platforms like Strategix.
    - **Key Elements**: Expert speakers, interactive Q&A, networking opportunities, and follow-up content.
    - **Goal**: Build trust, establish authority, and drive sign-ups.

### **2. Registration and Onboarding Touchpoints**

- **Profile Setup and Verification Wizard**
    - **Experience**: A streamlined onboarding process where CXOs can quickly set up their profiles, verify their credentials, and define their engagement preferences.
    - **Key Elements**: Step-by-step guidance, pre-populated data fields (from LinkedIn), progress indicators, and document upload functionality.
    - **Goal**: Ensure high-quality profiles and a quick onboarding experience to increase engagement readiness.
- **Onboarding Webinars and Tutorials**
    - **Experience**: Live onboarding webinars and interactive tutorials that help CXOs understand how to maximize their visibility and engagement on the platform.
    - **Key Elements**: Step-by-step guides, live Q&A, and downloadable content.
    - **Goal**: Reduce friction and prepare CXOs for successful engagements.

### **3. Engagement and Interaction Touchpoints**

- **Personalized Match Notifications**
    - **Experience**: Real-time notifications about potential startup matches that align with the CXO’s skills, experience, and availability.
    - **Key Elements**: Mobile push notifications, email alerts, in-app notifications with match details and quick response options.
    - **Goal**: Drive engagement by keeping CXOs informed and proactive in seeking new opportunities.
- **In-App Messaging and Engagement Proposal Tools**
    - **Experience**: An integrated messaging system that allows CXOs to communicate directly with interested startups and submit tailored engagement proposals.
    - **Key Elements**: One-click message initiation, customizable proposal templates, and digital signatures.
    - **Goal**: Simplify the process of securing engagements and improving conversion rates.

### **4. Engagement Execution Touchpoints**

- **Engagement Management Dashboard**
    - **Experience**: A centralized dashboard where CXOs can manage their engagements, view timelines, track deliverables, and collaborate with startup teams.
    - **Key Elements**: Customizable dashboards, goal-setting tools, progress trackers, and automated status updates.
    - **Goal**: Enhance productivity and ensure a smooth execution of engagements.
- **CXO Community and Peer Support Forum**
    - **Experience**: An exclusive forum where CXOs can connect, share insights, and seek advice from peers and Strategix experts.
    - **Key Elements**: Discussion boards, expert Q&A sessions, and peer recognition features.
    - **Goal**: Build a supportive network that encourages retention and continuous learning.

### **5. Feedback, Reputation Building, and Retention Touchpoints (Continued)**

- **Performance Feedback and Ratings**
    - **Experience**: After each engagement, CXOs receive detailed feedback and ratings from startups, which are prominently displayed on their profiles. Positive feedback can enhance their visibility and credibility on the platform.
    - **Key Elements**: Feedback summaries, star ratings, qualitative comments, "Top Performer" badges for high ratings, and personalized improvement tips for lower ratings.
    - **Goal**: Encourage CXOs to maintain high standards of service, helping them build a strong reputation and attract more engagements.
- **Post-Engagement Reviews and Case Study Opportunities**
    - **Experience**: CXOs who consistently receive high ratings are invited to co-create case studies that highlight their successful engagements. These case studies are published on the Strategix platform, website, and social channels.
    - **Key Elements**: Professional write-ups, co-branded content, downloadable PDFs, and featured spots in newsletters.
    - **Goal**: Recognize top-performing CXOs, build their personal brand, and attract more premium opportunities.
- **Professional Development and Learning Opportunities**
    - **Experience**: Access to exclusive content such as executive coaching sessions, leadership workshops, and advanced training programs in strategic areas (e.g., digital transformation, crisis management).
    - **Key Elements**: Webinar series, certification programs, mentorship opportunities, and discounts on external courses.
    - **Goal**: Enhance CXO skills, increase their value on the platform, and boost retention through continuous professional development.

### **6. Loyalty and Advocacy Touchpoints**

- **Loyalty Rewards Program**
    - **Experience**: CXOs earn points or credits for each successful engagement, high ratings, and participation in community activities. These points can be redeemed for platform benefits like discounted fees, featured profile spots, or access to exclusive startup pitches.
    - **Key Elements**: Tiered loyalty program (Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum), points leaderboard, and regular updates on rewards status.
    - **Goal**: Foster loyalty, encourage continued use of the platform, and reward active and high-performing CXOs.
- **CXO Referral Program**
    - **Experience**: Encourage CXOs to refer their peers to the platform. For each successful referral, they receive benefits such as monetary rewards, increased visibility, or exclusive access to premium engagements.
    - **Key Elements**: Easy referral links, automated tracking, and transparent reward structures.
    - **Goal**: Expand the pool of high-quality CXOs on the platform and leverage word-of-mouth marketing.
- **Community Recognition and Awards**
    - **Experience**: Annual or quarterly awards recognizing "CXO of the Month/Year," "Best in Industry," or "Most Innovative Strategy." Winners are featured on the platform, social media, and newsletters.
    - **Key Elements**: Virtual award ceremonies, featured spotlights, certificates, and exclusive badges on profiles.
    - **Goal**: Build a sense of pride and community among CXOs, driving engagement and platform loyalty.
