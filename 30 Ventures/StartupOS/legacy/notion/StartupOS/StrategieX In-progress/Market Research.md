# Market Research

- **Market Size and Opportunity**: Estimate the Total Addressable Market (TAM), Serviceable Available Market (SAM), and Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM). Even at this early stage, showing an understanding of market size and opportunity helps attract early-stage investors.
- **Target Audience**: Clearly define your target customer segments (early-stage startups, growth-stage startups, CXOs) and outline their needs, behaviors, and potential adoption barriers.
- **Competitive Landscape**: Conduct a high-level analysis of the competitive landscape. Identify existing solutions, their limitations, and how Strategix offers a differentiated approach.
