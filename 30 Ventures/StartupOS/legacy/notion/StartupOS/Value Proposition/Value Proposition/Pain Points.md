# Pain Points

Founders: No time, no team, no clarity, scattered tools, lack of expert guidance, fear of failure
Fractional CXOs: Chasing bad leads, unstructured roles, lack of visibility, limited bandwidth
Investors & Accelerators: Can’t support all startups equally, lack of execution visibility, poor differentiation
Startup Communities & Platforms: Drop-off after initial engagement, limited tools, weak monetization, undifferentiated content
