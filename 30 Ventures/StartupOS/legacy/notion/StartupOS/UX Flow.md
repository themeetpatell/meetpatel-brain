# UX Flow

### **1. Dynamic Role-Based Dashboard (Home)**

> Smart, role-aware homepage
> 
- Founders: see KPIs, AI prompts, next-step tasks, open deals
- CXOs: active projects, assigned tasks, playbooks, payouts
- VCs: portfolio health, alerts, active CXO support
- Communities: member activity, engagement analytic

### **2.Copilot Hub**

> Access to copilots by domain
> 
- Finance Copilot (recon, runway, tax, pricing)
- Hiring Copilot (JD → Interview → Onboarding)
- GTM Copilot (campaign, outreach, growth ideas)
- Fundraising Copilot (deck builder, investor CRM, data room)

### **3. Execution Workspace**

> The operating layer for startups
> 
- Task flows, OKRs, check-ins
- Milestones + momentum tracking
- Workflow templates, progress bars
- Playbook library (editable, sharable)

### **4. CXO Marketplace**

> Expert matching and project management
> 
- Discovery: match by stage, pain, and objective
- Scope builder: define role, time, deliverables
- Engagement dashboard: chats, documents, milestones
- Ratings + reputation layer

### **5. Insights Engine**

> Smart, real-time reporting
> 
- Founders: personal KPIs, growth curves, traction reports
- VCs: portfolio overview, execution scores
- CXOs: impact analytics, delivery metrics
- Communities: engagement reports, referral performance

### **6. Ecosystem Hub**

> Everything external
> 
- Templates & tools marketplace
- Referral + revenue-sharing panel
- VC/Community co-branded portals
- Partner APIs / integrations
