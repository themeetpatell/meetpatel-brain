# Features

### **1. Founders**

**Need**: Structure, speed, leadership, clarity

**Features**:

1. **AI Copilots per Domain** – Finance, GTM, Hiring, Ops copilots that *do*, not just suggest
2. **Startup Dashboard** – A real-time cockpit with KPIs, team tasks, capital runway, and goals
3. **Stage-Aware Onboarding** – Product adapts to startup lifecycle (idea, MVP, pre-seed, seed, etc.)
4. **Fractional CXO Marketplace** – On-demand vetted experts available for outcome-based projects
5. **Execution Playbooks** – Ready-to-launch GTM, hiring, fundraising workflows embedded into the OS
6. **Investor Data Room Builder** – One-click setup for pitch decks, cap table, traction, and KPIs
7. **Momentum Tracker** – A nudge-based system that keeps them moving weekly
8. **Co-Founder & Team Stack Builder** – AI-recommended talent suggestions + hiring workflows
9. **A Real-Time, Living Map of the Global Startup Ecosystem**

### **2. Fractional CXOs**

**Need**: Qualified work, structure, visibility, scale

**Features**:

1. **Smart Matching Engine** – Match by stage, role, goals, and industry—not just resume
2. **Scoped Engagement Builder** – Set timeline, deliverables, pricing, and outcomes per project
3. **Reputation Graph** – Verified performance, testimonials, and portfolio display
4. **CXO Dashboard** – Central workspace to manage multiple startups, meetings, and tasks
5. **Copilot Integration** – Use AI copilots alongside to scale their delivery (e.g., finance copilots assisting CFOs)
6. **Billing & Payout Management** – Built-in invoicing, milestone tracking, and escrow options
7. **Playbook Publishing** – CXOs can contribute their own methods to the StartupOS library

### **3. VCs & Accelerators**

**Need**: Portfolio enablement, differentiation, performance visibility

**Features**:

1. **Portfolio Command Center** – View startup progress, KPIs, and health metrics across all investments
2. **Co-branded Founder OS** – Offer StartupOS with your logo, values, onboarding layer
3. **Execution Insights Engine** – Track which startups are executing, which are stuck, and why
4. **Accelerator Playbooks** – Add your own templates, milestones, and guides
5. **CXO Pool Access** – Help your startups hire fractional leaders with pre-vetted, ready-to-deploy experts
6. **Funding Pipeline Monitor** – Track which startups are pitch-ready or need strategic help
7. **Founder Activation Report** – Monthly insights into product usage, momentum, and gaps

### **4. Communities & Platforms**

**Need**: Retention, monetization, unique value

**Features**:

1. **White-Labeled StartupOS** – Offer your own branded version with your identity and member routing
2. **Affiliate + Rev Share Program** – Earn on each startup activated and CXO engagement closed
3. **Gamified Member Dashboards** – Your members get progress bars, unlockables, and stage goals
4. **Community Leader Admin Panel** – Track usage, engagement, startup growth per member
5. **Integrated Events + Perks Layer** – Host AMAs, challenges, or share community-only perks in-platform
6. **Founder Wall of Progress** – Public or private progress boards founders can opt into

### **Unified Execution Dashboard**

> One central place to track goals, workflows, KPIs, and momentum—customized by role.
> 
- Founders see product, hiring, and capital metrics
- CXOs see project scope and performance
- VCs see portfolio progress
- Communities see member activation

### **AI Copilot Framework**

> Domain-specific AI copilots (Finance, Ops, Hiring, GTM) that assist every user—no matter the role.
> 
- Founders use them to do the work
- CXOs use them to scale delivery
- VCs see better execution from their portfolio
- Communities offer AI support as a value layer

### **Stage-Aware Smart Onboarding**

> Every user’s journey adapts to startup lifecycle (idea → scale) and role-specific context
> 
- Ensures focus, relevance, and momentum from day one

### **Milestone & Outcome Tracking Engine**

> Define and measure success across users—whether it’s traction, delivery, fundraising, or engagement
> 
- Used for contracts, reporting, investor updates, and accountability

### **Marketplace Access (People + Playbooks)**

> Cross-role marketplace for:
> 
- Fractional CXOs
- Prebuilt playbooks
- Templates and expert libraries
- Smart matching based on pain points, stage, and urgency

### **Communication & Collaboration Layer**

> Lightweight task assignment, progress nudges, and contextual chat for team + advisor interactions
> 
- Think “Slack + Notion + OKR” style, embedded in workflows

### **Gamified Progress System**

> Points, badges, and stage unlocks create momentum and retention
> 
- Keeps users engaged, motivated, and accountable
- Especially powerful in communities and early-stage founder journe

### **Billing, Contracts & Access Control**

> Built-in logic for paid engagements, outcome-based contracts, and freemium-to-premium upgrades
> 
- Protects all parties and aligns incentives
- Enables monetization for CXOs, communities, and platform partners
