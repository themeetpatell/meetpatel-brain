## **🧠 MindMate Feature Set (No Swipes. Only Signals.)**

### **1. VisionMatch™ Engine (Core Matchmaking AI)**

- Matches based on alignment of:
    - Long-term vision
    - Life/work philosophy
    - Risk appetite
    - Builder archetype (hacker, hustler, designer, artist, strategist, etc.)
- Think of it like DNA matching — but for founders.

---

### **2. Pitch-to-Date™ Profiles**

- Founders upload a 60–90 sec intro video (pitch-style):
    - Who I am
    - What I’m building
    - What I value in love
    - My life rhythm
- Adds realness, removes ghosting, filters the unserious.

---

### **3. Compatibility Dashboard**

- Transparent score breakdown:
    - Mission Alignment: 88%
    - Risk Sync: 75%
    - Emotional Availability: 91%
    - Work-Life Design Fit: 82%
- Instead of “hot or not,” it’s “do our lives orbit the same future?”

---

### **4. Inner Circle Referrals**

- You get endorsed by people in your network.
- Vouching happens via “soft intros” like YC-style references.
- Unlock *verified vibes* instead of verified blue ticks.

---

### **5. Co-Builder Chemistry Quizzes**

- Deep, psych-verified modules:
    - “How do you handle failure?”
    - “Your ideal weekend?”
    - “How do you show up in chaos?”
- Your answers train the AI and unlock better matches.

---

### **6. Collab-Date™ Mode**

- Schedule virtual co-working dates
- Do a 30-min async check-in before the first call:
    - Mood
    - What I’m building today
    - My headspace
- Shared calendar slots with in-app “break & talk” prompts

---

### **7. Heartbeat Check-Ins**

- Micro-prompts daily/weekly:
    - “How was your week emotionally?”
    - “Where do you feel misunderstood?”
- Shared vulnerability leads to real connection. Also: a real-time emotional health log.

---

### **8. Date-as-a-Project Tracker**

- Notion-style board for the relationship:
    - Shared goals
    - Boundaries
    - Red flag signals
    - Gratitude moments
- This *isn’t just love.* It’s a growth sprint.

---

### **9. Build & Bond Retreats (Offline)**

- Curated, non-cringy, invite-only events:
    - Founder wellness + intimate vibe matching
    - Think “TED meets tantra” not “speed dating”
- AI gives retreat invites based on behavior + openness

---

### **10. No-Swipe Discovery Feed**

- A smart vertical scroll:
    - AI-curated list of 3–5 profiles/day
    - Deep profiles (vision, values, day-in-life, story, not stats)
    - You can react with voice, video, or thoughtful responses

---

### **11. “Red Flag Radar”**

- AI detects potential mismatches in value systems or lifestyles early
- Sends both parties soft alerts with reflection prompts like:
    - “Your co-intentions may be misaligned on work-life balance. Want to talk it out?”

---

### **12. Time Capsule Mode**

- A journal of your founder-love journey:
    - Day 1: How you felt when you matched
    - Day 30: Emotional patterns
    - Day 90: Life changes together
- Personal and private. But epic.

---

## **✨ Bonus: Founder Dating UX Principles**
