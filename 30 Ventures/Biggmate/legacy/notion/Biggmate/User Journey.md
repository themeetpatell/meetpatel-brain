# User Journey

### **1. Home (Discovery Hub)**

Goal: Help founders discover pitches, people, and opportunities.

**Features:**

- **Founder Feed** → active pitches, trending ideas, recommended matches.
- **AI Suggestions** → “Top 3 cofounders for you this week.”
- **Pitch Quick View** → headline only, click to reveal deeper profile.
- **Filters** → by skills, industry, stage, geography.
- **Actions** → View Pitch • Send Pitch • Save for Later.

👉 *Mindset:* First-touch inspiration, like a startup Tinder × LinkedIn.

### **2. Pitches (4 Tabs)**

Goal: Manage all founder connections in a structured pipeline.

**Subsections:**

- **Sent Pitches** → all founders you’ve reached out to.
- **Received Pitches** → inbound interest awaiting response.
- **Accepted Matches** → mutual connections that move to Workspace.
- **Declined** → archive of rejected connections.

**Features:**

- Engagement stats (views, likes, response rates).
- Status tags (Pending, Accepted, Declined).
- “Nudge” feature if no reply after 7 days.

👉 *Mindset:* This is your **founder CRM.**

### **3. Workspace (4 Tabs)**

Goal: Give matched cofounders tools to validate, plan, and start building.

**Subsections:**

- **Build (Vision + Roles + Docs)**
    
    Everything needed to define and align the **startup foundation**.
    
    - **Vision Board** → Problem, Solution, Market, Roadmap.
    - **Roles & Equity** → Role definitions, equity calculator, founder agreement templates.
    - **Docs & Legal** → NDA, incorporation guide, IP assignment, privacy/ToS templates.
    
    → *what we’re building and how we’re structured.*
    
- **Execute (Tasks + Milestones + Team)**
    
    Tools to actually **work together and make progress**.
    
    - **Task Manager** → Notion-lite Kanban (To Do, In Progress, Review, Done).
    - **Milestones Engine** → Guided 30-day sprints (Alignment → Validation → Prototype → Pitch).
    - **Team View** → Active members, roles, skills, status, contributions.
    
    → *how we move forward as a team.*
    
- **Fund (Finance + Investors + Launch)**
    
    Everything around **money, readiness, and external-facing growth.**
    
    - **Funding Tracker** → Committed $, investor pipeline, target raise.
    - **Pitch Readiness** → Deck upload, financial projections, validation checklist.
    - **Investor Room** → Data room, demo day access, 1-click intros.
    - **Launch Dashboard** → When teams graduate to public product + funding stage.
    
    → *how we bring in resources to grow.*
    
- **Hub (Community + Copilot + Insights)**
    
    The **meta layer** that makes the platform sticky & differentiated.
    
    - **Community Feed** → Success stories, AMAs, events, guilds (AI, fintech, etc).
    - **AI Copilot** → Nudges (*“You’re 60% aligned on pace—sync with Sarah this week”*), smart matching, pitch feedback.
    - **Insights Dashboard** → Progress % (tasks, milestones, funding, team health).
    
    → Keeps founders **engaged, connected, and guided** beyond just tools.
    

👉 *Mindset:* “We matched, now let’s actually build.”

👉 *Differentiator:* Unlike other platforms, Mindmate doesn’t stop at chat — it creates startup momentum.

### **4. Launch (Graduation Layer)**

Goal: Turn teams into official startups and showcase them.

**Subsections:**

- **Startup Passport** → auto-generated profile (team, vision, traction, milestones).
- **Pitch & Showcase** → upload deck, demo video, public startup profile.
- **Investor Hub** → lightweight data room, intro requests.
- **Graduation Badge** → Idea • MVP • Beta • Funded.

👉 *Mindset:* Feels like a **ceremony** — you’re no longer just founders, you’re a startup.

👉 *Next Step:* Seamless migration into StartupOS or Zerohuman (future ecosystem play).

### **5. Community (Network Layer)**

Goal: Keep founders engaged, learning, and motivated.

**Subsections:**

- **Events Hub** → mixers, pitch nights, AMAs with investors.
- **Founder Guilds** → industry-specific groups (AI, fintech, healthtech).
- **Stories & Wins** → curated success stories, milestones achieved.
- **Leaderboard** → most active founders, top startups.

**Features:**

- Invite-only → reduces spam/noise.
- AI Copilot integration → weekly prompts and recommendations.

👉 *Mindset:* The “stickiness” layer → people stay active between milestones.

## **Cross-Cutting Features**

- **AI Copilot:**
    - Smart recommendations (cofounder matches, idea validation prompts).
    - Automated nudges (“3 tasks are overdue in your workspace, want me to prioritize them?”).
    - Conflict resolution prompts (“Your teammate rated risk appetite higher than you — schedule an alignment check-in”).
- **Verification Layer:**
    - Identity check (LinkedIn, GitHub, or email).
    - Skills verification (portfolio, work history, endorsements).
    - Paid verification for premium profiles.
- **Gamification:**
    - Streaks for completing milestones.
    - Badges: “Most Active Founder,” “Investor Ready,” “Top 1% Match.”
    - Graduation badges (Idea → MVP → Funded).
- **Notifications & Nudges**
    - In-app + email notifications.
    - Weekly progress reports (“You completed 70% of your sprint, keep momentum up”).

## **Problem Statement**

**Founders can’t reliably find the right cofounder or build early momentum.**

Directories = too noisy. Accelerators = too selective. Most matches die after a chat.

**Mindmate solves this by combining matching + execution:**

- **Find** the right partner.
- **Build** together with structure.
- **Launch** with credibility.

**Result:** A global founder factory.

## **👥 User Journeys**

### **Journey A: Aspiring Founder → Startup Builder**

1. **Discovers Home Feed** → sees trending startup pitches.
2. **Sends a Pitch** → gets accepted, moves into Workspace.
3. **Builds in Workspace** → validates idea, sets equity split, completes 30-day sprint.
4. **Graduates in Launch** → Startup Passport + Investor-ready profile.

---

### **Journey B: Experienced Operator → Cofounder**

1. **Joins Platform with Profile** → tags skills (CTO, Growth, Finance).
2. **Receives Pitches** → filters by industry & stage.
3. **Accepts Match** → Workspace opens → defines equity & role.
4. **Starts Building** → moves straight into Execute tab (tasks, milestones).

---

### **Journey C: Startup Team → Investors**

1. **Graduates Workspace → Launch tab unlocks.**
2. **Uploads Deck & Demo Video.**
3. **Launch Dashboard** → visible in Showcase, eligible for Demo Days.
4. **Investor Access** → data room shared → intros initiated.

## **💰 Monetization Model**

- **Freemium:** Browse pitches, basic matching.
- **Premium Founder ($20–50/mo):**
    - Verified badge.
    - Unlimited pitches.
    - Advanced filters (skills, funding stage).
    - Workspace AI nudges unlocked.
- **Investor/Accelerator Access ($499+/yr):**
    - Curated startup deal flow.
    - Demo Day participation.
    - Priority data room access.
- **Graduation Success Fee (long term):**
    - % success fee when funded startups graduate.
