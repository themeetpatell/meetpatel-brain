# Finanshels Branding Case Study

In just six months, Finanshels transformed its brand presence with a focused strategy across digital channels, PR pushes, and community engagement. The result was a shift from early traction into market leadership visibility.

**Before vs After (6 Months)**

| **Metric** | **Before (Sept 2024 – Feb 2025)** | **After (Mar – Aug 2025)** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Website Active Users** | 87K | 266K (+205%) |
| **Sessions** | 116K | 348K (+200%) |
| **Engagement Events** | 474K | 1.4M (3×) |
| **New Users** | 86K | 264K (3×) |
| **Returning Users** | 12K | 29K (2.4×) |
| **Facebook Views** | 278K | 4.3M (+1,446%) |
| **Instagram Views** | 37K | 2.24M (+8,900%) |
| **Instagram Followers** | 179 | 2,351 (+1,200%) |
| **Leads from Instagram** | 314 | 2,000 (+535%) |
| **LinkedIn Followers** | 29K | 30.2K (+4%) |
| **Employee Brand Mentions** | 25 | 200+ |
| **Customer Brand Mentions** | 1 | 20 |

### **Key Initiatives**

- **Campaigns:** Launched **HalaTax**, **Leaders of Impact**, and **Binary Cabs** branding.
- **Events:** Hosted **Founder Dinners**, **Roundtables**, and partnered with AstroLabs & Let’sWork.
- **Content:** Rolled out **Humans of Finanshels**, AMA sessions, client stories, and podcasts.
- **PR:** Secured placements in **Khaleej Times**, **Gulf News**, and industry podcasts.
- **Employer Branding:** Employee videos, celebrations, and internal campaigns boosted 200+ organic mentions.

### **Impact (6 Months)**

- **3× website growth:** 87K → 266K active users, 1.4M+ engagement events.
- **Social explosion:** Instagram views soared 59× (37K → 2.24M), Facebook hit 4.3M views.
- **Leads at scale:** 2,000+ Meta leads generated with creative campaigns (6× increase).
- **Brand trust:** Direct traffic share +37%, 200+ employee mentions, 20 customer brand mentions.
- **Community strength:** Offline dinners, events, and podcasts built lasting recall among UAE founders.
