# Brand metrics

### TOP Metrics

- **Branded search impressions** → proof that people *actively look* for you.
- **Direct traffic share** → shows brand pull vs. paid push.
- **Engagement growth rate** (comments, reposts, shares) → signals emotional resonance, not vanity likes.
- **Referral leads / customers** → brand strong enough that customers sell for you.
- **User-generated content (UGC) volume & reshares** → audience creating on your behalf.
- **Net Promoter Score (NPS)** → cleanest measure of advocacy.
- **Earned media coverage** (press pickups, mentions) → external validation.
- **% revenue influenced by brand-led channels** (direct, organic, referral) → ties brand to business.

### **1. Brand Awareness**

- Branded search impressions (Google Search Console, Google Trends)
- Direct traffic share (Google Analytics)
- Social media reach & impressions (LinkedIn, Instagram, Twitter, etc.)
- PR coverage, media mentions, or earned media value

---

### **2. Brand Engagement**

- Engagement rates (likes, comments, reposts, saves, shares)
- Follower/community growth (LinkedIn followers, WhatsApp group members, newsletter subs)
- Event/webinar attendance and participation
- Time spent on brand content (website dwell time, video watch time)

---

### **3. Brand Consideration**

- Website conversion rates from branded campaigns
- Landing page performance (CTR, bounce rate, form fills, demo bookings)
- Repeat visits to website
- Brand-related inbound leads vs. cold leads

---

### **4. Customer Loyalty & Advocacy**

- Net Promoter Score (NPS)
- Customer referrals generated
- User-generated content (UGC) volume and reshares
- Community-led initiatives (word-of-mouth, organic mentions)

---

### **5. Revenue-Linked Brand Impact**

- % of revenue influenced by brand-led channels (direct, organic, referral)
- Customer acquisition cost (CAC) from brand-driven sources vs. paid ads
- ARPU (average revenue per user) uplift from strong brand trust
- Partnerships or collaborations driven by brand reputation

---

### **6. Employer Branding (if relevant)**

- Inbound job applications per opening
- Offer acceptance rate
- Glassdoor/LinkedIn reviews
- Internal engagement with employer brand content
