# Chap 0: Revisiting the Eternal Love

*"**Love is not just about being together but also about respecting each other's choices and making sacrifices for the greater good."*** - The Eternal Love

"The Eternal Love" is a captivating novel that tells a heart-touching story of two individuals whose lives become intertwined in ways they never imagined. Aariv is a man who has been through various struggles in life, causing him to shut down emotionally and reject the idea of personal life. On the other hand, Radha is a woman who is full of life and love and believes in the power of true love.

When Aariv and Radha cross paths, it's clear that they are destined to cross each other. Radha sees through Aariv's emotional barriers and understands that there is more to him than meets the eye. She helps him open up to the idea of love and teaches him the true meaning of loving someone unconditionally, valuing emotions, and personal life. Aariv, who was once emotionally detached, begins to feel alive again and experiences love in its purest form. Together, they embark on a beautiful journey filled with moments of happiness and love.

During those five years that Aariv and Radha were together, they created a lifetime of memories that they would never forget. They traveled to exotic places and experienced new cultures together, which helped them understand each other better. They laughed and danced under the stars, and shared secrets that they had never shared with anyone else. They talked about their future, their dreams, and their deepest fears, which helped them grow closer to each other.

Aariv and Radha's love was so pure and strong that they felt invincible when they were together. They cherished every moment that they spent together, whether it was a quiet night in the city or an adventure in a far-off land. They had a special connection that was unlike anything else in the world. They could communicate without words, and they knew each other's thoughts and feelings without having to say anything. Despite their deep love for each other, Aariv and Radha knew that society would not accept their relationship. They had to keep their love a secret and could never be seen together in public. Aariv knew that one day they would have to say goodbye, but he promised to love her until the end.

Unfortunately, societal norms and circumstances prevented them from getting married. At the beginning of their relationship, Aariv promised Radha that he would not touch her in a romantic way until they were married. He also promised to leave her if her parents rejected their marriage. When that time arrived, Aariv had to keep his promise and leave Radha. During their last meeting, she cried a lot in front of him, but he could not cry to make her feel strong. Instead, he promised to always protect her even if he was not there with her. He made her realize the reality of their love and life, and eventually she became mature enough to happily leave him for her parents and never met again. He always wanted to goodbye her with the same emotions and love with which he got her in his life earlier.

Aariv turned into a reclusive person, but he never forgot his love for Radha. He kept reminiscing about their happy memories and cherished every moment that they spent together. He knew that he would never find another person who could fill the void in his heart that Radha left. Radha got happily married to a man who is best for her in a true sense and is convinced.

In conclusion, "The Eternal Love" is a heartwarming story of love, sacrifice, and the power of true love. It teaches us that love is not just about being together but also about respecting each other's choices and making sacrifices for the greater good. The novel portrays the true essence of love and how it can change a person's life for the better. To love and win, the best thing. To love and lose, the next best!

And here we go for “The Endless Love”!
