## Phase 3 — Own (Days 61–90)

**Theme:** Take ownership of a client or workstream. Deliver independently with light-touch review. Demonstrate judgement on escalation.

### Client Work — First Solo Assignment
- [ ] Client introduced to [Employee Name] by manager (email or call)
- [ ] First solo deliverable completed: [Specify — e.g., monthly bookkeeping pack for Client X]
- [ ] CT or VAT return prepared and filed independently (reviewed once by senior before submission)
- [ ] One proactive client communication sent (deadline reminder, regulatory update)

### Quality & Compliance
- [ ] Zero overdue filings or missed internal deadlines in Days 61–90
- [ ] All client files organised to Finanshels standards and accessible to the team
- [ ] No open compliance flags (CDD, goAML, record-keeping) on assigned clients

### Knowledge Consolidation
- [ ] Completed all mandatory training modules with quiz scores ≥ 80%
- [ ] Attended at least 1 internal tax-update session
- [ ] Asked at least 1 substantive tax question to Tax Lead (demonstrates initiative)

### Day 90 Performance Review
Date scheduled: [DD MMM YYYY]

Review covers:
- [ ] Technical accuracy on deliverables produced in Phase 2 and 3
- [ ] Client service quality (internal feedback from buddy and manager)
- [ ] Timeliness: were all Phase deadlines met?
- [ ] Compliance discipline: CDD, record-keeping, escalation behaviour
- [ ] Learning pace: quiz scores, module completion, knowledge demonstrated
- [ ] Goals for the next 90 days
