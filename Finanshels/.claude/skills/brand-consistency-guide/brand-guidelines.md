## Positioning

See the `master-brand` skill for the authoritative positioning. In short:
Finanshels is the **Finance OS for the GCC's fastest-growing SMEs** — an
always-on financial infrastructure, *not* "just an accounting firm" and *not* a
software tool. Primary claim: *"UAE's Most Reliable Accounting Partner for SMEs."*
Recall line: *"Think Finance. Think Finanshels."* Always lead **WHY → WHAT** and
anchor to a proof point (7,000+ businesses served, 150+ qualified accountants).

**What we stand for:** Consistency, clarity, and genuine expertise. Every client touchpoint should feel like one firm with one standard. We don't just file returns; we keep businesses financially healthy and compliant.

## Target Audiences

### Primary: Founders / CEOs of UAE SMEs
- Often first-time business owners navigating UAE compliance
- Stretched thin; want compliance "handled"
- Fear penalties more than they understand the rules
- Respond to: confidence, simplicity, clear next steps, "we've got this"

### Secondary: Finance leads (Finance Managers, CFOs) at growing SMEs
- More technically literate
- Want depth and accuracy, not hand-holding
- Respond to: precision, references to specific regulations, professional tone

### Tertiary: Prospective clients discovering Finanshels via content/social
- May have no prior accounting partner
- Respond to: educational content, demystifying UAE tax, friendly expertise

## Visual & Verbal Identity Summary

- **Tone:** Professional but approachable. Confident without arrogance. Expert without being intimidating.
- **Register:** Slightly formal for official documents; conversational for social/email.
- **Person:** First-person plural ("we", "our", "us") for Finanshels; "you" for the client.
- **Length:** Short sentences for complex ideas. Long sentences only for lists or nuanced caveats.
- **Spelling:** British English (UAE convention). Organisation, recognised, licence (noun), tax advisor.

### Brand colours (exact hex — never approximate)

- **Bright Orange** `#F16610` — CTAs, highlights, energy
- **Deep Blue** `#0A2B3B` — headers, authority, dark backgrounds
- **Blue-gray** `#334756` — body text, UI elements
- Semantic (reports/dashboards): Good `#4CAF50`, Watch `#FF9800`, Action `#F44336`

### Proof points (exact wording — never alter the numbers)

- **7,000+ businesses served** — never write "1,300" or "hundreds of clients"
- **150+ qualified accountants** — never write "65"
- Trusted by: Abwaab, Silkhause, Dubai Future District Fund, Cotu Ventures
- Never invent statistics (transaction volumes, AED figures, entity counts). Use only these approved figures or those defined in the `master-brand` skill.

## Do / Don't

### DO
- Lead with the benefit or the point, not the background
- Name specific UAE regulations and bodies (FTA, EmaraTax, Federal Decree-Law No. 47 of 2022)
- Acknowledge that compliance can feel overwhelming, then immediately offer a solution
- Use numbers and specifics ("9% CT rate", "AED 375,000 threshold", "28th of the following month")
- End every client communication with a clear, low-friction next step
- Define acronyms on first use for non-specialist audiences
- Use bullet points to break up regulatory lists
- Say "reach out to our team" not "contact us at your earliest convenience"

### DON'T
- Use alarmist language: "urgent", "you could face severe penalties", "you are at risk"
- Start sentences with "Please be advised that…", "It should be noted that…", "Kindly note…"
- Use empty superlatives: "amazing", "world-class", "cutting-edge", "game-changing"
- Write in passive voice when active voice is available
- Reference non-UAE tax systems (IRS, HMRC, GST India, CPE) unless explicitly comparing
- Use ALL CAPS for emphasis — use bold instead
- End emails without a next step or offer to help
- Use "per se", "vis-à-vis", "inter alia", or Latin legalese
- Write paragraphs longer than 5 lines for client-facing communications

## Messaging Pillars

### 1. We simplify UAE compliance
*Proof:* We explain CT, VAT, and payroll in plain English. Our clients always know what's happening and why.

### 2. We are proactive, not reactive
*Proof:* We flag upcoming deadlines, not after-the-fact problems. Our clients are never surprised by a compliance obligation.

### 3. We are genuinely expert in UAE tax
*Proof:* We know the FTA frameworks, EmaraTax workflows, and QFZP conditions. We cite actual regulations.

### 4. We are built for startups and SMEs
*Proof:* Our pricing, processes, and communication style are designed for founders, not listed companies.
