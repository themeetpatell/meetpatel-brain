# Strategy Recommendation Builder

Use this after completing `client-analysis.md`. Translate the analysis flags into a prioritized, service-linked recommendation set for the client memo.

---

## How to use this document

1. Pull flags from the completed `client-analysis.md`.
2. Work through the recommendation menu below — pick every item that applies.
3. Rank recommendations by priority: Critical → High → Medium → Low.
4. For each recommendation, confirm the Finanshels service that delivers it.
5. Paste the final recommendation set into `templates/tax-strategy-template.md`.

> Priority: CRITICAL | Finanshels service: CT Registration

### Small Business Relief Election

**If revenue ≤ AED 3M and tax period ends on or before 31 Dec 2026:**
> **Recommendation: Elect Small Business Relief for the current tax period.**
> With revenue of approximately AED [X], you qualify to be treated as having no taxable income for this period. This eliminates CT liability and simplifies the filing. Importantly, this relief sunsets for tax periods ending after 31 Dec 2026 — act now to benefit. Finanshels will prepare and file the election with your CT return.
> Priority: HIGH | Finanshels service: CT Return Filing

### QFZP Eligibility Review

**If free zone entity — QFZP status uncertain or borderline:**
> **Recommendation: Commission a formal QFZP eligibility review before filing your first CT return.**
> If your entity qualifies as a Qualifying Free Zone Person, 0% CT applies to your qualifying income. If it does not qualify — or if conditions are not properly documented — 9% applies to all income. The four conditions (qualifying activities, adequate substance, de minimis non-qualifying income, audited financials) must all be met and evidenced. Finanshels will assess each condition against your current operations and provide a written eligibility opinion.
> Priority: HIGH | Finanshels service: CT Advisory / QFZP Review

---

### QFZP — Audited Financials

**If free zone entity needs audit to protect QFZP status:**
> **Recommendation: Engage an FTA-approved auditor to prepare audited financial statements.**
> Audited financials are a mandatory condition for QFZP status — unaudited accounts forfeit the 0% rate. Finanshels can prepare audit-ready financials and coordinate with an approved auditor on your behalf.
> Priority: HIGH | Finanshels service: Audit-Ready Financials / Audit Support

### Transfer Pricing Documentation

**If related-party transactions exist:**
> **Recommendation: Prepare transfer pricing documentation and include the related-party disclosure form in your CT return.**
> UAE CT requires that all related-party and connected-person transactions are conducted on arm's-length terms. A disclosure form must accompany your CT return. Above the FTA's prescribed thresholds, a formal master file and local file are required. Undocumented TP positions carry penalty exposure. Finanshels will prepare the disclosure and, where required, the TP documentation set.
> Priority: HIGH | Finanshels service: Transfer Pricing Advisory

---

### VAT Registration — Mandatory

**If taxable supplies exceed AED 375,000 and entity is not registered:**
> **Recommendation: Register for VAT on EmaraTax immediately.**
> Your taxable supplies have exceeded the mandatory registration threshold of AED 375,000. Operating without VAT registration exposes you to backdated VAT liability, surcharges, and administrative penalties. Finanshels will complete your VAT registration and prepare any required voluntary disclosure for the period of non-registration.
> Priority: CRITICAL | Finanshels service: VAT Registration + Voluntary Disclosure

### VAT Registration — Voluntary

**If supplies/expenses are between AED 187,500 and AED 375,000:**
> **Recommendation: Consider voluntary VAT registration to reclaim input tax.**
> You are currently below the mandatory threshold, but voluntary registration allows you to reclaim VAT paid on business costs (rent, equipment, professional services). If your input costs are significant, voluntary registration may produce an immediate cash-flow benefit. Finanshels will model the input-tax recovery and register you if it makes commercial sense.
> Priority: MEDIUM | Finanshels service: VAT Advisory + Registration

### VAT Return Compliance

**If returns are overdue or client is recently registered:**
> **Recommendation: Bring VAT returns up to date and implement a quarterly compliance calendar.**
> VAT returns and payments are due by the 28th of the month following the tax period. Overdue returns attract daily penalties. Finanshels provides a fully managed VAT return service, including review of input tax claims and reconciliation to accounting records each quarter.
> Priority: HIGH (if overdue) / MEDIUM (if current) | Finanshels service: VAT Return Filing

---

### Bookkeeping & Accounting

**If accounting records are not IFRS-compliant or are incomplete:**
> **Recommendation: Migrate to IFRS-compliant bookkeeping to support CT and VAT filings.**
> UAE CT requires taxable income to be calculated using an accepted accounting basis (IFRS or IFRS for SMEs for revenue ≤ AED 50M). Cash-basis or inconsistent records create risk at audit. Finanshels will implement a clean chart of accounts, bring records up to date, and maintain monthly bookkeeping going forward.
> Priority: HIGH | Finanshels service: Bookkeeping & Accounting

---

### Record Retention Policy

**If no formal retention policy is in place:**
> **Recommendation: Implement a 7-year record retention policy.**
> UAE CT law requires financial records to be retained for 7 years from the end of the relevant tax period. This covers invoices, contracts, bank statements, payroll records, and VAT documentation. Finanshels will provide a records management checklist and document storage guidance.
> Priority: MEDIUM | Finanshels service: Compliance Advisory

---

### UBO Register

**If UBO filing is unknown or outstanding:**
> **Recommendation: File and maintain the UBO register with your relevant registrar.**
> All UAE entities are required to maintain and file a register of Ultimate Beneficial Owners, shareholders, and nominee directors. Non-compliance attracts administrative penalties. Finanshels will prepare and file the register as part of your annual compliance package.
> Priority: MEDIUM | Finanshels service: Compliance / PRO Support

---

### Management Reporting

**If client has no regular financial visibility:**
> **Recommendation: Implement monthly management accounts to track taxable income in real time.**
> Without regular management accounts, CT liability can be a surprise at year-end, making cash planning difficult. Finanshels provides monthly P&L, balance sheet, and a rolling CT liability estimate so you always know where you stand.
> Priority: MEDIUM | Finanshels service: Management Reporting / Outsourced CFO
