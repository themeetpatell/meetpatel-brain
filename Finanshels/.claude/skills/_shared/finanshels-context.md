## 2. UAE Corporate Tax (CT)

Governed by **Federal Decree-Law No. 47 of 2022**. Administered by the **FTA**.

| Item | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Effective from | Financial years starting **on or after 1 June 2023** |
| Rate — Tier 1 | **0%** on taxable income up to **AED 375,000** |
| Rate — Tier 2 | **9%** on taxable income above AED 375,000 |
| Large MNEs | **15% Domestic Minimum Top-up Tax (DMTT)** for groups with consolidated revenue ≥ EUR 750M, for financial years starting on/after 1 Jan 2025 (OECD Pillar Two) |
| Free Zone | **Qualifying Free Zone Person (QFZP)**: 0% on *Qualifying Income*, 9% on non-qualifying income — conditions must be met (adequate substance, qualifying activities, de minimis, audited financials) |
| Small Business Relief | Revenue ≤ **AED 3M** per tax period → treated as having no taxable income. Available for tax periods ending on or before **31 Dec 2026** |
| Natural persons | Subject to CT if business turnover > **AED 1M** per calendar year |
| Registration | Mandatory for all taxable persons via **EmaraTax**; FTA deadlines set by licence-issuance month |
| Return & payment | Filed and paid within **9 months** of the end of the tax period |
| Record keeping | Retain records for **7 years** |

**Transfer Pricing:** UAE CT applies the OECD arm's-length principle. Related-party
and connected-person transactions require a disclosure form with the CT return;
master file / local file documentation applies above prescribed thresholds.

## 3. UAE VAT

Governed by **Federal Decree-Law No. 8 of 2017**. Administered by the **FTA**.

| Item | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Standard rate | **5%** (effective 1 Jan 2018) |
| Mandatory registration | Taxable supplies > **AED 375,000** in the past 12 months or expected in next 30 days |
| Voluntary registration | Taxable supplies / expenses > **AED 187,500** |
| Return filing | Quarterly or monthly via EmaraTax; due by the **28th** of the month following the tax period |
| Categories | Standard-rated (5%), zero-rated (e.g. exports, certain education/healthcare), exempt (e.g. certain financial services, bare land, local passenger transport) |

## 4. Other Compliance Obligations

- **AML/CFT:** Accounting firms and auditors are **DNFBPs**. Obligations: register
  on the **goAML** portal and the sanctions-list system, perform Customer Due
  Diligence (CDD), screen against sanctions lists, file SAR/STR reports.
- **UBO (Ultimate Beneficial Owner):** Entities must maintain and file UBO,
  shareholder, and nominee director registers with the relevant registrar.
- **ESR (Economic Substance Regulations):** Largely **legacy** — per Cabinet
  Decision No. 98 of 2024, ESR notifications/reports apply only to financial
  years **1 Jan 2019 – 31 Dec 2022**. No ESR filing for later periods. Mention
  only for historical/back-period work.
- **Audited financial statements:** Required for QFZPs and for taxable persons
  with revenue exceeding **AED 50M**, among others.
- **Accounting standards:** IFRS; **IFRS for SMEs** permitted where revenue
  ≤ AED 50M.

## 5. Payroll & People

- **WPS (Wages Protection System):** Salaries paid through MOHRE-approved channels.
- **End-of-service gratuity** and leave governed by **Federal Decree-Law No. 33 of 2021** (UAE Labour Law).
- **GPSSA** pension contributions apply for UAE/GCC nationals.

## 7. How Skills Should Behave

1. **Jurisdiction:** UAE only. Never reference IRS, US states, or CPE.
2. **Accuracy:** Cite the figure *and* tell the user to confirm against current
   FTA guidance. Never invent a deadline or rate.
3. **Brand voice:** Clear, confident, plain English. Founder-first ("you/your").
   No jargon without a short explanation. Helpful and precise — never alarmist,
   never corporate filler. For any client-facing or public content, follow the
   `master-brand` skill: lead WHY → WHAT, anchor to a proof point (7,000+
   businesses / 150+ accountants), and end with a clear next step.
4. **Not legal/tax advice:** Outputs are professional work product to be reviewed
   by a qualified Finanshels team member before reaching a client.
5. **Privacy:** Treat all client financial data as confidential. Never include
   real client identifiers in examples.
6. **Consistency:** Same structure, same tone, same quality on every output —
   that is the brand.
