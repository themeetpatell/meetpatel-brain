## 📝 Key Things to Define (by Skill)

### prospect-qualification
Define your **Ideal Customer Profile (ICP):**
- Revenue range (e.g., $500K - $10M)
- Industries served (all? specific ones?)
- Company stage (bootstrapped vs funded?)
- Red flags (what makes someone bad fit?)
- Scoring rubric (what = 90? what = 40?)

### engagement-letter-generator
Define your **legal & engagement basics:**
- Finanshels legal entity name & address
- Base engagement letter template structure
- Required compliance language for your state
- Fee structure options (fixed? hourly? retainer?)
- Scope of work examples

### compliance-calendar
Define your **tax deadlines:**
- What entity types do you serve? (S-corp, LLC, C-corp, etc.)
- What jurisdictions? (Federal, state, local, specific states?)
- 2026 deadline calendar (all relevant dates)
- Preparation checklists for each deadline
- Extension options & how to file

### engagement-budget-calculator
Define your **pricing model:**
- Service types you offer
- Complexity levels (simple, medium, complex)
- Typical hours by role (partner, manager, senior, junior)
- Your hourly rates by role
- Overhead allocation %
- Target profit margin %

### client-onboarding-guide
Define your **onboarding process:**
- Welcome email template
- Documents needed by service type
- System setup procedures (accounting software, file structure, etc.)
- Kickoff call agenda & goals
- Timeline (days/weeks from signature to active work)
- Roles & responsibilities (who does what)

## 💡 Pro Tips

**Tip 1: Use Your Real Processes**
Don't make up examples. Use actual engagements, client types, and workflows from Finanshels.

**Tip 2: Be Specific**
❌ "Fast response" → ✅ "Usually respond same-day"  
❌ "Complex engagement" → ✅ "Multi-entity setup with S-corp election"

**Tip 3: Ask Questions if Stuck**
If you don't know something, ask! Don't guess.
- "What's our typical revenue range for ideal clients?"
- "What are all the tax deadlines we track?"
- "How do we structure pricing?"

**Tip 4: Test One Example**
Before marking "DONE", test one example prompt with Claude. Does the output look right?

**Tip 5: Keep it Simple**
These skills should be 1-2 pages each. Not 10-page manuals.
