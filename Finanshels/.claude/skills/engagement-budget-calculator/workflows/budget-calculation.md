# Budget Calculation Reference

> Internal reference for the `engagement-budget-calculator` skill.
> All figures are internal guidance only and must be approved by a manager before use in client proposals.
> Verify UAE compliance deadlines against current FTA guidance on EmaraTax before committing dates.

---

## 1. Tier Definitions

| Tier | Label      | Annual Revenue (AED) | Transaction Volume     |
|------|------------|----------------------|------------------------|
| 1    | Micro      | ≤ 500K               | Low (< 100/mo)         |
| 2    | Small      | 500K – 3M            | Low–Medium (< 300/mo)  |
| 3    | Mid        | 3M – 15M             | Medium (100–500/mo)    |
| 4    | Growth     | 15M – 50M            | Medium–High (300–800/mo)|
| 5    | Enterprise | > 50M                | High (> 500/mo)        |

Note: For multi-entity engagements, classify by per-entity revenue, then flag aggregate scope for Manager review.

## 2. Base Hour Ranges by Engagement Type and Tier

Hours shown are **per year** for one-off engagements, or **per month** for recurring work (marked `/mo`).

### 2a. Bookkeeping & Accounting (monthly, recurring)

| Tier | Base hours/month | Notes                                      |
|------|------------------|--------------------------------------------|
| 1    | 4–8              | Simple ledger, bank recon, basic P&L       |
| 2    | 8–16             | Full bookkeeping, monthly management pack  |
| 3    | 16–30            | Multiple cost centres, intercompany entries|
| 4    | 30–55            | Complex ledger, consolidation possible     |
| 5    | 55–100+          | Senior-led, likely needs dedicated resource|

### 2b. VAT Filing (per filing period — quarterly or monthly)

| Tier | Hours/filing | Notes                                             |
|------|--------------|---------------------------------------------------|
| 1    | 2–4          | Simple output/input, no partial exemption         |
| 2    | 4–8          | Reconciled to management accounts                 |
| 3    | 8–14         | Multi-supplier, possibly zero-rated exports       |
| 4    | 14–22        | Capital goods, partial exemption possible         |
| 5    | 22–40        | Complex, may require specialist VAT review        |

Quarterly filing: ×4 per year. Monthly filing: ×12 per year.
VAT return due: **28th of the month following the tax period** (verify on EmaraTax).

### 2c. Corporate Tax Return (annual, one-off)

| Tier | Hours/year | Notes                                                   |
|------|------------|---------------------------------------------------------|
| 1    | 6–10       | Small Business Relief likely; CT return still required  |
| 2    | 10–20      | Standard CT computation, minimal adjustments            |
| 3    | 20–40      | Depreciation schedules, interest limitation, TP review  |
| 4    | 40–70      | Full TP disclosure, group transactions, elections       |
| 5    | 70–120+    | Director oversight; possible DMTT flag if MNE group     |

CT return and payment due: **within 9 months of financial year-end**.
CT registration: mandatory via EmaraTax — FTA deadline depends on licence-issuance month.

### 2d. Audit Support (one-off or annual)

| Tier | Hours/year | Notes                                                   |
|------|------------|---------------------------------------------------------|
| 1–2  | 10–20      | Preparing schedules, responding to queries              |
| 3    | 20–40      | Audit-ready financials package, variance analysis       |
| 4    | 40–70      | Full working papers, consolidation, group audit liaison |
| 5    | 70–130+    | Statutory audit support; required for revenue > AED 50M |

Audited financials are **mandatory** for QFZPs and entities with revenue > AED 50M.

### 2e. CFO Retainer (monthly, recurring)

| Tier | Hours/month | Notes                                                          |
|------|-------------|----------------------------------------------------------------|
| 1–2  | 8–16        | Monthly reporting, cash flow, investor-ready deck              |
| 3    | 16–30       | Board pack, budget vs. actuals, financing support              |
| 4    | 30–50       | Full FP&A, treasury, strategic advisory                        |

## 5. Blended Rate Guidance (AED per hour, internal)

| Role                 | Indicative rate (AED/hr) |
|----------------------|--------------------------|
| Accountant           | 150–200                  |
| Senior Accountant    | 200–280                  |
| Manager              | 280–380                  |
| Director / Partner   | 380–550                  |
| Blended (team avg)   | 180–250                  |

Use the **blended rate of AED 200–220/hr** for standard proposals unless the engagement is Director-heavy (then use 250+).

Minimum viable blended rate: **AED 150/hr**. Flag any proposal that falls below this to management before sending.

---

## 6. Fee Structure Templates

### Recurring Retainer (bookkeeping / CFO)
```
Monthly retainer = (monthly hours × blended rate) × [1 + margin %]
```
Standard margin: 25–35% above cost. Adjust for strategic/anchor clients.

### Per-Filing (VAT / CT)
```
Filing fee = (hours per filing × blended rate) × [1 + margin %]
```

### Onboarding Fee (one-off)
Typically 1× to 1.5× the monthly retainer. Waived only on multi-year contract sign-up — Manager approval required.

## 7. Compliance Deadline Quick Reference

| Obligation                    | Deadline rule                                      |
|-------------------------------|----------------------------------------------------|
| VAT return + payment          | 28th of month following tax period                |
| CT return + payment           | 9 months after financial year-end                  |
| CT registration               | FTA-set deadline based on licence-issuance month; verify on EmaraTax |
| Audited financials (QFZP)     | Before CT return filing                            |
| UBO register update           | Within 60 days of any change                       |
| Record retention              | 7 years minimum                                    |

**Always verify deadlines on EmaraTax or current FTA public notices before committing to a client.**

---

## 8. Small Business Relief Note

Where client revenue is ≤ **AED 3M** for the tax period (and the period ends on or before 31 Dec 2026), the entity may elect Small Business Relief — treated as having zero taxable income. CT return is still required. Mention in the engagement scope and flag that the client should confirm eligibility with the Finanshels tax team.
