## 3. Exclusions from Confidential Information

Information is not Confidential Information if and to the extent it:

(a) is or becomes publicly available through no act or omission of the Receiving Party;

(b) was in the Receiving Party's lawful possession prior to disclosure by the Disclosing Party, as evidenced by written records predating the disclosure;

(c) is independently developed by the Receiving Party without reference to or use of the Confidential Information, as evidenced by written records;

(d) is received by the Receiving Party from a third party who has the right to disclose it without restriction and without breach of any obligation of confidence; or

(e) is required to be disclosed by applicable law, regulation, a court of competent jurisdiction, or a competent regulatory or law enforcement authority, subject to Clause 4 below.

## 4. Regulatory and Legal Disclosure Carve-out

4.1 Nothing in this Agreement shall prevent either Party from disclosing Confidential Information to the extent required by applicable law, regulation, a court of competent jurisdiction, or a competent regulatory or law enforcement authority, including but not limited to:

(a) disclosures required under UAE Federal Decree-Law No. 20 of 2018 on Anti-Money Laundering and Combating the Financing of Terrorism and Illegal Organisations and its implementing regulations, and any successor or amending legislation;

(b) disclosures required by the UAE Federal Tax Authority or any other competent UAE regulatory authority;

(c) disclosures required by the UAE Central Bank, the Ministry of Economy, or any other supervisory body with jurisdiction over either Party.

4.2 Where a Party is required to make a disclosure under Clause 4.1, it shall (to the extent legally permitted and practicable):

(a) give the other Party as much advance notice of the required disclosure as is reasonably possible;

(b) co-operate with the other Party in seeking a protective order or other appropriate relief; and

(c) disclose only the minimum amount of Confidential Information necessary to satisfy the legal or regulatory requirement.

4.3 For the avoidance of doubt, nothing in this Agreement shall prevent [CONFIRM: Finanshels / Party 1 Short Name] from filing a Suspicious Activity Report or Suspicious Transaction Report with the UAE Financial Intelligence Unit via the goAML portal, or from complying with any obligation arising from its status as a Designated Non-Financial Business and Profession (DNFBP) under UAE AML/CFT law.

## 5. Obligations of the Receiving Party

5.1 The Receiving Party shall:

(a) keep the Confidential Information strictly confidential and not disclose it to any third party without the prior written consent of the Disclosing Party, except as expressly permitted by this Agreement;

(b) use the Confidential Information solely for the Purpose and for no other purpose whatsoever;

(c) restrict access to the Confidential Information to those of its directors, officers, employees, and professional advisers (including sub-contractors, where permitted under Clause 5.3) who have a legitimate need to know for the Purpose and who are bound by confidentiality obligations no less restrictive than those set out in this Agreement;

(d) not copy, reproduce, or record the Confidential Information except as strictly necessary for the Purpose;

(e) apply at least the same degree of care to protect the Confidential Information as it applies to its own confidential information of similar sensitivity, and in any event no less than reasonable care;

(f) promptly notify the Disclosing Party in writing upon becoming aware of any unauthorized access to, use of, or disclosure of the Confidential Information, and provide reasonable assistance in minimizing the impact of such unauthorized disclosure.

5.2 The Receiving Party shall not reverse-engineer, disassemble, or attempt to derive the composition of any product, process, or methodology disclosed as Confidential Information.

**12.4 Severability.** If any provision of this Agreement is found by a court of competent jurisdiction to be invalid, illegal, or unenforceable, that provision shall be severed from the Agreement, and the remaining provisions shall continue in full force.

**12.5 Notices.** Any notice under this Agreement shall be in writing and delivered by hand, registered post, or email (with read receipt or delivery confirmation) to the addresses set out in the signature block below.

## Template Usage Notes

**Choosing the right options:**

| Scenario | Recommended Choices |
|----------|-------------------|
| Client NDA (mutual) | Mutual framing; Option B sub-contractors (with restrictions); 3-year obligation; Dubai courts |
| Vendor / SaaS provider | One-way (Finanshels receives); Option A sub-contractors not permitted; 3-year obligation |
