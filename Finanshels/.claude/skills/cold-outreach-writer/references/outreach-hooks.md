# UAE Outreach Hook Library

A library of real, UAE-tax-specific outreach triggers. Each hook maps to a
segment and the angle to use. Use exactly ONE hook per sequence.

> Tax law changes. Verify every threshold and deadline against current FTA
> guidance before sending. Last reviewed: 2026-05-18.

---

## How to use this library

1. Match the prospect's segment to a hook below.
2. Confirm the hook is true and time-relevant for them right now.
3. Use the stated angle for Touch 1; rotate to value / proof / breakup after.
4. If no hook genuinely applies, the prospect is not ready for cold outreach.

---

## 1. Corporate Tax registration & first return

**Segments:** mainland LLCs, free zone entities, natural persons with business
turnover > AED 1M.

| Hook | Angle for Touch 1 |
|------|-------------------|
| First CT return + payment due within 9 months of financial year-end | "Your deadline is [date] — most founders underestimate the prep time." |
| CT registration still outstanding on EmaraTax | "FTA registration deadlines are set by licence month — let us check yours." |
| Year-end approaching with no CT-ready books | "Clean closing now means a calm filing later — not a scramble." |

Value insight to use in Touch 2: the 0% band up to AED 375,000 and 9% above —
but only with correctly prepared books; messy records cost more than the tax.

## 2. Small Business Relief window

**Segments:** SMEs with revenue ≤ AED 3M.

| Hook | Angle |
|------|-------|
| Small Business Relief available for tax periods ending on/before 31 Dec 2026 | "There is a relief you may qualify for — and a window that closes." |

Value insight: relief means treated as having no taxable income, but you must
elect correctly and still meet filing obligations.

## 3. Free Zone / QFZP decisions

**Segments:** newly licensed or established free zone companies (DMCC, JAFZA,
RAKEZ, DIFC, ADGM, etc.).

| Hook | Angle |
|------|-------|
| New free zone licence issued | "The QFZP choices you make early shape your tax bill for years." |
| QFZP status at risk — substance, qualifying income, or audit conditions | "0% on qualifying income only holds if four conditions are met." |
| Audited financials required for QFZP | "QFZP status needs audited statements — best to plan the audit now." |

Value insight: QFZP gives 0% on qualifying income, 9% on the rest — the split
and the de minimis test decide the outcome.

## 4. VAT compliance & penalty risk

**Segments:** trading, e-commerce, services businesses near or above thresholds.

| Hook | Angle |
|------|-------|
| Taxable supplies crossed AED 375,000 — mandatory registration | "Crossing the threshold triggers a registration clock — miss it and penalties follow." |
| VAT return due by the 28th of the month after the tax period | "Late or wrong VAT returns are the most common avoidable penalty we see." |
| Received an FTA VAT penalty notice | "A penalty notice often signals a fixable process gap — and sometimes a voluntary disclosure route." |
| Voluntary registration opportunity above AED 187,500 | "Voluntary registration can let you reclaim input VAT earlier." |

Value insight: 7-year record retention and the 28th-of-month cadence — process,
not effort, is what keeps VAT clean.

## 5. Growth & funding triggers

**Segments:** startups that recently raised, are raising, or are scaling fast.

| Hook | Angle |
|------|-------|
| Recently announced a funding round (public news only) | "Investors will ask for clean, monthly, audit-ready numbers — get ahead of it." |
| Scaling headcount fast | "Payroll, WPS, and reporting get messy fast at this stage." |
| Approaching AED 50M revenue | "Above AED 50M you need audited statements — and IFRS, not IFRS for SMEs." |

Value insight: a monthly management-accounts rhythm and an outsourced CFO view
turn finance from a fire drill into a decision tool.

## 6. Operational pain triggers

**Segments:** any SME with a finance gap.

| Hook | Angle |
|------|-------|
| Bookkeeper or accountant just left | "A gap in your books is a gap in your compliance — let us bridge it." |
| Behind on bookkeeping / no clean financials | "Back-period clean-up before deadlines beats a year-end panic." |
| Payroll / WPS handled manually | "WPS errors create labour and banking headaches — automation removes them." |

Value insight: the cost of an in-house finance function vs an always-on partner.

## Hooks NOT to use

- Generic "we offer accounting services" — not a hook.
- Invented urgency ("deadline this week" when it is not).
- Fear-only framing with no helpful next step — Finanshels is never alarmist.
- Anything referencing non-UAE tax (IRS, UK, EU).
- A pricing or discount "offer" — the ask is always a call.
