# Lead Magnet Outline Template

> Use this to choose a format, structure the asset, and brief the opt-in page.
> A Finanshels team member must review before publication.

---

## 1. Format Pull Table

Pick the format that matches the funnel stage and the segment's mindset.

| Format | Best funnel stage | Why it pulls | Good UAE topics |
|--------|-------------------|--------------|-----------------|
| Checklist | Top — awareness | Fast, deadline-driven, low effort to consume | CT registration steps; VAT-return prep checklist; year-end close |
| Guide / explainer | Middle — consideration | Builds trust, answers "how does this apply to me" | Free zone QFZP guide; Small Business Relief guide; CT for startups |
| Template | Middle / bottom | Tangible tool the prospect keeps using | Bookkeeping tracker; expense categorisation template; board-report template |
| Calculator concept | Bottom — decision | High intent; quantifies their own situation | CT liability estimator; VAT-threshold checker; gratuity calculator |

> A calculator is delivered as a *concept spec* (inputs, logic, outputs) for the
> product/web team to build — this skill does not build the calculator itself.
