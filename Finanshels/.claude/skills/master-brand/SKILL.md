## 🎯 Finanshels Brand Position (Single Source of Truth)

| **Recall Line** | "Think Finance. Think Finanshels." |

## ❓ WHY We Exist (Lead with this always)

Every founder deserves financial clarity. Too many UAE businesses fail not because the idea was wrong — but because the finances were a mess. 

We exist to change that.

**7,000+ businesses trusted us with their numbers. We take that seriously.**

### Message Order Rule

## 🗣️ Voice & Tone Rules (ALWAYS Follows)

### What We Always Do

✅ **Direct, confident, plain English**  
✅ **Founder-first language** ("you/your", not "clients/businesses")  
✅ **Specific over generic** ("7,000+ clients" not "hundreds of clients")  
✅ **Action-oriented** ("Fix your books today" not "We help with bookkeeping")  
✅ **Regional authority** ("Built for the UAE, scaling the GCC")  

### What We Never Do

❌ **Jargon without translation**  
❌ **Passive voice** ("Services are offered" → "We deliver")  
❌ **Over-promise** ("We guarantee success" → "We've helped 7,000+ founders grow")  
❌ **Corporate filler** ("synergies", "holistic", "leverage our expertise")  
❌ **Impersonal or arrogant tone**

## 🎨 Visual Identity

### Brand Colors (Exact Hex — Use These)

**Primary:**
- **Bright Orange** `#F16610` — CTAs, highlights, energy
- **Deep Blue** `#0A2B3B` — headers, authority, dark backgrounds
- **Blue-gray** `#334756` — body text, UI elements

**Secondary:**
- **Deep Saffron** `#FFA534` — UI accents, warmth
- **Peach Fuzz** `#FFDFB7` — backgrounds, contrast
- **Light Gray** `#DDDDDD` — neutral sections

**Accent:**
- **Light Peach** `#FFF7E9` — page backgrounds
- **Platinum Gray** `#EEEEEE` — content areas

**Semantic (Reports/Dashboards):**
- **Good/Green** `#4CAF50`
- **Watch/Amber** `#FF9800`
- **Action/Red** `#F44336`

### Typography

**Fonts:**
- **Primary:** Inter (headlines, body, buttons, UI)
- **Secondary:** Helvetica (quotes, callouts, stats, labels)

**Size Scale:**
- H1: 48–64px
- H2: 36–48px
- H3: 24–32px
- Body: 16–20px (desktop), 14–18px (mobile)
- Labels/captions: 12–14px
- Footnotes: 10–12px

**Line Height:** 1.2x for headings, 1.5x for body

### Logo Usage Rules

- **Light backgrounds:** Full logo (dark wordmark + orange "shels")
- **Dark backgrounds:** White/reversed version
- **Icon only:** Gauge mark in orange + dark navy (standalone use)
- **Tagline lockup:** "Automate Accounting & Tax Operations" under full logo
- **Never:** stretch, recolor, or add effects to the logo

## 🔧 Generation Rules (Follow Every Time)

### Rule 1: WHY Before WHAT
Every piece of content must open with purpose, not service description.

❌ "We offer bookkeeping services..."  
✅ "Most founders waste 40 hours/month on bookkeeping. Finanshels automates it."

### Rule 2: Anchor to Primary Claim
Always reference "UAE's Most Reliable Accounting Partner for SMEs."

❌ "We're an accounting firm."  
✅ "As the UAE's most reliable accounting partner for SMEs, we..."

### Rule 3: Clear CTA
Never end without directing the reader somewhere.

❌ "We help with your finances."  
✅ "Get your books audit-ready in 14 days. Start free trial."

### Rule 4: Use Proof Points
Use at least once per material. Default to "7,000+ businesses" and "150+ qualified accountants."

❌ "We have a lot of experience."  
✅ "7,000+ UAE businesses trust Finanshels — backed by 150+ qualified accountants."

### Rule 5: Brand Colors (No Approximations)
For documents/decks, apply brand colors exactly:
- Bright Orange `#F16610` for CTAs
- Deep Blue `#0A2B3B` for headers

### Rule 6: Never "Just an Accounting Firm"
Always include reliability + scale + tech angle.

❌ "An accounting firm for SMEs"  
✅ "The Finance OS for ambitious SMEs — accounting, tax, and CFO insights in one platform"

### Rule 7: Match Tone to Channel
Refer to tone-by-channel table. Website ≠ TikTok ≠ Investor pitch.

### Rule 8: Import Inter Font in Code
For HTML/React artifacts, import Inter from Google Fonts:
```html
<link href="https://fonts.googleapis.com/css2?family=Inter:wght@400;600;700&display=swap" rel="stylesheet">
```

### Rule 9: Correct Numbers (Always)
- **7,000+** businesses served (NEVER "1,300")
- **150+** qualified accountants (NEVER "65")

### Rule 10: Regional Authority
Always include UAE/GCC angle. Never generic Middle East language.

❌ "We serve the Middle East."  
✅ "Built for the UAE, scaling the GCC."

## 🎯 How to Use This Master Brand Skill

### For Content Teams
1. Start with WHY (our mission)
2. Check the tone for your channel
3. Use a proof point (7,000+ or 150+)
4. End with clear CTA
5. Validate against Brand DNA (Smart, Credible, Ambitious, Regional, Human, Infrastructure)

### For Design Teams
1. Use exact hex colors (no approximations)
2. Inter font for all text
3. Logo rules (don't stretch/recolor)
4. Apply color semantics for dashboards/reports

### For Sales/BD Teams
1. Lead with WHY (financial clarity, founder focus)
2. Show proof (7,000+ businesses)
3. Position as infrastructure, not service
4. Match founder ambitions (0→100 scaling)

### For Product/Engineering Teams
1. Use Smart trait (highlight AI/automation)
2. Regional First positioning
3. Infrastructure language (not "features", think "system")
4. Apply color semantics for UI (Green for good, Amber for watch, Red for action)
