### Model quick-reference
| Model | Use when | Partner does | Finanshels does |
|-------|----------|--------------|-----------------|
| Referral | Partner trusts but stays hands-off | Introduces the client | Everything after the intro |
| Co-marketing | Both have audiences to reach | Shares content / events | Co-creates content, delivers service |
| White-label / embedded | Partner wants finance under their brand | Owns the client relationship | Delivers accounting in the background |
| Reseller | Partner sells finance as part of a bundle | Sells and bills | Delivers the engagement |
