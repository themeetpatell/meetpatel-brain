# Life Review Cadence

## Daily
- check energy
- capture emotional state
- log small wins and friction
- shut down cleanly

## Weekly
- review sleep, training, habits, and emotional tone
- identify what restored energy
- cut one recurring drain

## Monthly
- step back across life areas
- assess alignment
- reset habits, routines, and priorities
