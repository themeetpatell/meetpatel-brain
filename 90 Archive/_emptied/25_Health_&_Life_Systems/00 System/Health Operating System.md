# Health Operating System

## Purpose
Create physical, emotional, and mental steadiness that supports long-term ambition.

## Core layers
- energy management
- sleep and recovery
- fitness and movement
- habit consistency
- reflection and recalibration
- emotional clarity

## Rule
Health systems should increase stability and performance without becoming another source of stress.
