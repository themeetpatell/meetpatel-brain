# Signal Evaluation Framework

## Evaluate each signal on:
- relevance
- recency
- repeatability
- strategic impact
- cost to ignore

## Levels
### Weak signal
Interesting but unproven. Watch only.

### Medium signal
Repeated pattern or credible move. Track closely.

### Strong signal
Clear implication for product, GTM, brand, or market behavior. Needs response or decision.
