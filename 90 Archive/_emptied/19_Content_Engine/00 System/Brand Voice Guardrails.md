# Brand Voice Guardrails

## Desired
- sharp
- founder-grade
- operator-aware
- human
- signal-rich
- commercially relevant

## Avoid
- fluff
- generic motivation
- robotic AI jargon
- startup clichés
- abstract writing with no point
- contrast structures like “not X but Y”

## Strong openings
- hidden truth
- operating lesson
- observed pattern
- market tension
- founder confession with a useful point
