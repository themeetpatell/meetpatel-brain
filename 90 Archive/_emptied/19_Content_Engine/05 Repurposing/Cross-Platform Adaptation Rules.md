# Cross-Platform Adaptation Rules

## LinkedIn
Depth, authority, readability, founder tone

## X
Sharp, compressed, thread-friendly, punchy

## Newsletter
More reflective, more expansive, higher signal density

## WhatsApp / broadcast
Very short, high curiosity, immediate relevance

## Short-form video
Hook first, one idea only, emotionally clear
