# Venture Portfolio Thesis

## Core idea
This portfolio is built around operating systems for business, growth, and life.

## Shared DNA
- AI-native workflows
- strong positioning
- high-trust execution
- systems over randomness
- leverage through product + process + brand

## Why this matters
Each venture should strengthen the others through:
- distribution
- proof
- talent
- technology patterns
- audience overlap
- execution learnings

## Test
Every venture should answer:
- Why does this deserve to exist?
- Why now?
- Why us?
- How does it compound the portfolio?
