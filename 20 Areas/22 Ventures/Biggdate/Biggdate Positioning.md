# Biggdate Positioning

## Core positioning
A dating product designed for people who want meaningful, better-fit connections with stronger intent and less noise.

## Strategic priorities
- premium perception
- trust and safety
- strong emotional resonance
- differentiated voice
- culturally sharp narrative

## Watchouts
- sounding generic
- drifting into typical swipe-app language
- losing brand sharpness
