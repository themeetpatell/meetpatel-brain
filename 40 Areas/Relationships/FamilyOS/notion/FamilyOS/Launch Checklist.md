## **🚨 Red Flags to Watch**

### **Product**

- ❌ < 20% activation rate
- ❌ > 10% monthly churn
- ❌ NPS < 30
- ❌ High support ticket volume

### **Business**

- ❌ CAC > $300
- ❌ Payback period > 12 months
- ❌ Burn rate > $300K/month (pre-seed)
- ❌ Runway < 6 months

### **Team**

- ❌ Co-founder conflict
- ❌ Key person risk (critical role unfilled)
- ❌ Culture issues (toxic behavior)
- ❌ Hiring too fast or too slow

## **💡 Key Success Factors**

### **1. Obsess Over User Experience**

Every pixel matters. Every flow matters. We're competing with Apple-grade products.

### **2. Move Fast, But Don't Break Things**

Speed is essential, but trust is everything. Don't compromise on security or privacy.

### **3. Build in Public**

Share the journey. Be transparent. Families want to trust a company that trusts them.

### **4. Focus on Retention Over Acquisition**

A family that stays is worth 20 families that churn. Make current users wildly successful.

### **5. Think 10 Years Out**

We're building infrastructure for generations. Every decision should consider longevity.
