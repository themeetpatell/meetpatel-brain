# Content Scorecard

Score each published piece from 1 to 10 on:
- clarity
- originality
- authority
- relevance
- brand fit
- engagement quality

## Reflection
- What pulled the right audience?
- What got vanity engagement only?
- Which post strengthened positioning?
- Which one should become a larger asset?
