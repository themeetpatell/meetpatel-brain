# Research Operating System

## Purpose
Turn scattered information into usable strategic insight.

## Flow
1. capture source
2. evaluate relevance
3. extract key points
4. classify signal strength
5. synthesize insight
6. connect to decisions or content

## Rules
- do not collect for the sake of collecting
- save only what can influence strategy, content, product, growth, or decision quality
- separate facts, signals, and interpretations
