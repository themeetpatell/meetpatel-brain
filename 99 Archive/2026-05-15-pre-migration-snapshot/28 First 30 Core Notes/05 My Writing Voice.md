# My Writing Voice

## Desired qualities
- sharp
- human
- founder-grade
- commercially aware
- signal-heavy
- culturally aware
- direct without sounding dead

## Avoid
- fluffy motivation
- robotic startup jargon
- generic AI language
- contrast structures like “not X but Y”
- decorative writing with no insight
