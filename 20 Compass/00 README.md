# 20 Compass — who I am, what I stand for, where I'm going

> The slow-moving layer. Identity, principles, vision, durable decisions.

## Subfolders
- `Identity/` — founder profile, voice, life timeline, content pillars, relationships
- `Principles/` — decision principles, life rules
- `Vision/` — 1/5/10-year vision, big bets
- `Decisions/` — durable life/career decisions (per-venture decisions live under `30 Ventures/<name>/Decisions/`)
- `Dossier/` — public bio, press, speaker pack

## Rules
- Edits here are *deliberate* — they change voice, identity, and strategy downstream
- Update Identity/ files quarterly or after a real shift
- Files here are loaded by `_AI/Contexts/meet.md`

## How agents use this folder
- `Identity/Writing Voice.md` is the source of truth for voice
- `Principles/` is loaded before any decision-frame agent runs
- `Vision/` is loaded for OKR-setting, quarterly review, and investor narrative
