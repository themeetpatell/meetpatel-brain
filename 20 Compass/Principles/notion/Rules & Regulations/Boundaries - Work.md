# Boundaries - Work

1. **Workload:** Avoid taking on more work or responsibilities than you can handle effectively and that's not yours.
2. **Worktime:** Avoid working during non-working hours for that project/career.
3. **Task Prioritization**: Set clear priorities for your tasks and projects. Focus on high-impact tasks that align with your goals and don't get sidetracked by less important work.
4. **Communication Hours**: Establish specific hours for work-related communication, such as emails and meetings. Outside of these hours, limit your responses to urgent matters only.
5. **Overtime Limit**: Determine a limit for how much overtime you are willing to put in. Avoid excessive overtime, which can lead to burnout and reduced productivity.
6. **Vacation Time**: Plan and take regular vacations to recharge and disconnect from work. During vacations, ensure that you are truly away from work responsibilities.
7. **Delegation**: Delegate tasks and responsibilities to others when possible. You don't have to handle everything on your own.
8. **Saying No to Additional Projects**: If you're already committed to existing projects, be prepared to say no to new work requests that may come your way.
