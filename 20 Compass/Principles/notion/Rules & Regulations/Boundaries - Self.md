# Boundaries - Self

1. **Saying No:** Say “No” to anything or anyone that isn’t family that's not giving me money or growth.
2. **Meditation Time:** No one is allowed while meditation time. There are no exceptions for this.
3. **Family Time:** Dedicate quality time for your family without work or other distractions.
4. **Emotional Boundaries:** Protect emotional well-being by limiting exposure to negativity and toxic relationships.
5. **Self-respect:** Anything that hurts your self-respect should be out of your life.
6. **No Excuses/Reasons:** Don't give or listen to any reasons or excuses. It's either Yes/No, Did/Didn't, It is/It isn't, 0%/100%, etc.
7. **Respond, Don't React:** Respond to anything with mind, keeping your emotions aside.
8. **Social Commitments:** No social participation unless it’s from Permanent People.
9. **Information Intake:** Control the information you consume, focusing on what is relevant and beneficial.
10. **Relationships:** Not making any kind of relationships with anyone who is not aligned to my path.
11. **Energy Levels:** Recognize when you need to conserve energy and limit activities that drain you.
12. **Mealtimes:** Dedicate time for meals without distractions, promoting mindful eating.
13. **Privacy:** Safeguard your personal information and digital privacy online.
14. **Negative Self-Talk:** Challenge and replace negative self-talk with positive affirmations.
15. **Controlled Thinking:** Have no thought without your permission.
