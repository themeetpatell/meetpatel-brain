# Personal Brand Positioning

## Core positioning
A founder-operator and systems thinker who builds AI-native ventures, execution systems, and strategic narratives that help startups move with more clarity and leverage.

## Perception goals
Be known for:
- sharp thinking
- usable systems
- founder-grade clarity
- signal-heavy writing
- strategic, not generic, AI-native thinking

## Avoid
- template founder content
- surface-level motivational posting
- vague innovation language
- AI buzzword theater
