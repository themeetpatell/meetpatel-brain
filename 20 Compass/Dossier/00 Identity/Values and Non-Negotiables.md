# Values and Non-Negotiables

## Values
- Excellence
- Velocity
- Leverage
- Integrity
- Compounding thinking
- Real usefulness
- Human depth with operational sharpness

## Non-Negotiables
- Do not ship vague thinking.
- Do not confuse activity with progress.
- Do not build brands that sound generic.
- Do not let systems become dependent on one person.
- Do not tolerate avoidable ambiguity in ownership.

## Red Flags
- Performative work
- Empty positioning
- Decision drift
- Dependency without documentation
- Repetition without learning
