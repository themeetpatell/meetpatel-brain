# Life Principles

- Build things that outlive mood.
- Protect energy like capital.
- Let systems carry discipline when emotion fluctuates.
- Choose compounding moves over impressive moves.
- Stay close to reality; narratives should match operating truth.
- Keep ambition large and execution practical.
- Use writing to clarify thinking.
- Use strategy to reduce waste.
- Keep love, beauty, and meaning inside the machine.
