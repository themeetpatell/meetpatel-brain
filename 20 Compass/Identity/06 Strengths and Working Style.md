# Strengths and Working Style

## Natural strengths
- Systems thinking
- Sharp pattern recognition
- High agency
- Speed with structure
- Founder empathy + operator discipline
- Turning ambiguity into frameworks
- Strong commercial instinct
- Narrative + positioning ability

## Working style
- Prefers outputs over abstraction
- Wants production-ready work, not theoretical drafts
- Likes clarity, speed, and strong standards
- Operates well across strategy, execution, product thinking, and communication
- Strong dislike for fluff, jargon, and vague startup language

## Decision style
- Bias toward action
- Looks for leverage and compounding value
- Thinks in systems, not isolated tasks
- Cares about differentiation, inevitability, and control

## Collaboration style
Works well when people are direct, prepared, and useful. Low patience for noise, weak thinking, or unnecessary complexity.
