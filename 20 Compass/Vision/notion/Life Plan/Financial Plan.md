# Financial Plan

**Boundaries**

- **No Lending Rule:** Not giving money to anyone unless they have no one in the world, not even for life-saving events. Get money back if it’s given to anyone on the time or else it’s pay with interest. Don’t spend money on anyone that is not going to give me double of it later.
- **Saving & Investment Rule:** Put money in emergency fund, saving fund, family fund, health fund, career fund, investment fund.
- **Necessity-Only Rule:** Spend money only on absolute necessities such as food, shelter, utilities, and healthcare.
- **Debt-Free Rule:** Avoid taking on unnecessary debt, and if you do borrow, prioritize paying it off as quickly as possible. Avoid taking Loans or using credit-cards
- **Avoid Materialism Rule:** Avoid spending on materialistic pleasures, luxury items, or extravagant displays of wealth.
- **Financial Review Rule:** Conduct a financial review or assessment at least once a year to evaluate your progress toward your financial goals.
- **Cost-Cutting Rule:** Regularly review your expenses and identify areas where you can cut costs without compromising your quality of life.
- **Opportunity Cost Rule:** Consider the opportunity cost of every financial decision—what could you achieve with the money if you didn't spend it on that particular item or experience?
- **Regular Bill Payments Rule:** Pay bills, loans, and credit card balances on time to avoid late fees and interest charges.
